## Switching from Another Lender

If your current mortgage is with another provider, switching to ToggleBank could save you money. Here's what we offer:

- **Free legal fees** on remortgages over £150,000 — we instruct a solicitor from our approved panel at no cost to you. For remortgages under £150,000, a contribution of £300 towards legal fees is provided.
- **Free standard valuation** — we arrange and pay for the valuation of your property.
- **No hidden charges** — the rate you see is the rate you get. Choose between a £999 arrangement fee or a fee-free product at a slightly higher rate.

## Switching Your Existing ToggleBank Deal

If your current ToggleBank fixed rate or tracker deal is ending, you can switch to a new product without a full application. We'll write to you approximately 3 months before your deal ends with your options. You can also check available rates at any time through Online Banking or the ToggleBank app.

- **No additional valuation required** in most cases
- **No legal fees** — we handle everything internally
- **Seamless transition** — your new rate starts the day after your current deal ends

## Early Repayment Charges

If you remortgage away from ToggleBank during a fixed rate period, or if you're repaying more than 10% of the outstanding balance per year, early repayment charges will apply:

| Time Remaining on Fixed Term | ERC (% of Balance Repaid) |
|---|---|
| More than 4 years remaining | 5% |
| 3–4 years remaining | 4% |
| 2–3 years remaining | 3% |
| 1–2 years remaining | 2% |
| Less than 1 year remaining | 1% |

No early repayment charges apply once you are on the Standard Variable Rate.

## Porting Your Mortgage

If you're moving home, you may be able to take your existing ToggleBank mortgage deal with you (known as porting). This avoids early repayment charges and lets you keep your current rate. Porting is subject to a new valuation and affordability assessment on the new property. If you need to borrow more, additional borrowing will be on a new rate.

## Eligibility

- Aged 18 or over (mortgage term must end before your 75th birthday)
- UK resident with the right to reside in the UK
- Property must be your main residence (not buy-to-let — see our BTL products)
- Satisfactory credit history — a credit check will be performed
- Minimum property value: £50,000

## How to Apply

1. **Check your options** — Use our online remortgage calculator at togglebank.co.uk/remortgage or call 0345 600 7000 to speak with an adviser.
2. **Get a Decision in Principle** — We'll confirm how much you can borrow, usually within 15 minutes.
3. **Full application** — Your adviser will guide you through the paperwork. We'll arrange the valuation and instruct solicitors.
4. **Completion** — Once everything is approved, your new mortgage completes and your previous lender is repaid.
