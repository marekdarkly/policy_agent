# ToggleBank Balance Transfer Credit Card

## Product Overview

The ToggleBank Balance Transfer Credit Card helps you take control of existing credit card debt with a market-leading **0% interest on balance transfers for 24 months**. Transfer balances from other credit cards and pay down your debt faster without accruing interest.

**Product Code:** TB-CREDIT-BALANCE-TRANSFER-2026
**Card Network:** Mastercard
**Issued by:** ToggleBank plc (authorised by the PRA, regulated by the FCA and PRA)

## Balance Transfer Details

- Transfers must be requested **within 60 days** of account opening to qualify for the 0% introductory rate
- Minimum transfer amount: **£100**
- Maximum transfer: up to **95% of your credit limit**
- Transfers from other ToggleBank credit cards are not eligible
- You can make multiple transfers (subject to available credit limit)
- Allow **5–7 working days** for the transfer to complete
- Continue making at least the minimum payment on your existing card(s) until the transfer is confirmed

---

## Minimum Payments

| Scenario | Minimum Payment |
|---|---|
| Balance over £1,000 | **2.5% of the outstanding balance** |
| Balance £25–£1,000 | **£25** |
| Balance under £25 | Full balance |

- Payments are due on the same date each month (shown on your statement)
- If you miss a payment, the introductory 0% rate **may be withdrawn**
- Setting up a Direct Debit for at least the minimum payment is strongly recommended

---

## Important Information About Payments

- Payments are allocated to the **highest-interest balance first** (in line with FCA rules)
- Avoid making purchases on this card — purchases accrue interest at 22.9% APR immediately, and payments will first reduce the highest-rate balance
- For the best outcome, use this card **solely for balance transfers** and use a separate card for everyday purchases

---

## Eligibility Requirements

- Must be a **UK resident** aged 18 or over
- Must have a UK bank account
- Subject to credit check and affordability assessment
- A good to excellent credit history is typically required for the full 24-month 0% period
- Applicants with thinner credit files may be offered a shorter introductory period

## Eligibility Checker

- Use the ToggleBank eligibility checker before applying

- See your likelihood of approval and the offer you may receive

## Managing Your Balance Transfer

- Track your introductory period end date in the ToggleBank app
- Set up payment reminders to avoid missed payments
- The app displays a countdown showing months remaining on the 0% offer
- If you cannot repay the balance before the introductory period ends, consider transferring the remaining balance to another 0% offer (subject to eligibility)

---

## How to Apply

1. Check your eligibility at togglebank.co.uk/eligibility (soft search)
2. Apply online — decision typically within 60 seconds
3. Request your balance transfer during the application or within 60 days
4. Receive your card within 5–7 working days
5. Activate via the ToggleBank app or by calling 0800 072 7272
