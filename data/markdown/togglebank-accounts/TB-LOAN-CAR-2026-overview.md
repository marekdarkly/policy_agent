# ToggleBank Car Finance

**Product Code:** TB-LOAN-CAR-2026
**Last Updated:** January 2026
**Status:** Available to new applicants

## Product Summary

ToggleBank offers two car finance options to help you get behind the wheel: Hire Purchase (HP) for customers who want to own the car outright, and Personal Contract Purchase (PCP) for those who prefer lower monthly payments with flexibility at the end of the agreement. Both products are available for new and used vehicles through our approved dealer network or direct from ToggleBank.

| Feature | Hire Purchase (HP) | Personal Contract Purchase (PCP) |
|---|---|---|
| Representative APR | 6.5% APR | 7.2% APR |
| Deposit | Minimum 10% | Minimum 10% |
| Term | 2–5 years | 2–4 years |
| Ownership | You own the car once all payments are made | Option to purchase at end via balloon payment |
| Monthly payments | Higher (paying off full value) | Lower (paying off depreciation only) |
| Mileage limit | None | Annual limit agreed at outset |
| Eligible vehicles (new) | Yes | Yes |
| Eligible vehicles (used) | Up to 8 years old at end of agreement | Up to 6 years old at end of agreement |

## How Hire Purchase Works

With Hire Purchase, you pay a deposit and then make fixed monthly payments over the agreed term. Once you've made all payments, you own the car. There is a nominal Option to Purchase fee of £1 at the end.

**HP Example:**
- Vehicle price: £20,000
- Deposit: £2,000 (10%)
- Amount financed: £18,000
- Term: 4 years (48 months)
- Monthly payment: £429.18
- Total amount payable: £22,600.64 (including deposit and Option to Purchase fee)
- Total cost of credit: £2,600.64
- Representative APR: 6.5%

## How Personal Contract Purchase Works

With PCP, your monthly payments cover the car's depreciation (the difference between the purchase price and its predicted value at the end of the agreement), not the full cost. At the end of the term, you have three options:

1. **Return the car** — Hand the car back with nothing further to pay (subject to condition and mileage limits)
2. **Pay the balloon payment** — Make the final Guaranteed Minimum Future Value (GMFV) payment and own the car outright
3. **Part exchange** — Use any equity in the car as a deposit on a new PCP agreement

**PCP Example:**
- Vehicle price: £25,000
- Deposit: £2,500 (10%)
- Amount financed: £22,500
- Term: 3 years (36 months)
- Monthly payment: £312.47
- Balloon payment (GMFV): £12,500
- Total amount payable: £26,248.92 (including deposit)
- Total cost of credit: £1,248.92 (if car is returned; higher if balloon payment is made)
- Representative APR: 7.2%

## Mileage and Condition (PCP)

PCP agreements include an annual mileage limit, agreed at the start of your contract. Common options:

| Annual Mileage Allowance | Excess Mileage Charge |
|---|---|
| 6,000 miles | 8p per mile |
| 8,000 miles | 8p per mile |
| 10,000 miles | 8p per mile |
| 12,000 miles | 6p per mile |
| 15,000 miles | 6p per mile |

If you exceed the mileage limit, excess mileage charges apply if you return the car. These charges do not apply if you purchase the car via the balloon payment. The car must also be returned in good condition, fair wear and tear accepted as defined by the British Vehicle Rental and Leasing Association (BVRLA) guidelines.

## GAP Insurance

Guaranteed Asset Protection (GAP) insurance covers the difference between your car's market value and the outstanding finance balance if the car is written off or stolen. Without GAP insurance, your motor insurance payout may not cover what you still owe on the finance agreement.

| GAP Product | Coverage | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Return to Invoice GAP | Pays difference between insurer payout and original invoice price | From £199 (one-off premium) |
| Return to Value GAP | Pays difference between insurer payout and outstanding finance | From £149 (one-off premium) |

GAP insurance is optional. You have 30 days from purchasing GAP insurance to cancel for a full refund. GAP insurance is underwritten by ToggleBank Insurance Services Ltd.

## Your Rights Under the Consumer Credit Act

- **Voluntary termination:** Under the Consumer Credit Act 1974, you have the right to voluntarily terminate your HP or PCP agreement once you have paid at least 50% of the total amount payable (including any balloon payment for PCP). The car must be returned in reasonable condition. Early termination may affect your credit score.
- **Cooling-off period:** You have 14 days from the date of signing to withdraw from the agreement without giving a reason. You must repay the credit and any interest accrued within 30 days.
- **Right to settle early:** You can settle the agreement in full at any time. You may be entitled to a rebate on future interest charges.

## Eligibility

- Aged 18 or over
- UK resident with a full UK driving licence
- Minimum annual income: £10,000
- Satisfactory credit history — a credit check will be performed
- Vehicle must be purchased from a UK-based dealer or private seller (HP only for private sales)
- Vehicle must be comprehensively insured for the duration of the agreement
