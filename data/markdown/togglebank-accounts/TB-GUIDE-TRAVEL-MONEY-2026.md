ToggleBank offers competitive foreign currency exchange with over **50 currencies** available. Whether you prefer cash notes, a prepaid travel card, or simply using your ToggleBank debit card abroad, we have options to suit your trip.

### Rates & Fees

- **No commission** on currency notes — the exchange rate is all-inclusive.
- Rates are competitive with the Post Office and major high-street providers. Check our live rates at [togglebank.co.uk/exchange-rates](https://togglebank.co.uk/exchange-rates).
- ToggleBank **price-matches** any lower high-street rate if you find one within 24 hours of purchase (terms apply).

### Buy-Back Guarantee

Changed your plans? ToggleBank will buy back **unused notes** at the **same rate you purchased them** if returned within **30 days** of purchase:

- Notes must be in **good condition** (unfolded, no writing or tears).
- Return in-branch with your **receipt or order confirmation**.
- Coins are **not eligible** for buy-back.
- Minimum buy-back amount: equivalent of **£50**.

## Emergency Cash Abroad

If your card is lost, stolen, or not working while abroad, ToggleBank can arrange **emergency cash**:

- Up to **£500 equivalent** in local currency.
- Available within **2 hours** via MoneyGram partner locations worldwide.
- Call our 24/7 international line: **+44 20 7946 0958**.
- A fee of **£25** applies for emergency cash disbursement.
- You will need to provide identity verification over the phone and collect from a MoneyGram location with photo ID.
