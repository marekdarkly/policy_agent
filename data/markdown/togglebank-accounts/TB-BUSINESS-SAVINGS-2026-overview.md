# ToggleBank Business Savings Account

**Product Code:** TB-BUSINESS-SAVINGS-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Regulated by:** Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA)

## Overview

The ToggleBank Business Savings Account helps businesses earn a competitive return on surplus cash. Three account types are available to suit different liquidity needs, from instant access to fixed-term deposits. All business savings accounts must be held alongside a ToggleBank Business Current Account.

## Account Types and Interest Rates

| Account Type | Gross Rate | AER | Minimum Balance | Access |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Business Instant Access Saver | 3.21% | **3.25% AER** | No minimum | Unlimited withdrawals |
| Business 95-Day Notice Saver | 3.69% | **3.75% AER** | No minimum | 95 days' written notice |
| Business 1-Year Fixed Rate Bond | 4.02% | **4.10% AER** | £10,000 | No access until maturity |

All rates are current as at 1 January 2026 and are subject to change. Fixed rate bonds lock the rate for the full term.

## Interest Payment

- **Instant Access Saver:** interest calculated daily, paid quarterly (March, June, September, December)
- **95-Day Notice Saver:** interest calculated daily, paid quarterly
- **1-Year Fixed Rate Bond:** interest calculated daily, paid quarterly or at maturity (your choice at account opening)

Interest can be paid into the savings account or credited to your ToggleBank Business Current Account.

## Deposit and Withdrawal Rules

| Rule | Instant Access | 95-Day Notice | 1-Year Fixed |

## Key Features

- **Online and app management** — view balances, transfer funds, and give notice via ToggleBank Business Online or the ToggleBank Business app
- **Instant transfers** — move money between your Business Current Account and Instant Access Saver in real time
- **Multiple accounts** — open up to 5 savings accounts of each type
- **Named accounts** — label each account for budgeting (e.g., "VAT Reserve", "Equipment Fund")
- **Rate alerts** — receive notifications when rates change

## Tax Information

- Interest is paid gross (without tax deducted)
- Businesses are responsible for reporting interest income to HMRC and paying any Corporation Tax or Income Tax due
- Sole traders and partnerships may benefit from the Personal Savings Allowance (£1,000 basic rate / £500 higher rate) on interest earned — consult your accountant for details

## Eligibility

- Available to UK-registered businesses holding a ToggleBank Business Current Account
- Sole traders, partnerships, LLPs, and limited companies
- Clubs, societies, and charities may also apply

## How to Apply

1. Log in to ToggleBank Business Online or the ToggleBank Business app
2. Select "Open a Savings Account" from the accounts menu
3. Choose your preferred account type
4. Fund the account via transfer from your Business Current Account
5. Account opens instantly (Instant Access and Notice accounts) or within 1 business day (Fixed Rate Bond)

## At Maturity (Fixed Rate Bond)

Fourteen days before your bond matures, ToggleBank will notify you of your options:

- Renew into a new fixed rate bond at the prevailing rate
- Transfer the balance to your Business Instant Access Saver
- Transfer the balance to your Business Current Account

If no instruction is received within 14 days of maturity, the balance and interest will be transferred to your Business Current Account.

## FSCS Protection

Deposits are protected by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme up to **£85,000** per eligible depositor per banking group. This limit covers the total of all deposits held with ToggleBank, including current and savings accounts. Businesses qualifying as small companies under the Companies Act 2006 are eligible for FSCS protection.
