## Overview

ToggleBank Online Banking gives you secure, 24/7 access to your accounts from any web browser. Manage your money, pay bills, and view statements — all from the comfort of your home or office.

## Registration

To register for Online Banking, you will need:

- Your **ToggleBank sort code and account number**
- A **UK-registered mobile phone number**
- A **valid email address**
- Your **debit card and PIN** (for initial verification)

Register online at [togglebank.co.uk/register](https://togglebank.co.uk/register) or call **0345 678 9012**. You will receive a One-Time Passcode (OTP) via SMS to verify your identity during setup.

## Security & Two-Factor Authentication

All Online Banking sessions are protected by **Strong Customer Authentication (SCA)** in compliance with PSD2 regulations.

- **App-based authentication** — Approve login via a push notification sent to the ToggleBank Mobile App (recommended).
- **SMS authentication** — Receive a 6-digit One-Time Passcode (OTP) to your registered mobile number.

Sessions automatically time out after **5 minutes** of inactivity. All data is transmitted using **TLS 1.3 encryption**.

## Accessibility

ToggleBank Online Banking is designed to meet **WCAG 2.2 Level AA** standards:

- Full **screen reader** compatibility (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
- **Keyboard-only** navigation throughout
- Adjustable text size and **high-contrast mode**
- Captions and transcripts for all video content
- Accessible PDF statements available on request

If you require alternative formats or additional support, contact our Accessibility Team at **accessibility@togglebank.co.uk** or call **0345 678 9000** (Relay UK supported).
