## Tax-Free Savings

Interest earned in a Cash ISA is **completely free of UK income tax**, regardless of your tax band. Unlike standard savings accounts, ISA interest does not count towards your Personal Savings Allowance.

| Taxpayer Type | Standard Savings Allowance | Cash ISA Allowance |
|---|---|---|
| Basic rate (20%) | £1,000 tax-free | £20,000 contributions per year |
| Higher rate (40%) | £500 tax-free | £20,000 contributions per year |
| Additional rate (45%) | £0 tax-free | £20,000 contributions per year |

For higher and additional rate taxpayers, a Cash ISA is particularly valuable as it provides unlimited tax-free interest.

## Flexible ISA — How It Works

The ToggleBank Cash ISA is a **Flexible ISA**, which means:

- If you **withdraw** money during the tax year, you can **replace** it later in the same tax year without the replacement counting as a new subscription.
- This gives you the freedom to access your cash for short-term needs without permanently losing part of your annual allowance.
- Flexibility resets at the start of each new tax year (6 April).

**Example:** You have contributed £15,000 this tax year and withdraw £5,000 in July. You can replace the £5,000 later in the year AND still contribute an additional £5,000 (up to your £20,000 allowance).

## Transferring Your ISA

- **Transfer in** from other ISA providers (cash or stocks & shares) — we handle the transfer process for you, typically completed within 15 working days.
- Transferring preserves your ISA tax benefits and does not count towards the current year's allowance (for previous years' subscriptions).
- **Transfer out** to another provider is also available at any time.

## Eligibility

- Aged **18 or over** (16 or over for Cash ISAs held before 6 April 2024)
- **UK resident** for tax purposes
- You can only subscribe to **one Cash ISA** per tax year (but you can hold ISAs with multiple providers from different tax years)

## Interest Rate Information

- The rate of **4.25% AER (variable)** is the annual rate applied to the full balance.
- Interest is calculated daily on the cleared balance and credited monthly.
- As this is a variable rate, it may change. We will provide at least **60 days' personal notice** before any rate reduction.
- Rate includes a **0.50% introductory bonus** for the first 12 months. After the bonus period, the rate will revert to the standard Cash ISA rate (currently 3.75% AER variable).
