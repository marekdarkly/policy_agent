## Overview

We understand that dealing with finances after the death of a loved one can feel overwhelming. ToggleBank has a dedicated Bereavement Team of trained specialists who will guide you through every step with care and compassion.

## Notifying ToggleBank

Please let us know as soon as possible so we can secure the accounts and support you through the process.

| Method | Details |
|---|---|
| Phone | **0345 678 9030** (Bereavement Team, Mon–Fri 09:00–17:00) |
| In-branch | Visit any ToggleBank branch — ask to speak to a bereavement specialist |
| Post | Bereavement Team, ToggleBank, PO Box 4567, Leeds, LS1 2TW |

You do not need to be an account holder or next of kin to notify us of a death. We will confirm the relationship and required documentation when we speak to you.

## Documents Required

| Document | When Required |
|---|---|
| **Death certificate** (original or certified copy) | Always required |
| **Grant of Probate** (England/Wales) or **Confirmation** (Scotland) | Required for sole accounts over £50,000 or when there is a will |
| **Letters of Administration** | Required when there is no will (intestacy) |
| **ID of executor or administrator** | Passport, driving licence, or biometric residence permit |
| **The will** (certified copy) | If applicable |
| **Marriage/civil partnership certificate** | If the surviving partner needs access to a joint product |

We will return all original documents by registered post within **5 working days**, or you can collect them in-branch.

## What Happens to Accounts

### Joint Accounts

- The **surviving account holder retains full access** to the joint account immediately.
- The deceased's name will be removed from the account upon request (with death certificate).
- Any joint overdraft becomes the responsibility of the surviving account holder.

### Sole Accounts

- The account is **frozen** once we are notified of the death, to protect the funds.
- No withdrawals can be made until the appropriate legal authority (Grant of Probate or Letters of Administration) is provided.
- **Exception:** funeral expenses (see below).

### Savings Accounts, ISAs, and Investments

- ISAs retain their **tax-free status** during the administration period.
- Inherited ISA allowance (Additional Permitted Subscription) is available to a surviving spouse or civil partner.
- Investment accounts are valued at date of death for probate purposes. ToggleBank will provide a valuation letter.

## Funeral Expenses

ToggleBank can release funds **before Probate** to cover funeral expenses:

- Up to **£5,000** can be released from the deceased's sole account(s) upon receipt of:
  - A copy of the **death certificate**
  - An **itemised funeral invoice** from the funeral director
  - The **executor's or administrator's ID**
- Payment is made directly to the **funeral director** (not to the executor personally).
- If the account balance is below £5,000, the full balance may be released for this purpose.

## Outstanding Debts

| Debt Type | What Happens |
|---|---|
| Overdraft | Repaid from the estate before distribution |
| Credit card balance | Repaid from the estate; interest is frozen from the date of death notification |
| Personal loan | Repaid from the estate; if the loan had PPI or life cover, a claim may settle the balance |
| Mortgage (sole) | Repaid from the estate or claimed against life insurance (if applicable) |
| Mortgage (joint) | The surviving borrower assumes full responsibility; life insurance may cover the balance |

ToggleBank will **freeze all interest and charges** on the deceased's debts from the date we are notified.

## Closing Accounts & Distributing Funds

Once Probate has been granted:

1. The executor provides the **Grant of Probate** and their ID.
2. ToggleBank closes the accounts and calculates the **final balance** (including any accrued interest).
3. Funds are transferred to the **executor's nominated account** within **10 working days**.
4. A **final statement** is issued for the executor's records.

## Timescales

| Process | Typical Duration |
|---|---|

| Funeral expenses release | 3–5 working days |
| Removing a name from a joint account | 5 working days |
| Account closure & fund distribution (after Probate) | 10 working days |
| ISA valuation letter | 10 working days |

## Bereavement Team

Our Bereavement Team consists of specially trained staff who understand the sensitivity of the situation. They will:

- Act as your **single point of contact** throughout the process
- Provide clear, written guidance tailored to your specific circumstances
- Coordinate with other ToggleBank departments on your behalf
- Signpost you to external support services including bereavement counselling (Cruse Bereavement Support: 0808 808 1677)
