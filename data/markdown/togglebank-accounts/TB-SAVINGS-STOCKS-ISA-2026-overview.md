# ToggleBank Stocks & Shares ISA

**Product Code:** TB-SAVINGS-STOCKS-ISA-2026
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026
**Account Type:** Stocks & Shares Individual Savings Account

---

## Overview

The ToggleBank Stocks & Shares ISA gives you tax-efficient access to a wide range of investment options, from ready-made portfolios to individual shares. Any growth and income within the ISA wrapper is free from UK Capital Gains Tax and Income Tax, making it an ideal vehicle for long-term wealth building.

**Capital at risk.** The value of investments can go down as well as up, and you may get back less than you invest.

## Investment Options

### Funds and ETFs

Choose from over 3,000 funds and ETFs across all major asset classes and geographies:

- **UK equities** — FTSE 100, FTSE 250, and AIM-listed companies
- **Global equities** — US, European, Asian, and emerging market funds
- **Fixed income** — government bonds, corporate bonds, and strategic bond funds
- **Multi-asset** — diversified funds spanning equities, bonds, and alternatives
- **ESG and sustainable** — funds with environmental, social, and governance mandates
- **Money market** — lower-risk, cash-like funds for cautious investors

### Individual Shares

Trade individual shares listed on:

| Exchange | Share Dealing Fee |
|---|---|
| London Stock Exchange (Main Market, AIM) | £6 per trade |
| NYSE / NASDAQ | £6 per trade |
| European exchanges (selected) | £6 per trade |

Regular investing into shares is available from £25/month per holding with no dealing charge.

### Ready-Made Portfolios

For investors who prefer a managed approach, ToggleBank offers five **risk-rated ready-made portfolios**:

| Portfolio | Risk Level | Typical Equity Allocation |
|---|---|---|
| Cautious | Low | 20–35% |
| Moderately Cautious | Low–Medium | 35–50% |
| Balanced | Medium | 50–65% |
| Moderately Adventurous | Medium–High | 65–80% |
| Adventurous | High | 80–100% |

- Portfolios are managed by ToggleBank Investment Management and rebalanced quarterly.
- Underlying fund charges apply (typically 0.15%–0.35% OCF) in addition to the platform fee.

## Tax Benefits

- **No Capital Gains Tax** on profits within the ISA
- **No Income Tax** on dividends or interest within the ISA
- No need to declare ISA investments on your self-assessment tax return

## Eligibility

- Aged **18 or over**
- **UK resident** for tax purposes
- You can subscribe to one Stocks & Shares ISA per tax year

## How to Open

1. **Online** — Open at togglebank.co.uk/stocks-isa
2. **Mobile app** — Invest from the ToggleBank app
3. **Transfer** — Transfer existing ISAs from other providers (cash or in specie)
