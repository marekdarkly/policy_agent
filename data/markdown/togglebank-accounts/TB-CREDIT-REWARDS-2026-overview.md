# ToggleBank Rewards Credit Card

## Product Overview

The ToggleBank Rewards Credit Card is designed for everyday spending, giving you **1% cashback** on all purchases with no category restrictions. Enjoy an introductory 0% interest period on purchases and access to exclusive cardholder benefits including airport lounge access.

**Product Code:** TB-CREDIT-REWARDS-2026
**Card Network:** Visa Signature
**Issued by:** ToggleBank plc (authorised by the PRA, regulated by the FCA and PRA)

## Cashback Rewards Programme

- **1% cashback** earned on all purchases — no caps, no rotating categories
- Cashback is calculated monthly and credited to your statement each anniversary
- Minimum cashback redemption: £5
- Cashback can be applied as a statement credit or paid to a ToggleBank current account
- Cashback is forfeited if the account is closed with an outstanding balance or is in arrears

## Eligibility Requirements

- Minimum gross annual income: **£15,000**
- Must be a **UK resident** aged 18 or over
- Must have a UK bank account
- Subject to credit check and affordability assessment
- Not currently bankrupt or subject to an Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)

---

## Minimum Payments

- The greater of **2.5% of the outstanding balance** or **£25**
- If the total balance is less than £25, the full balance is due
- Interest accrues on the remaining balance if the statement balance is not paid in full
- Persistent debt notifications will be issued if only minimum payments are made for 18 consecutive months, in line with FCA regulations
