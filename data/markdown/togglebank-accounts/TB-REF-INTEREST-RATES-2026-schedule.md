## Rate Change Notification Policy

- **Variable rate changes** — ToggleBank will notify you at least **14 days** before any increase to a variable interest rate (or immediately for decreases).
- **Fixed rate products** — Your rate is guaranteed for the fixed period. Any change on maturity will be communicated at least **30 days** in advance.
- Notifications are sent by **email**, **in-app message**, and **letter** (if you have opted for postal correspondence).
