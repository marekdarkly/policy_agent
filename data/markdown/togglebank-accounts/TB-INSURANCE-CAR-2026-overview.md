## Cover Levels

| Cover Level | What's Included | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Comprehensive** | Damage to your car (accident, fire, theft, vandalism), third party injury and property damage, windscreen, courtesy car, personal accident, medical expenses | Most drivers — fullest protection |
| **Third Party, Fire & Theft** | Third party injury and property damage, fire damage to your car, theft of your car | Drivers wanting mid-level cover at a lower premium |
| **Third Party Only** | Third party injury and property damage only — no cover for your own vehicle | Minimum legal requirement — lowest premium |

## Named Driver Policies

- Add up to **4 named drivers** to your policy
- Each named driver must be disclosed with their licence details, claims history, and convictions
- Named drivers build their own no-claims history on request (letter of experience provided)
- Fronting (listing an inexperienced driver as a named driver when they are the main user) is fraud and will void the policy

## Telematics / Black Box Option

Designed for **young and new drivers** (ages 17–25) to help reduce premiums through safe driving:

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Discount Potential | Up to **30% discount** based on driving behaviour |
| How It Works | A small telematics device is fitted to your car (free installation) or an app-based alternative is available |
| What's Measured | Speed, braking, cornering, time of day, mileage |
| Scoring | Monthly driving score visible in the ToggleBank app |
| No Curfew | No restrictions on when you can drive — night-time driving affects your score but does not invalidate cover |
| Removal | Device can be removed free of charge at policy end |

## Eligibility

- Driver must be a **UK resident** aged **17 or over** with a valid UK, EU, or international driving licence (held for at least 12 months)
- Vehicle must be **registered and kept in the UK**
- Vehicle modifications must be declared
- All drivers, claims, and convictions in the last 5 years must be disclosed
