## Rental Income Requirements

To qualify for a ToggleBank Buy-to-Let Mortgage, the expected rental income from the property must meet the following stress tests:

| Borrower Type | Rental Coverage Ratio | Stress Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Individual (basic rate taxpayer) | 145% of mortgage payment | 5.5% |
| Individual (higher rate taxpayer) | 145% of mortgage payment | 5.5% |
| Limited company (SPV) | 130% of mortgage payment | 5.5% |

For example, if the mortgage payment at the 5.5% stress rate would be £1,000 per month, the rental income must be at least £1,450 per month for individual borrowers.

## Interest-Only Lending

Interest-only is the most common repayment method for buy-to-let mortgages. Monthly payments cover only the interest, with the full capital balance due at the end of the mortgage term. Acceptable repayment strategies include:

- Sale of the mortgaged property
- Sale of other investment properties
- Accumulated equity from capital growth
- Savings, investments, or pension funds

## Limited Company Lending

We offer buy-to-let mortgages to Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) — limited companies set up specifically for property investment. Benefits of borrowing through a limited company include potential tax efficiency, particularly for higher and additional rate taxpayers.

- Company must be a UK-registered SPV with SIC codes 68100, 68201, or 68209
- All directors and shareholders with 25%+ ownership must provide personal guarantees
- Trading companies and overseas companies are not eligible

## Tax Considerations for Landlords

The tax landscape for buy-to-let has changed significantly. Key points to be aware of:

- **Section 24 (mortgage interest relief):** Since April 2020, mortgage interest can no longer be deducted from rental income for individual landlords. Instead, you receive a 20% tax credit on mortgage interest payments. This particularly affects higher and additional rate taxpayers.
- **Stamp duty surcharge:** An additional 3% stamp duty land tax surcharge applies to purchases of additional residential properties (properties valued over £40,000).
- **Capital Gains Tax:** Gains on the sale of buy-to-let properties are subject to CGT at 18% (basic rate) or 24% (higher rate). The annual CGT allowance for 2025/26 is £3,000.

ToggleBank does not provide tax advice. We recommend consulting a qualified tax adviser before making buy-to-let investment decisions.

- Must already own your own home (either outright or with a mortgage)

- Minimum property value: £75,000
- Maximum 10 buy-to-let properties with ToggleBank (maximum aggregate BTL lending of £5,000,000)
