# ToggleBank Wealth Management

**Product Code:** TB-INVEST-WEALTH-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Regulated by:** Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)

## Overview

ToggleBank Wealth Management provides a comprehensive, personalised investment and financial planning service for clients with £250,000 or more in investable assets. Each client is paired with a dedicated investment manager who builds and manages a bespoke portfolio aligned with your objectives, risk tolerance, and tax position.

## Eligibility

- Minimum investable assets: **£250,000** (across all ToggleBank accounts)
- Available to individuals, joint applicants, trusts, and pension schemes
- Assets held with other providers can be consolidated into ToggleBank to meet the minimum threshold

## Service Model

| Feature | Discretionary Management | Advisory Management |
|---|---|---|
| Investment decisions | ToggleBank manages on your behalf | You approve each decision |
| Speed of execution | Immediate — no delay for approval | Dependent on your response time |
| Reporting | Quarterly valuations and reviews | Quarterly valuations and reviews |
| Best for | Clients who prefer a hands-off approach | Clients who want involvement in decisions |

Both service models include the full suite of financial planning and wealth management services.

## Dedicated Investment Manager

Your investment manager is your primary point of contact and is responsible for:

- Understanding your financial goals, obligations, and time horizon
- Constructing and managing your portfolio
- Proactively communicating market developments and portfolio changes
- Coordinating with your accountant, solicitor, or other advisers as needed
- Availability by phone, email, and video call during business hours

## Bespoke Portfolio Construction

Portfolios are individually constructed using a wide universe of investment instruments:

- UK and international equities (direct shareholdings)
- Fixed income (government bonds, investment-grade and high-yield corporate bonds)
- Collective investments (funds, ETFs, investment trusts)
- Alternative investments (commercial property, infrastructure, private equity funds)
- Structured products (capital-protected and growth-linked notes)
- Cash and money market instruments

Asset allocation is tailored to your specific objectives — no two client portfolios are identical.

Wealth Management clients have access to holistic financial planning covering:

| Planning Area | Services Included |

| **Retirement planning** | Income projections, pension drawdown strategy, annuity comparison |

## Review Schedule

| Review Type | Frequency | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Portfolio performance review | Quarterly | Written report + call or meeting |
| Investment strategy review | Quarterly | Discussion with investment manager |

Between reviews, your investment manager monitors your portfolio daily and will contact you proactively if action is recommended.

## Commitment and Notice

- ToggleBank recommends a minimum **5-year investment horizon** for wealth management clients
- There is no lock-in period — you may withdraw funds or close the service at any time
- 30 days' written notice to terminate the wealth management agreement
- Partial withdrawals can be processed within 5 business days
