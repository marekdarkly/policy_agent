# ToggleBank Easy Access Savings Account

**Product Code:** TB-SAVINGS-EASY-2026
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026
**Account Type:** Easy Access Savings Account

---

## Overview

The ToggleBank Easy Access Savings Account offers a competitive variable rate with complete freedom to deposit and withdraw your money whenever you need it. With no minimum balance, no fixed term, and no withdrawal limits, it's a straightforward way to earn interest on your savings.

## Key Features

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Interest rate | **3.75% AER (variable)** |
| Access | **Instant** — withdraw any time, no notice required |
| Withdrawal limits | **Unlimited** — no restrictions |
| Minimum balance | **£0** (no minimum) |
| Maximum balance | £1,000,000 per account |
| Accounts per person | Up to **5** Easy Access accounts |
| Interest payment | **Monthly** (credited to the account or to a nominated account) |
| FSCS protection | Yes — up to £85,000 |

## Interest Details

| Balance Tier | AER (Variable) |
|---|---|
| £0.01 – £1,000,000 | **3.75%** |

- Interest is calculated daily on the cleared balance and paid on the last working day of each month.
- You can choose to have interest paid into the savings account (compounding) or into a nominated ToggleBank current account.
- The rate is variable and may change. We will give at least **60 days' personal notice** before any reduction.

## Why Choose Easy Access?

- **No lock-in** — your money is never tied up, unlike fixed-rate bonds or notice accounts
- **No penalties** — withdraw as much or as little as you want, as often as you want, with no charges
- **Emergency fund** — ideal for building an emergency fund of 3–6 months' expenses
- **Short-term savings** — save for upcoming expenses like holidays, home improvements, or a new car
- **Multiple accounts** — open up to 5 accounts to organise savings by goal (e.g., "Holiday Fund", "Emergency Fund", "New Car")

## Tax on Savings Interest

Interest on this account is **paid gross** (without tax deducted). However, it may be subject to income tax depending on your circumstances:

| Tax Band | Personal Savings Allowance |
|---|---|
| Basic rate (20%) | £1,000 tax-free interest per year |
| Higher rate (40%) | £500 tax-free interest per year |
| Additional rate (45%) | £0 tax-free interest |

If your total savings interest exceeds your Personal Savings Allowance, HMRC will usually collect tax owed through your tax code. For tax-free savings, consider the ToggleBank Cash ISA.
