**Product Code:** TB-STUDENT-CURR-2026

## Overview

The ToggleBank Student Current Account is designed specifically for university students, offering fee-free banking throughout your studies and for one year after graduation. With an interest-free overdraft that grows each year, a free Railcard, and cashback perks, it's built to support you financially while you focus on your degree.

## Key Features

| Feature | Detail |

| UK ATM withdrawals | Free |

| Apple Pay / Google Pay | Supported |
| Contactless limit | £100 per transaction |
| Interest on credit balances | 0.00% AER |

## Interest-Free Overdraft

One of the standout benefits of the Student Current Account is the **interest-free arranged overdraft** that builds over your three years of study:

| Academic Year | Maximum Interest-Free Overdraft |
|---|---|
| Year 1 | **£1,000** |
| Year 2 | **£2,000** |
| Year 3 | **£3,000** |
| Year after graduation (grace year) | £3,000 (reducing) |

- The overdraft limit increases automatically at the start of each academic year, subject to continued eligibility.
- During the **grace year** (the first 12 months after your course ends), your interest-free overdraft is maintained at £3,000 and then gradually reduced over the following 12 months.
- After the grace period, any remaining overdraft will convert to the standard Everyday Current Account arranged overdraft rate (currently 39.9% EAR variable).
- Overdraft approval is subject to status and affordability assessment.

## Free 16–25 Railcard

- Receive a **free 16–25 Railcard** (worth £30) when you open your account, giving you **1/3 off rail fares** across Great Britain for the first year.
- The Railcard is delivered digitally to the Railcard app within 7 days of account opening.
- Renewal in subsequent years is at your own cost.

|---|---|
| Supermarkets (selected) | Up to 2% |
| Coffee shops (selected) | Up to 5% |

- Participating retailers are listed in the ToggleBank app and may change from time to time.

## Eligibility

To open a Student Current Account, you must:

- Be aged **18–25** at the time of application
- Be enrolled (or have an unconditional offer) on a full-time course of at least **2 years** at a **UK university or higher education institution**
- Provide a valid UCAS number or university enrolment confirmation
- Be a UK resident with a UK address
- Pass our identity verification checks

## How to Apply

1. **Online** — Apply at togglebank.co.uk/student with your UCAS number or enrolment letter
2. **Mobile app** — Download the ToggleBank app and apply with photo ID
3. **In branch** — Bring your university offer letter or student ID, plus photo ID and proof of address
4. **CASS** — Switch from your existing account in 7 working days via the Current Account Switch Service

## After Graduation

- Your account automatically converts to a ToggleBank **Everyday Current Account** after the 12-month grace period.
- Alternatively, if you meet the eligibility criteria, you can upgrade to a **Premium Current Account** or apply for any other ToggleBank product.
- We will write to you before your grace year ends to explain your options.
