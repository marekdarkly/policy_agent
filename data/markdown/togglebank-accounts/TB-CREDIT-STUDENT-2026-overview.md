# ToggleBank Student Credit Card

## Product Overview

The ToggleBank Student Credit Card is designed to help UK university students **build a positive credit history** responsibly. With a manageable credit limit, no annual fee, free credit score monitoring, and built-in budgeting tools, it's the smart first step towards long-term financial health.

**Product Code:** TB-CREDIT-STUDENT-2026
**Card Network:** Visa Classic
**Issued by:** ToggleBank plc (authorised by the PRA, regulated by the FCA and PRA)

| Detail | Information |
|---|---|

- Making at least the minimum payment on time every month
- Staying within your credit limit
- Not using the card for cash advances regularly

## Eligibility Requirements

- Must be **aged 18–25**
- Must be **enrolled at a UK university** (undergraduate or postgraduate)
- Must have a UK residential address
- Must have a UK bank account (ToggleBank or other provider)
- No minimum income requirement
- Subject to a basic credit and identity check
- UCAS confirmation or university enrolment letter may be requested

---

## Minimum Payments

- The greater of **2.5% of the outstanding balance** or **£5**
- If the total balance is less than £5, the full balance is due
- Setting up a Direct Debit for at least the minimum payment is strongly recommended to avoid missed payments
