# ToggleBank Merchant Services

**Product Code:** TB-BUSINESS-MERCHANT-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Regulated by:** Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)

## Overview

ToggleBank Merchant Services enables businesses to accept card payments in-store, online, and on the go. With next-day settlement to your ToggleBank Business Current Account, competitive transaction rates, and full PCI DSS compliance, ToggleBank Merchant Services is designed to help businesses of all sizes get paid quickly and securely.

## Accepted Payment Methods

- Visa (credit and debit)
- Mastercard (credit and debit)
- American Express
- Contactless (up to £100 per transaction)
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Samsung Pay

## Channel Options

| Channel | Equipment | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **In-store — Countertop** | Wired terminal with PIN pad | Fixed retail locations, reception desks |
| **In-store — Portable** | Wireless terminal (Wi-Fi/Bluetooth) | Restaurants, salons, pop-up events |
| **Online** | Payment gateway integration | E-commerce, subscription services |
| **Mobile** | Compact card reader + smartphone app | Market traders, tradespeople, delivery |

## Transaction Rates

| Card Type | In-Store Rate | Online Rate |
|---|---|---|
| UK consumer debit (Visa/Mastercard) | 1.25% | 1.40% |
| UK consumer credit (Visa/Mastercard) | 1.50% | 1.65% |
| American Express | 1.95% | 2.10% |
| International / commercial cards | 2.50% | 2.75% |
| Contactless / mobile wallet | Same as underlying card type | N/A |

- No minimum monthly transaction requirement
- No setup fee for existing ToggleBank Business Current Account holders
- Terminal rental from £15/month (countertop) or £20/month (portable)
- Mobile card reader: one-off purchase of £29 + VAT

## Settlement

- **Next-day settlement** — funds from card transactions appear in your ToggleBank Business Current Account by the next business day
- Settlement to non-ToggleBank accounts is available but takes 2–3 business days
- Weekend and bank holiday transactions settle on the next business day

## Online Payment Gateway

ToggleBank's payment gateway integrates with leading e-commerce platforms:

- **Shopify** — plug-in available from the Shopify App Store
- **WooCommerce** — free WordPress plugin
- **Magento** — extension available
- **Custom integration** — RESTful API with full developer documentation at developer.togglebank.co.uk

Gateway features include:

- Hosted payment pages (fully customisable branding)
- Tokenisation for repeat customers
- 3D Secure 2.0 (Strong Customer Authentication compliant)
- Recurring billing and subscription management
- Multi-currency acceptance — 25+ currencies with automatic conversion to GBP

## Multi-Currency Acceptance

Accept payments in over 25 currencies. Transactions are automatically converted to GBP at ToggleBank's daily exchange rate plus a 1.5% conversion margin. Supported currencies include EUR, USD, AUD, CAD, CHF, JPY, SEK, NOK, DKK, HKD, SGD, NZD, PLN, CZK, and more.

## Security and Compliance

- **PCI DSS Level 1** certified — the highest level of payment security
- **Fraud monitoring** — real-time transaction screening powered by machine learning
- **Chargeback management** — dedicated support team to assist with dispute resolution
- **End-to-end encryption** — all cardholder data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Velocity checks** — configurable rules to flag unusual transaction patterns

## Reporting Dashboard

Access the ToggleBank Merchant Portal for:

- Real-time transaction monitoring
- Daily, weekly, and monthly settlement reports
- Refund processing
- Chargeback tracking
- Export to CSV/Excel for reconciliation
- Multi-site reporting for businesses with multiple locations

## How to Apply

1. Hold or open a ToggleBank Business Current Account
2. Apply online at togglebank.co.uk/merchant-services or call 0345 678 9013
3. Provide business details — trading name, registered address, estimated monthly card turnover
4. Complete identity verification for all directors/partners
5. Terminal delivery within 5 business days; payment gateway activation within 2 business days
