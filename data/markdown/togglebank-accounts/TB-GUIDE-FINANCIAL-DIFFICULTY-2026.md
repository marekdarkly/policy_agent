## Overview

If you are struggling to keep up with payments or manage your finances, ToggleBank is here to help. We will treat you with **no judgement** and all conversations are **strictly confidential**. There is no shame in asking for support — it is the responsible thing to do.

## How We Can Help

| Support Option | Description |
|---|---|
| Breathing Space | 60-day protection from creditor action, interest, and charges (Standard or Mental Health Breathing Space) |
| Payment holidays | Temporary pause on loan or mortgage repayments (typically 1–3 months) |
| Reduced payment arrangements | Lower monthly payments on credit cards, loans, or overdrafts for an agreed period |
| Interest/charge freezes | Freeze or reduce interest and charges on your account to help you manage debt |
| Extended loan terms | Restructure your loan over a longer term to reduce monthly payments |
| Overdraft review | Reassess your overdraft limit and create a plan to reduce it gradually |

## Breathing Space Scheme

The Breathing Space (Debt Respite) Scheme gives you time to get debt advice without creditors taking enforcement action:

- **Standard Breathing Space:** 60 days of protection. Interest, fees, and charges on debts are frozen. Creditors must stop all enforcement action.
- **Mental Health Crisis Breathing Space:** Lasts for the duration of treatment plus 30 days. Available through an Approved Mental Health Professional.
- Contact a **debt advice provider** (see below) to apply — ToggleBank cannot apply on your behalf, but we will fully co-operate once notified.

## Payment Holidays

- Available on **personal loans** and **mortgages**.
- Typical duration: **1 to 3 months** (assessed case-by-case).
- Interest continues to accrue during the holiday, increasing the total amount repayable.
- Not available on credit cards (reduced payment arrangements offered instead).
- To request, call **0345 678 9025** or use secure messaging.

## Impact on Your Credit File

It is important to understand how financial difficulty support may affect your credit record:

| Arrangement | Impact on Credit File |
|---|---|
| Payment holiday (COVID-era) | No adverse reporting |
| Payment holiday (standard) | May be reported as "arrangement to pay" depending on duration |
| Reduced payment arrangement | Reported as "arrangement to pay" — this may affect future borrowing |
| Interest/charge freeze | Not directly reported, but missed payments prior to the arrangement may appear |
| Breathing Space | A flag is placed; this is not itself negative but is visible to lenders |
| Default | Registered after sustained non-payment (typically 3–6 missed payments); stays on file for 6 years |

We will always explain the credit file impact **before** any arrangement is put in place, so you can make an informed decision.

## Vulnerability Support

ToggleBank provides additional support for customers in vulnerable circumstances:

- **Accessible formats** — Large print, Braille, and audio for all correspondence
- **British Sign Language (BSL) interpreters** — Available in-branch by appointment and via video relay
- **Speech relay service** — For customers with speech or hearing difficulties (Relay UK)
- **Nominated contacts** — Appoint a trusted person to speak to us on your behalf for specific queries
- **Power of Attorney** — Full support for registered attorneys (see TB-GUIDE-POWER-OF-ATTORNEY-2026)
- **TEXAS protocol** — All staff trained in recognising and responding to vulnerability using the TEXAS framework (Thank, Explain, eXplicit consent, Ask, Signpost)
- **Specialist training** — Our Financial Support team is trained by mental health charities and debt advice organisations

## Getting in Touch

You can reach our dedicated **Financial Support Team**:

- **Phone:** 0345 678 9025 (Mon–Fri, 08:00–20:00; Sat, 09:00–14:00)
- **Secure messaging:** Select "Financial difficulty" as the topic
- **In-branch:** Ask to speak to a Financial Support Advisor (available by appointment)

You do not need to explain your situation in detail to reception staff — simply say you need help with payments and you will be connected to a trained specialist.
