## COVID-19 Cover

- **Cancellation** if you or a travelling companion test positive within 14 days of departure
- **Medical expenses** if you contract COVID-19 during your trip
- **Quarantine accommodation** costs up to £1,500 if required to self-isolate abroad
- **FCDO travel advisory changes** — cancellation covered if a "do not travel" advisory is issued after booking

## Pre-Existing Medical Conditions

- Many common conditions covered **at no extra cost** (e.g. controlled asthma, high blood pressure managed by medication)
- Online medical screening during the quote process
- Conditions not covered as standard can often be included for an additional premium
- All travellers on the policy must complete medical screening
