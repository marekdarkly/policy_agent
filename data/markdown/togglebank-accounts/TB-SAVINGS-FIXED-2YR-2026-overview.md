## Overview

The ToggleBank 2-Year Fixed Rate Bond locks in a **guaranteed interest rate** for 24 months, providing long-term certainty on your savings returns. Ideal for savers who are confident they won't need immediate access to their funds and want to benefit from a competitive fixed rate over a longer horizon.

## Interest Options

Choose your preferred interest payment method at the point of account opening:

| Option | Rate | How It Works |
|---|---|---|
| **At maturity** | 4.35% AER | Interest accrues daily and is paid as a lump sum after 24 months. Maximises total return through compounding. |
| **Monthly** | 4.26% AER | Interest is paid monthly into a nominated ToggleBank current or savings account. Provides regular income. |

Your chosen option is fixed for the full term and cannot be changed.

## Comparing 1-Year and 2-Year Fixed Rate Bonds

| Feature | 1-Year Fixed Bond | 2-Year Fixed Bond |
|---|---|---|
| Interest rate (at maturity) | 4.50% AER | 4.35% AER |
| Interest rate (monthly) | 4.41% AER | 4.26% AER |
| Term | 12 months | 24 months |
| Rate certainty | 1 year | 2 years |
| Best for | Shorter commitment, higher rate | Longer certainty, protection against rate falls |

While the 1-Year Bond offers a marginally higher rate, the 2-Year Bond provides **double the duration of rate certainty** — valuable if interest rates are expected to fall.

## Deposit Rules

- Fund your bond with one or more deposits within **14 days** of opening.
- No additional deposits are accepted after the 14-day funding window.
- Funds must originate from a UK bank account held in your name.

## Withdrawal Restrictions

- **No withdrawals are permitted** during the 24-month fixed term.
- Exceptions apply only for:
  - **Death** of the account holder
  - **Serious financial hardship** (at our discretion)
  - **Court order**

If early access is granted in exceptional circumstances, an early closure charge equivalent to **180 days' interest** may be applied.

## What Happens at Maturity

We will contact you at least **14 days before maturity** to discuss your options:

1. **Reinvest** into a new Fixed Rate Bond at the current rate
2. **Transfer** to a ToggleBank savings or current account
3. **Withdraw** to an external bank account

If no instructions are received, proceeds will be transferred to a ToggleBank Easy Access Savings Account at the prevailing rate.

## Eligibility

- Aged **18 or over**
- **UK resident** with a valid UK address
- Must hold or open a ToggleBank current account
