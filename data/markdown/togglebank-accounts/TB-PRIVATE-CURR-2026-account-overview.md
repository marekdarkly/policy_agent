## Dedicated Private Banker

Every Private Banking client is paired with a **dedicated private banker** — a senior relationship professional based in the UK who serves as your single point of contact for all banking and financial matters.

Your private banker can assist with:

- Holistic financial planning and wealth structuring
- Bespoke lending solutions (including property finance, lombard lending, and bridging)
- Tailored investment guidance in conjunction with our investment specialists
- Estate and succession planning introductions
- Cross-border banking coordination
- Priority and same-day account services

Your private banker is contactable directly by phone, email, or secure message, and will proactively reach out to discuss opportunities and reviews.

## Multi-Currency Banking

The Private Banking Current Account supports multiple currencies, allowing you to hold, send, and receive funds in:

| Currency | Features |
|---|---|
| **GBP** (British Pound) | Primary account currency |
| **EUR** (Euro) | Hold balances, send/receive SEPA payments |
| **USD** (US Dollar) | Hold balances, international wires |
| **CHF** (Swiss Franc) | Hold balances, international wires |

- Currency conversions between held currencies are executed at **preferential interbank rates** with no markup.
- Additional currencies may be available on request.

## Bespoke Lending

Private Banking clients have access to flexible, individually structured lending:

- **Overdraft facilities** — arranged on bespoke terms; rates significantly below standard products
- **Lombard lending** — borrow against your investment portfolio at preferential rates
- **Property finance** — bespoke mortgage solutions for primary residences, second homes, and investment properties, with higher loan values available
- **Bridging finance** — short-term lending to support property chains or time-sensitive opportunities

All lending is subject to individual assessment, affordability, and security requirements.

## Eligibility

The Private Banking Current Account is available to individuals who meet **one or more** of the following criteria:

- **By invitation** from ToggleBank based on your existing relationship
- **£250,000 or more** in savings, investments, or assets held with ToggleBank
- **Significant income** or professional standing (assessed individually)

Additional requirements:

- Aged 18 or over
- UK resident (international clients considered on a case-by-case basis)
- Satisfactory identity verification and enhanced due diligence checks
