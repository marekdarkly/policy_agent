# ToggleBank Business Current Account

**Product Code:** TB-BUSINESS-CURR-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Regulated by:** Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA)

## Overview

The ToggleBank Business Current Account is designed for UK-based businesses of all sizes, from sole traders to limited companies. It provides everyday banking with integrated tools to help manage cash flow, send invoices, and connect to popular accounting platforms.

## Eligibility

- Sole traders
- Partnerships
- Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs)
- Limited companies registered with Companies House
- Business must be UK-registered with a UK trading address
- At least one director or partner must be a UK resident

## Monthly Fee

| Condition | Monthly Fee |
|---|---|
| First 18 months after account opening | **Free** |
| After 18 months — balance below £5,000 | **£7.50/month** |
| After 18 months — balance £5,000 or above | **Free** |

The monthly fee is assessed on the last business day of each calendar month based on the closing balance.

## Transaction Fees

| Transaction Type | Fee |
|---|---|
| Faster Payments (in and out) | Free |
| Direct Debits (set up and payments) | Free |
| Standing orders | Free |
| Cheque deposits | Free |
| CHAPS payments | £20 per payment |
| Cash deposits — first £1,000/month | Free |
| Cash deposits — above £1,000/month | 0.7% of the excess |
| Cash withdrawals at ToggleBank ATMs | Free |
| Cash withdrawals at non-ToggleBank ATMs | £0.65 per withdrawal |
| International payments | See International Business Services schedule |

Cash deposit allowances reset on the 1st of each calendar month and do not roll over.

## Included Features

- **Dedicated UK-based business support team** — available Monday to Friday, 8am–8pm and Saturday, 9am–2pm
- **ToggleBank Business app** — real-time balance alerts, instant payment approvals, receipt scanning
- **Multi-user access** — add up to 10 users with customisable permissions (view-only, payment initiator, full admin)
- **Invoicing tools** — create, send, and track invoices directly from the app or online banking
- **Accounting software integration** — automatic bank feed connections to:
  - Xero
  - QuickBooks
  - FreeAgent
  - Sage Business Cloud
- **Business debit card** — Visa Business, contactless-enabled, Apple Pay and Google Pay compatible
- **Spending insights** — automatic categorisation of business expenditure

## Overdraft

An arranged business overdraft is available subject to credit assessment.

| Overdraft Limit | Representative EAR |
|---|---|
| Up to £5,000 | 15.9% EAR (variable) |
| £5,001–£25,000 | 12.9% EAR (variable) |
| Over £25,000 | By arrangement |

- No arrangement fee for overdrafts under £10,000
- Unauthorised borrowing fee: £5/day (capped at £35/month)

## How to Apply

1. Apply online at togglebank.co.uk/business or via the ToggleBank Business app
2. Provide identification for all directors/partners (passport or UK driving licence)
3. Provide proof of business address (utility bill or business rates letter, dated within 3 months)
4. For limited companies: certificate of incorporation and memorandum of association
5. Decision typically within 1 business day; account ready to use within 3 business days

## Important Information

- ToggleBank plc is authorised by the PRA and regulated by the FCA and PRA (Financial Services Register No. 204578)
- Deposits are protected by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) up to £85,000 per eligible depositor per banking group
- Business accounts are eligible for FSCS protection where the business qualifies as a small company, charity, or unincorporated association under FSCS rules
