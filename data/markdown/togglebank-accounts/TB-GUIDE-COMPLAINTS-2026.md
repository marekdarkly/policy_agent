# ToggleBank Complaints Process Guide

**Document Reference:** TB-GUIDE-COMPLAINTS-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Classification:** Customer-Facing

---

## Overview

ToggleBank is committed to providing excellent service, but we recognise that things can sometimes go wrong. If you are unhappy with any aspect of our service, we want to hear from you so we can put things right.

## How to Complain

| Channel | Details |
|---|---|
| In-branch | Speak to any member of staff at your local ToggleBank branch |
| Phone | **0345 678 9020** (dedicated complaints line, Mon–Fri 08:00–20:00, Sat 09:00–14:00) |
| Email | complaints@togglebank.co.uk |
| Letter | Customer Relations Team, ToggleBank, PO Box 4567, Leeds, LS1 2TW |
| Secure messaging | Via Online Banking or Mobile App (select "Make a complaint") |

## What Happens Next

### Step 1 — Acknowledgement

- You will receive an **acknowledgement** of your complaint promptly, usually within **1 working day**.
- You will be given a unique **complaint reference number** for tracking.

### Step 2 — Investigation

- A dedicated **case handler** will be assigned to investigate your complaint.
- They may contact you to gather further information or clarify details.

### Step 3 — Resolution

We aim to resolve complaints as follows:

| Complaint Type | Target Response Time |
|---|---|
| Payment-related complaints (e.g. failed payments, unauthorised transactions) | **15 business days** (extendable to 35 days in exceptional circumstances) |
| All other complaints | **8 weeks** |

- You will receive a **final response letter** setting out our findings, any action we have taken, and any redress offered.

## Tracking Your Complaint

- Track the status of your complaint online at [togglebank.co.uk/complaints/track](https://togglebank.co.uk/complaints/track) using your complaint reference number.
- You can also check progress by calling the dedicated complaints line or via secure messaging.

## If You Are Not Satisfied

If you are unhappy with our final response, or if **8 weeks** have passed since you first complained and you have not received a final response, you can refer your complaint to the **Financial Ombudsman Service (FOS)** free of charge.

### Financial Ombudsman Service (FOS)

| Contact Method | Details |
|---|---|
| Phone | **0800 023 4567** (free from mobiles and landlines) or **0300 123 9123** |
| Email | complaint.info@financial-ombudsman.org.uk |
| Website | [financial-ombudsman.org.uk](https://financial-ombudsman.org.uk) |
| Post | Financial Ombudsman Service, Exchange Tower, London, E14 9SR |

You must refer your complaint to the FOS **within 6 months** of receiving our final response letter. The FOS is an independent body that resolves disputes between financial businesses and their customers.

## Our Commitment

ToggleBank takes all complaints seriously. We use complaint data to improve our products and services. Key commitments:

- Every complaint is treated **fairly and impartially**.
- We will not treat you differently for making a complaint.
- Vulnerable customers receive **additional support** throughout the process.
- We publish **annual complaints data** on our website in line with FCA requirements, including the number of complaints received, the proportion resolved within timescales, and the main areas of complaint.

## Compensation & Redress

Where we have made an error, we may offer:

- **Refund of charges** incorrectly applied
- **Correction of credit file** entries if affected by our error
- **Goodwill payment** for distress and inconvenience (assessed on a case-by-case basis)
- **Interest** on money you were out-of-pocket (at 8% simple per annum, the FOS standard rate)
