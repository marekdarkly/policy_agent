## Eligibility

- Aged 18 or over (mortgage term must end before your 75th birthday)
- UK resident with the right to reside in the UK
- Must be your first property purchase (neither applicant can have previously owned residential property)
- Minimum income: £20,000 per annum (sole) or £30,000 combined (joint)
- Maximum borrowing: 4.5× gross annual income (up to 5.5× for applicants earning over £75,000, subject to affordability assessment)
- Satisfactory credit history — a credit check will be performed

## Repayment Options

- **Capital repayment:** Monthly payments cover both the interest and a portion of the capital, so the mortgage is fully repaid by the end of the term. This is the most common choice for first-time buyers.
- **Interest-only:** Monthly payments cover only the interest. You must have an acceptable repayment plan to repay the capital at the end of the term (e.g., ISA, pension, investment portfolio, or sale of another property). Interest-only is not available above 75% LTV for first-time buyers.

## How to Apply

1. **Get a Decision in Principle** — Apply online at togglebank.co.uk/mortgages, by phone, or in-branch. You'll receive a decision in as little as 15 minutes. A DIP is valid for 90 days and does not commit you to borrowing.
2. **Find your property** — Once you have your DIP, you can house-hunt with confidence, knowing your budget.
3. **Submit a full application** — When you've had an offer accepted on a property, your mortgage adviser will help you complete the full application.
4. **Valuation and underwriting** — We'll arrange a valuation of the property and complete our checks. This stage typically takes 1–2 weeks.
5. **Mortgage offer issued** — Once approved, we'll issue a formal mortgage offer, usually valid for 6 months.
