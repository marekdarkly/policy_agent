# ToggleBank Life Insurance

## Product Overview

ToggleBank Life Insurance provides financial security for your loved ones if you die during the policy term. Choose from **level term**, **decreasing term**, or **whole of life** cover, with optional critical illness protection and a free trust setup service. A dedicated bereavement support service is included with every policy.

## What's Included as Standard

- **Terminal illness cover**: receive your full payout early if diagnosed with a terminal illness and given fewer than 12 months to live (not available on Over-50s Plan)
- **Bereavement support service**: free access to a 24/7 bereavement helpline and up to 6 counselling sessions for your beneficiaries, provided by an independent specialist partner
- **Free trust setup**: place your policy in trust so the payout goes directly to your beneficiaries without going through probate — faster payment and potentially reduced inheritance tax
- **Waiver of premium** (first 12 months free): if you cannot work due to illness or injury, your premiums are waived until you recover

## Optional Add-Ons

| Add-On | Details |
|---|---|
| **Critical Illness Cover** | Pays a lump sum if you are diagnosed with one of **48 specified critical illnesses** (e.g. cancer, heart attack, stroke). Can be added to Level or Decreasing Term policies. Increases the monthly premium. |
| **Children's Critical Illness** | Covers your children (from 30 days to age 21) for a range of critical illnesses — pays £25,000 per child. Free when adult critical illness cover is selected. |
| **Fracture Cover** | Lump sum payments for broken bones — from £500 (minor fractures) to £5,000 (major fractures). |

---

## Joint Policies

- Available on Level Term and Decreasing Term policies
- Two options:
  - **First death**: pays out on the first death — the policy then ends
  - **Dual life**: pays out on each death separately — effectively two individual policies at a discounted rate
- Joint policies are typically 10–15% cheaper than two separate individual policies

## Underwriting and Application

- **Instant decision** for most applicants (Level and Decreasing Term up to £500,000)
- Online medical questionnaire — no GP report or medical exam for most applications
- GP report or nurse screening may be required for higher cover amounts or complex medical histories
- Smoker and non-smoker rates available (non-smoker defined as tobacco-free for 12+ months)

---

## Premium Factors

Your premium is based on:

- Age at application
- Cover amount and policy term
- Smoking status
- Health and medical history
- Occupation and lifestyle activities (e.g. hazardous sports)
- BMI
