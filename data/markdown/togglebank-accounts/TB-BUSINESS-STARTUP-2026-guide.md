## Choosing the Right Business Structure

| Structure | Liability | Tax | Registration | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Sole Trader** | Unlimited personal liability | Income Tax + National Insurance via Self Assessment | Register with HMRC | Freelancers, consultants, sole operators |
| **Partnership** | Unlimited personal liability (each partner) | Each partner pays Income Tax on their share | Register with HMRC; partnership agreement recommended | Two or more people sharing profits |
| **Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)** | Limited to capital invested | Each member pays Income Tax on their share | Register with Companies House | Professional services firms |
| **Limited Company** | Limited to share capital | Corporation Tax on profits; dividends taxed on shareholders | Register with Companies House | Most trading businesses seeking liability protection |

ToggleBank can open accounts for all four structures. Consider taking professional advice from an accountant or solicitor before deciding.

## Registering Your Business

**Sole traders:** Register for Self Assessment with HMRC at gov.uk/register-for-self-assessment. You can trade under your own name or a business name.

**Limited companies and LLPs:** Register with Companies House at gov.uk/set-up-limited-company. You will need:

- A company name (checked against existing names at Companies House)
- A registered office address in England, Wales, Scotland, or Northern Ireland
- At least one director (who must be aged 16 or over)
- Details of shareholders and share capital (for limited companies)
- A memorandum of association and articles of association
- Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) code for your business activity

Registration costs £12 online (or £30 by post) and typically completes within 24 hours online.

## VAT Registration

You must register for VAT if your taxable turnover exceeds **£90,000** in any 12-month rolling period (2025/26 threshold). You may register voluntarily below this threshold if it benefits your business (e.g., to reclaim VAT on purchases).

- Register online at gov.uk/vat-registration
- VAT returns are filed quarterly (or monthly if preferred)
- ToggleBank's accounting software integrations support MTD-compatible VAT filing

## Making Tax Digital (MTD)

Under MTD rules, businesses must keep digital records and submit returns using MTD-compatible software.

- **MTD for VAT** — mandatory for all VAT-registered businesses
- **MTD for Income Tax Self Assessment (ITSA)** — mandatory from April 2026 for sole traders and landlords with income over £50,000

ToggleBank's integrations with Xero, QuickBooks, FreeAgent, and Sage ensure your records and submissions are MTD-compliant.

## Insurance Requirements

Depending on your business, you may legally or practically need:

- **Employers' liability insurance** — legally required if you employ staff (minimum £5m cover)
- **Public liability insurance** — covers injury or damage to third parties
- **Professional indemnity insurance** — essential for consultants, advisors, and regulated professions
- **Contents and equipment insurance** — protects business assets
- **Cyber insurance** — covers data breaches and cyber incidents

ToggleBank partners with selected insurers to offer discounted business insurance. Visit togglebank.co.uk/business-insurance.
