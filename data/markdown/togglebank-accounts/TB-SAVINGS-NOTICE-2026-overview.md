## Overview

The ToggleBank 90-Day Notice Savings Account offers a **higher interest rate than instant access** in exchange for giving 90 days' notice before making a withdrawal. It's the ideal middle ground for savers who want a better return than easy access but don't want to lock their money away for a full fixed term.

## How the Notice Period Works

To make a withdrawal from this account, you must give **90 calendar days' notice**:

1. **Submit a notice** — via the mobile app, online banking, in branch, or by phone
2. **Wait 90 days** — your notice period begins the day after you submit it
3. **Funds released** — on the 91st day, the requested amount is transferred to your nominated account

| Step | Timeline |
|---|---|
| Day 1 | You submit a withdrawal notice for £5,000 |
| Days 2–90 | Notice period — funds continue earning interest at the full rate |
| Day 91 | £5,000 is transferred to your nominated account |

- You can have **multiple notices running simultaneously**.
- You can cancel a notice at any time before the funds are released.
- There is no option for immediate or penalty-based early withdrawal.

## When Is a Notice Account Right for You?

A 90-Day Notice Account is well suited for:

- **Medium-term savings** — money you don't need today but may need within the next year
- **Planned expenses** — if you know you'll need funds in a few months (e.g., a holiday, home renovation, or tax bill), give notice in advance
- **Earning more than easy access** — benefit from a higher rate while retaining the ability to access your funds
- **Supplement to emergency fund** — keep 3 months' expenses in easy access and additional savings in a notice account for a blended approach

## Deposits

- **No limit** on the number or value of deposits (up to the £1,000,000 account maximum).
- Deposits can be made by bank transfer, standing order, or from your ToggleBank current account via the app.
- The minimum balance of £1,000 must be maintained at all times. If a withdrawal would reduce the balance below £1,000, you must close the account instead.

Interest is paid **gross**. Depending on your tax band and Personal Savings Allowance, you may owe income tax on interest earned. For tax-free savings, consider the ToggleBank Cash ISA.
