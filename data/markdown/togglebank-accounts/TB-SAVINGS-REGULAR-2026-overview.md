# ToggleBank Regular Saver Account

**Product Code:** TB-SAVINGS-REGULAR-2026
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026
**Account Type:** Regular Savings Account (Fixed Rate, 12 Months)

---

## Overview

The ToggleBank Regular Saver rewards consistent monthly saving with a market-leading **5.00% AER fixed rate** for 12 months. By committing to save between £25 and £250 each month via standing order, you build a disciplined savings habit while earning one of the best rates available.

## Key Features

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Interest rate | **5.00% AER (fixed for 12 months)** |
| Monthly contribution | **£25 minimum — £250 maximum** |
| Payment method | **Standing order** from a ToggleBank current account |
| Term | **12 months** (from date of first contribution) |
| Withdrawals | Permitted (see conditions below) |
| Accounts per person | **1** Regular Saver per individual |
| FSCS protection | Yes — up to £85,000 |

## How It Works

1. **Open** your Regular Saver online, in the app, or in branch
2. **Set up a standing order** from your ToggleBank current account for £25–£250 per month
3. **Save consistently** — your standing order pays in on the same date each month
4. **Earn 5.00% AER** on the balance for the full 12-month term
5. **At maturity** — your savings plus interest are paid into your nominated account

## What Happens If You Miss a Month?

Consistent saving is key to earning the headline rate:

| Scenario | Impact on Rate |
|---|---|
| All 12 payments made | **5.00% AER** for full term |
| 1 missed payment | Rate drops to **Easy Access rate (currently 3.75% AER)** from the date of the missed payment onwards |
| 2+ missed payments | Rate remains at Easy Access rate for remainder of term |

- A "missed payment" means no standing order credit is received in a calendar month.
- If you resume payments after a miss, the higher rate is **not reinstated**.
- Setting a sensible, affordable monthly amount is recommended to avoid missing payments.

## Withdrawals

- You **can** make withdrawals during the 12-month term.
- However, any withdrawal will cause the interest rate to **drop to the Easy Access rate (currently 3.75% AER)** for the remainder of the term.
- Withdrawn funds cannot be replaced to restore the 5.00% rate.
- If you need penalty-free access to your savings, consider the ToggleBank Easy Access Savings Account instead.

## Eligibility

- Aged **18 or over**
- **UK resident** with a valid UK address
- Must hold a **ToggleBank current account** (Everyday, Premium, Student, or Private)
- **One Regular Saver** per person (you cannot hold multiple simultaneously)

## What Happens at Maturity

At the end of the 12-month term:

1. The account balance (capital + interest) is transferred to a ToggleBank account of your choice
2. You can open a **new Regular Saver** at the prevailing rate (subject to availability)
3. If no instructions are received, proceeds transfer to your ToggleBank Easy Access Savings Account

We will notify you at least **14 days before maturity** with your options.
