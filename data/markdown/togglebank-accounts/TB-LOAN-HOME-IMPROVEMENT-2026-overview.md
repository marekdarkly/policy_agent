ToggleBank is committed to supporting sustainable home improvements. A **0.5% APR discount** is available on both unsecured and secured home improvement loans when the funds are used for qualifying energy efficiency improvements:

| Ground source heat pump | £15,000 – £35,000 |
| Solar PV panels | £5,000 – £11,000 |

## Eligibility

**Unsecured loan:**
- Aged 18 or over
- UK resident with a permanent UK address
- Minimum annual income: £10,000
- Satisfactory credit history

**Secured loan (additional requirements):**
- You must be a homeowner with sufficient equity in your property
- Property must be in England, Wales, or Scotland
- Minimum property value: £75,000
- A professional valuation will be carried out at ToggleBank's cost
- Independent legal advice may be required

## Early Repayment

- **Unsecured loans:** No early repayment fees. You can repay in full or make overpayments at any time without penalty.
- **Secured loans:** An early repayment charge of up to 2 months' interest may apply if you settle the loan within the first 5 years. No early repayment charges apply after year 5.

## How to Apply

**Unsecured (up to £25,000):**
1. Apply online at togglebank.co.uk/home-improvement — decision in approximately 60 seconds
2. Funds paid same day for existing customers (if approved before 4pm), next working day for new customers
3. Also available by phone (0345 600 7000) or in-branch

**Secured (£25,001–£100,000):**
1. Call 0345 600 7000 or visit any ToggleBank branch to discuss your project with an adviser
2. We'll arrange a property valuation and guide you through the application
3. Typical processing time: 2–4 weeks from application to funds
