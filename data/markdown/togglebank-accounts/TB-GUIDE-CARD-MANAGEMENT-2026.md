## Overview

This guide covers everything you need to know about managing your ToggleBank debit and credit cards — from reporting a lost card to setting spending controls and raising transaction disputes.

## Reporting Lost or Stolen Cards

If your card is lost or stolen, act immediately:

| Method | Availability | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Mobile App** | 24/7 | Tap your card > **Freeze card** (instant) |
| **Phone** | 24/7 | Call **0345 678 9099** (UK) or **+44 20 7946 0958** (abroad) |
| **Online Banking** | 24/7 | Cards > Select card > **Report lost/stolen** |

Freezing your card in the app is instant and **reversible** — if you find the card, simply unfreeze it. Reporting a card as lost or stolen permanently cancels it and triggers a replacement.

## PIN Services

- **Forgotten PIN:** Request a PIN reminder letter via Online Banking, the Mobile App, or by phone. Delivered to your registered address within **3–5 working days**.
- **Change PIN:** Change your PIN at any **ToggleBank ATM** using your existing PIN.
- **PIN not received:** If a new card PIN has not arrived within 7 days, contact us to resend.

## Contactless Payments

- **Contactless limit:** £100 per transaction.
- **Cumulative limit:** After **5 consecutive contactless transactions** or a cumulative spend of **£300** (whichever comes first), you will be asked to enter your PIN.
- Contactless can be **disabled** via the Mobile App or Online Banking if preferred.

## Card Controls (In-App)

Manage your card security from the Mobile App:

| Control | Description |
|---|---|
| Disable contactless | Block all contactless (tap-and-go) transactions |
| Disable online payments | Block all card-not-present / e-commerce transactions |
| Disable ATM withdrawals | Block all cash machine withdrawals |
| Disable international payments | Block all transactions outside the UK |
| Set spending limits | Set a daily or monthly maximum spend amount |
| Freeze card | Temporarily block **all** transactions (reversible) |

Changes take effect **immediately** and can be reversed at any time.

## Card Replacement

| Replacement Type | Delivery Time | Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Standard replacement | 3–5 working days | Free |
| Express (next working day) | Next business day (order before 14:00) | £10 (debit) / £15 (credit) |
| Emergency card abroad | Up to 48 hours (via courier) | £25 |

Replacement cards are sent to your **registered address** only, for security reasons.

## Travelling Abroad

- ToggleBank uses **automatic fraud detection** — you **no longer need to notify the bank** before travelling.
- For added assurance, you can add a **travel note** in the Mobile App (Cards > Travel settings) to reduce the chance of legitimate transactions being flagged.
- **Non-sterling transaction fee:** 2.75% on Basic accounts, 0% on Premium and Private accounts.
- **ATM withdrawal abroad:** additional fee of £1.50 per withdrawal on Basic accounts (free on Premium/Private).

## Transaction Disputes

If you notice an unauthorised or incorrect transaction on your statement:

### Chargeback (Debit Cards & Credit Cards)

- Available for transactions where goods/services were **not received**, were **faulty**, or the merchant has **ceased trading**.
- Must be raised within **120 days** of the transaction date.
- Contact ToggleBank to initiate — we will submit a dispute to the card network (Visa/Mastercard).
- Typical resolution time: **30–45 days**.

### Section 75 Protection (Credit Cards Only)

- Applies to purchases between **£100 and £30,000** made on a ToggleBank credit card.
- The card issuer (ToggleBank) is **jointly and severally liable** with the retailer under the Consumer Credit Act 1974.
- Covers: misrepresentation, breach of contract, goods not as described, non-delivery.
- No time limit, but claims are stronger when raised promptly.

| Protection | Card Type | Amount Range | Time Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Chargeback | Debit & Credit | Any amount | 120 days |
| Section 75 | Credit only | £100–£30,000 | No statutory limit |
