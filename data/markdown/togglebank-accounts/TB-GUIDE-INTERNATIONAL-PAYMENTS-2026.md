## Overview

ToggleBank supports international money transfers to over **200 countries and territories** in **40+ currencies**. Whether you are paying an overseas supplier, supporting family abroad, or buying a holiday home, we offer competitive rates and transparent fees.

## Transfer Types

| Transfer Type | Processing Time | Fee (Basic/Premium) | Fee (Private Banking) |
|---|---|---|---|
| SWIFT (standard) | 3–5 business days | £25 / £15 | Free |
| SWIFT (express) | 1–2 business days | £40 / £30 | £10 |
| SEPA (Eurozone, EUR only) | Same day (if before 14:00) | £5 / Free | Free |
| SEPA Instant (Eurozone, EUR only) | Within 10 seconds | £10 / £5 | Free |

Fees shown are per transaction. Intermediary bank charges may apply for SWIFT transfers and are outside ToggleBank's control.

## Exchange Rates

- Rates are sourced from global interbank markets and updated **every 15 minutes** during business hours (08:00–17:00 GMT, Monday to Friday).
- Outside business hours, rates are fixed at the last available market rate.
- A **rate lock** is available: secure the displayed rate for **24 hours** while you gather the required information. Rate locks can be arranged online, in-app, or by phone.
- The ToggleBank exchange rate includes a margin above the interbank mid-rate:

| Account Type | FX Margin |
|---|---|
| Basic Current Account | 2.75% |
| Premium Current Account | 1.50% |
| Private Banking | 0.50% (negotiable for amounts over £50,000) |

## Required Information

To send an international payment, you will need:

- **Recipient's full name** (as it appears on their bank account)
- **Recipient's bank name and address**
- **IBAN** (International Bank Account Number) — required for European transfers
- **BIC / SWIFT code** — 8 or 11 characters identifying the recipient's bank
- **Purpose of payment** — a short description (e.g. "family support", "property purchase", "invoice payment")
- **Amount and currency**

## Compliance & Verification

All international payments are subject to **anti-money laundering (AML)** and **sanctions screening** checks in accordance with UK regulations.

- Payments to **sanctioned countries, entities, or individuals** will be declined.
- **Large value payments** (over **£10,000** equivalent) may require additional verification. You may be asked to provide:
  - Supporting documentation (e.g. invoice, property contract)
  - Source of funds declaration
  - Enhanced identity verification
- Processing of large value payments may take an **additional 1–2 business days** for compliance review.

## Tracking Your Payment

- **SWIFT GPI tracking** — Track SWIFT payments end-to-end using the unique reference number provided at confirmation.
- **In-app status** — View the current status (submitted, processing, completed) in your transaction history.
- You will receive a **push notification** and **email confirmation** once the payment has been sent.

## Cancellations & Amendments

- Payments can be **cancelled** before they are processed. SEPA payments submitted before the cut-off may not be cancellable once submitted.
- Amendments to recipient details after submission require a **recall request** (SWIFT recall fee: £25). Success is not guaranteed as it depends on the receiving bank.
