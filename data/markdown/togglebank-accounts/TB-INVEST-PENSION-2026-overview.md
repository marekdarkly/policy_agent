## Overview

The ToggleBank SIPP gives you control over your retirement savings with access to a wide range of investments, tax relief on contributions, and flexible options when you come to draw your pension. Whether you are building your pension, consolidating old pots, or approaching retirement, the ToggleBank SIPP is designed to be straightforward and cost-effective.

## Tax Relief on Contributions

Pension contributions receive tax relief at your marginal rate, making them one of the most tax-efficient ways to save.

| Tax Band | Your Contribution | Tax Relief Added | Total Invested |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic rate (20%) | £800 | £200 | £1,000 |
| Higher rate (40%) | £800 | £200 (auto) + £200 (via Self Assessment) | £1,000 effective cost = £600 |
| Additional rate (45%) | £800 | £200 (auto) + £250 (via Self Assessment) | £1,000 effective cost = £550 |

Basic rate relief (20%) is automatically claimed by ToggleBank and added to your SIPP. Higher and additional rate taxpayers claim the extra relief through their Self Assessment tax return.

## Annual Allowance

- **Standard annual allowance:** £60,000 per tax year (or 100% of your UK earnings, whichever is lower)
- **Carry forward:** if you have not used your full allowance in the previous 3 tax years, you may carry forward the unused amount
- **Tapered annual allowance:** if your threshold income exceeds £200,000 and adjusted income exceeds £260,000, your allowance may be reduced (minimum £10,000)
- **Money Purchase Annual Allowance (MPAA):** if you have flexibly accessed pension benefits, your allowance for money purchase contributions is reduced to £10,000

## Accessing Your Pension

You can access your SIPP from **age 55** (rising to age 57 from 6 April 2028).

| Option | Details |
|---|---|
| **Tax-free lump sum** | Withdraw up to 25% of your pension pot tax-free (known as Pension Commencement Lump Sum) |
| **Flexi-access drawdown** | Keep your pension invested and draw income as needed; income is taxed as earnings |
| **Uncrystallised funds pension lump sum (UFPLS)** | Take lump sums directly — 25% of each withdrawal is tax-free, 75% taxed as income |
| **Annuity purchase** | Use some or all of your pot to buy a guaranteed income for life from an annuity provider |

You can combine options — for example, take your 25% tax-free lump sum and move the rest into drawdown.

## Employer Contributions

Your employer can contribute directly to your ToggleBank SIPP:

- Employer contributions do not count against your personal tax relief
- They do count towards the annual allowance
- Employer contributions are an allowable business expense and not subject to National Insurance
- Set up via the employer portal at togglebank.co.uk/employer-pensions

## Eligibility

- UK residents and relevant UK individuals aged 18 or over
- Non-earners can contribute up to £3,600 gross per year
- No maximum fund size

## How to Open

1. Apply online at togglebank.co.uk/sipp or via the ToggleBank app
2. Provide identification (passport or UK driving licence) and National Insurance number
3. Choose your investment approach (DIY or ready-made portfolio)
4. Set up contributions (lump sum, regular monthly, or both)
5. Initiate any pension transfers
6. Your SIPP is ready to invest within 2 business days

## Important Information

- A pension is a long-term investment not normally accessible until age 55 (57 from 2028)
- The value of your pension can go down as well as up, and you may get back less than has been paid in
- Tax treatment depends on individual circumstances and may change
- Taking money from your pension may affect your entitlement to means-tested benefits
- ToggleBank Investment Services Ltd is authorised and regulated by the FCA (FRN 567890)
- The Financial Services Compensation Scheme covers eligible pension investments up to £85,000
