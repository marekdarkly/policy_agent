# ToggleBank Business Credit Card

## Product Overview

The ToggleBank Business Credit Card is designed for UK businesses — **sole traders, partnerships, and limited companies** — to streamline expenses and earn cashback on business spending. With up to 56 days interest-free on purchases, integrated expense management tools, and free employee cards, it simplifies day-to-day business finances.

**Product Code:** TB-CREDIT-BUSINESS-2026
**Card Network:** Visa Business
**Issued by:** ToggleBank plc (authorised by the PRA, regulated by the FCA and PRA)

## Cashback on Business Spending

- **1.5% cashback** on all business purchases — no category restrictions
- Cashback is calculated monthly and credited annually to your business statement
- No cap on cashback earnings
- Cashback can be redeemed as a statement credit or paid to a ToggleBank business current account
- Minimum redemption: £10

## Eligibility Requirements

- Available to **sole traders, partnerships, and limited companies** registered in the UK
- Minimum annual business turnover: **£25,000**
- Business must have been trading for at least **12 months**
- The primary cardholder must be a director, partner, or sole trader aged 18 or over
- Subject to business credit check and personal guarantee from the primary cardholder

---

## Credit Limit

- Credit limits from **£1,000 to £50,000** based on business turnover and creditworthiness
- Credit limit reviews available every 6 months
- Temporary credit limit increases can be requested for large planned purchases

---

## Minimum Payments

- The greater of **2.5% of the outstanding balance** or **£25**
- Full balance can be repaid interest-free if paid by the statement due date (up to 56 days)
- Direct Debit options: minimum payment, fixed amount, or full balance
