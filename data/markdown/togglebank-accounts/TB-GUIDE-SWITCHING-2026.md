## Overview

Switching your current account to ToggleBank is simple, safe, and guaranteed. The **Current Account Switch Service (CASS)** moves everything for you — including Direct Debits, standing orders, and your salary — in just **7 working days**.

## How the Switch Works

1. **Choose your ToggleBank account** — Open a new current account online, in-app, in-branch, or by phone.
2. **Pick your switch date** — Select a date that suits you (minimum 7 working days from request).
3. **We do the rest** — ToggleBank contacts your old bank and arranges the transfer of:
   - All **Direct Debits** and **standing orders**
   - **Salary** and **benefit payments** (your employer/payer is notified automatically)
   - **Remaining balance** from your old account
4. **Your old account is closed** (unless you choose a partial switch).

## What Gets Transferred

| Item | Full Switch | Partial Switch |
|---|---|---|
| Closing balance | ✓ Transferred | ✗ Not transferred |
| Direct Debits | ✓ Moved to ToggleBank | ✓ Selected items only |
| Standing orders | ✓ Moved to ToggleBank | ✓ Selected items only |
| Salary / benefit payments | ✓ Automatically redirected | ✗ You must notify payers |
| Old account | Closed | Stays open |
| Payment redirect | ✓ 3 years | ✓ 3 years |

## Payment Redirect Service

Any payments accidentally sent to your old account details will be **automatically redirected** to your new ToggleBank account for **3 years** after the switch date. This applies to both incoming payments and Direct Debits.

## The Switch Guarantee

The Current Account Switch Service comes with a guarantee:

- The switch will be completed on your **chosen date**.
- If anything goes wrong during the switch, ToggleBank will **refund any interest or charges** you incur as a result of the error.
- You will **not be left without access** to your money during the switch.

The guarantee is backed by **Bacs**, the organisation that runs the UK payment system.

## What You Need

- **Existing bank account details** — sort code and account number
- **Valid photo ID** — passport, UK driving licence, or biometric residence permit
- **Proof of address** (if not already a ToggleBank customer) — utility bill, council tax bill, or HMRC letter dated within the last 3 months

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I switch a joint account?**  
Yes. Both account holders must agree to the switch and provide valid ID.

**Can I switch my business account?**  
The CASS service is available for small business accounts and sole trader accounts. Contact our Business Banking team for larger business accounts.

**What happens to my overdraft?**  
Your ToggleBank overdraft facility (if agreed) will be available from your switch date. Any overdraft with your old bank will be settled as part of the switch.

**Can I cancel the switch?**  
Yes, you can cancel at any time before the switch date by contacting us.
