# ToggleBank Income Protection Insurance

## Product Overview

ToggleBank Income Protection Insurance replaces a portion of your income if you are unable to work due to **illness or injury**. With cover that pays up to **60% of your gross income**, flexible deferred periods, and the option to choose guaranteed premiums, it provides a financial safety net for the duration of your working life.

| Feature | Details |

## Deferred Period Options

The deferred period is the waiting time between the start of your incapacity and when benefit payments begin. Choosing a longer deferred period reduces your monthly premium.

| Deferred Period | Best For | Impact on Premium |
|---|---|---|
| **4 weeks** | Those with limited sick pay or savings | Highest premium |
| **8 weeks** | Employees with 1–2 months' employer sick pay | Moderate premium |
| **13 weeks** | Employees with 3 months' employer sick pay or SSP | Lower premium |
| **26 weeks** | Those with 6 months' sick pay or significant savings | Lowest premium |

- Select a deferred period that aligns with your employer's sick pay provision
- Benefit payments begin after the deferred period and continue for as long as you remain unable to work (subject to policy terms)

---

## Occupation Definitions

You can choose how "unable to work" is defined under your policy:

| Definition | Description | Premium Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Own Occupation** | Pays out if you cannot perform **your specific job** | Higher premium — strongest cover |
| **Any Occupation** | Pays out only if you cannot perform **any job suited to your education, training, or experience** | Lower premium — more restrictive |

- **Own occupation** is recommended where available, as it provides the broadest protection
- Availability of own occupation cover depends on your specific role

## Premium Options

| Option | Details |
|---|---|
| **Guaranteed Premiums** | Fixed for the life of the policy — never increase regardless of age, health changes, or claims |
| **Reviewable Premiums** | Lower initial cost, but premiums may be reviewed (and increased) by the insurer, typically every 5 years |
| **Age-Banded Premiums** | Premiums increase at set age milestones (e.g. every 5 years) — a middle ground between guaranteed and reviewable |

## What's Covered

- **Illness**: any medical condition that prevents you from working, including mental health conditions (e.g. depression, anxiety, stress)
- **Injury**: physical injuries sustained at work, at home, or during leisure activities
- **Recurring conditions**: if you return to work and the same condition forces you off again, the deferred period is waived for recurrences within 12 months
- **Rehabilitation support**: phased return-to-work support with partial benefit payments during transition back to full-time work

---

## What's Not Covered

- Unemployment due to redundancy (this is not an income protection event)
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Injuries sustained while committing a criminal act
- Claims arising from alcohol or drug misuse (some exceptions may apply)
- Incapacity arising within the first 30 days of the policy from a pre-existing condition not disclosed at application

---

## Eligibility Requirements

- Must be a **UK resident** aged **18–59**
- Must be in **paid employment** (employed or self-employed)
- Minimum annual income: **£15,000**
- Maximum benefit: the lower of £10,000/month or 60% of gross income
- Subject to medical underwriting (online health questionnaire)
