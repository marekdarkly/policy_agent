## Monthly Account Fee

The Everyday Current Account has **no monthly fee**. There is no minimum balance or funding requirement.

## Overdraft Charges

| Overdraft Type | Rate / Charge |
|---|---|
| Arranged overdraft | **39.9% EAR (variable)** |
| Unarranged overdraft buffer | **£25** (no charges within buffer) |
| Unarranged overdraft (beyond buffer) | 39.9% EAR (variable) |
| Refused payment fee | £0 (we do not charge for refused payments) |

- Interest on arranged overdrafts is calculated daily on the end-of-day overdrawn balance and debited monthly.
- The **£25 unarranged buffer** means that if you go overdrawn without an arranged facility, or exceed your arranged limit, no interest or fees are charged on the first £25 of unarranged borrowing.
- We will send you an alert by SMS, email, or push notification when your balance approaches zero and when you enter overdraft.
- You have until 11:59pm to bring your balance back within your arranged limit to avoid charges for that day.

## Representative Example

If you had an arranged overdraft of £1,200 at 39.9% EAR (variable) and used £500 for 30 days, the approximate interest charge would be £16.30.
