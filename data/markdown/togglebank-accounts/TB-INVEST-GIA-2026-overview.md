# ToggleBank General Investment Account (GIA)

**Product Code:** TB-INVEST-GIA-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Regulated by:** Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)

## Overview

The ToggleBank General Investment Account is a flexible, unwrapped investment account for clients who have used their ISA allowance or want unrestricted access to a wide range of investments. There is no annual contribution limit and no restrictions on withdrawals.

## Charges

| Fee Type | Amount |
|---|---|
| Platform fee | 0.25% per year on total holdings (capped at £45 per quarter) |
| Fund dealing | **Free** |
| Share dealing — standard | £6 per trade |
| Share dealing — first month | **Free** (unlimited trades) |
| Regular investing (monthly) | **No dealing fee** |
| Dividend reinvestment | **Free** |
| Paper contract notes (optional) | £1 per note |
| Account closure / transfer out | Free |

The platform fee is calculated daily and collected quarterly. The £45/quarter cap means you pay no more than £180/year regardless of portfolio size.

## Regular Investing

- Invest from **£25 per month** into any fund or ETF
- No dealing fees on regular investments
- Set up, amend, or cancel at any time via online account or app
- Payments collected by Direct Debit on the 8th of each month
- Investments purchased on the next dealing day after collection

## Tax Information

The GIA is not a tax wrapper. You may be liable for:

- **Capital Gains Tax (CGT)** — gains above the annual exempt amount of **£3,000** (2025/26 tax year) are taxable at 18% (basic rate) or 24% (higher/additional rate) for most assets
- **Dividend Tax** — dividends above the **£500** annual allowance (2025/26) are taxed at 8.75% (basic), 33.75% (higher), or 39.35% (additional)
- **Income Tax** — bond interest and property income distributions may be taxable

ToggleBank provides an annual consolidated tax certificate to help with your Self Assessment return.

## Eligibility

- UK resident individuals aged 18 or over
- Joint accounts available for two holders
- No maximum investment limit
- No minimum lump sum (except £500 for ready-made portfolios)

## How to Open

1. Apply online at togglebank.co.uk/investing or via the ToggleBank app
2. Complete a suitability questionnaire to determine your risk profile
3. Choose DIY or a ready-made portfolio
4. Fund the account via bank transfer, debit card, or regular Direct Debit
5. Account opens and is ready to trade within 1 business day
