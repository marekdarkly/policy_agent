# ToggleBank Premium Current Account — Fees and Charges

## Monthly Account Fee

| Item | Premium Current Account | Everyday Current Account |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly fee | £15 | Free |

The monthly fee is debited on the same calendar date each month. If you close your account mid-month, a pro-rated refund will be applied.

## Overdraft Charges

| Overdraft Type | Rate / Charge |
|---|---|
| Arranged overdraft | **15.9% EAR (variable)** |
| Unarranged overdraft (daily fee) | £5 per day |
| Unarranged overdraft (monthly cap) | £60 per calendar month |
| Unarranged overdraft buffer | £0 (charges apply from first penny) |
| Refused payment fee | £0 (we do not charge for refused payments) |

- Interest on arranged overdrafts is calculated daily on the overdrawn balance and charged monthly.
- If you go overdrawn without an arranged overdraft or exceed your limit, the unarranged daily fee applies for each day you remain in unarranged overdraft.
- We will send you alerts via SMS, email, or push notification when your balance falls below £100 and when you enter overdraft.

## International Payments and Transfers

| Service | Charge |
|---|---|
| Outbound international transfers (SWIFT) | **Free** up to £10,000 per calendar month |
| Outbound international transfers (over £10,000/month) | £15 per transfer |
| Inbound international transfers | Free |
| Currency conversion markup | **0%** on Mastercard/Visa exchange rate |
| International ATM withdrawals | **Free** (worldwide) |

Premium Current Account holders benefit from fee-free international transfers up to a generous monthly threshold and zero currency conversion markup, making this account ideal for frequent travellers and those with overseas commitments.
