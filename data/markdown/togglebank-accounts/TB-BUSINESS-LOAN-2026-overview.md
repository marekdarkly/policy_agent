# ToggleBank Business Loans

**Product Code:** TB-BUSINESS-LOAN-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Regulated by:** Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA)

## Overview

ToggleBank Business Loans provide flexible funding for UK businesses, whether you are investing in growth, purchasing equipment, managing cash flow, or launching a new venture. Loans are available from £1,000 to £500,000 with repayment terms from 1 to 25 years.

## Loan Options at a Glance

| Feature | Unsecured Loan | Secured Loan | Start-Up Loan |
|---|---|---|---|
| Loan amount | £1,000–£50,000 | £10,000–£500,000 | £500–£25,000 |
| Repayment term | 1–7 years | 1–25 years | 1–5 years |
| Interest rate | From 7.5% APR | From 5.9% APR | 6.0% APR (fixed) |
| Rate type | Fixed or variable | Fixed or variable | Fixed only |
| Security required | None | Property or business assets | None |
| Early repayment fees | None (under £25,000) | See schedule below | None |
| Decision time | Within 48 hours | 5–10 business days | Within 48 hours |

All rates are representative and subject to credit assessment. The rate offered depends on the loan amount, term, business financials, and credit history.

## Unsecured Business Loans

- No security or guarantees required for loans up to £50,000
- Fixed monthly repayments for budgeting certainty (fixed rate option)
- Variable rate option linked to Bank of England base rate + margin
- No early repayment charges on loans under £25,000
- Early repayment on loans of £25,000–£50,000: up to 2 months' interest
- Decision within 48 hours for applications with complete documentation

## Secured Business Loans

Secured loans are available for larger borrowing requirements and typically attract lower interest rates.

**Acceptable security includes:**

- Commercial property
- Residential property (with independent legal advice)
- Plant, machinery, and equipment
- Stock or inventory (subject to valuation)
- Debenture over business assets

| Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio | Maximum |
|---|---|
| Commercial property | 70% |
| Residential property | 75% |
| Plant and equipment | 50% |

**Early repayment charges (secured loans):**

| Remaining Term | Charge |
|---|---|
| More than 5 years | 3% of outstanding balance |
| 2–5 years | 2% of outstanding balance |
| Under 2 years | 1% of outstanding balance |

## Start-Up Loans

ToggleBank participates in the UK Government's Start Up Loans scheme, administered by the British Business Bank.

- Available to businesses trading for fewer than 2 years (or pre-revenue)
- Fixed interest rate of 6.0% APR
- No arrangement fees or early repayment charges
- Free business mentoring for 12 months after drawdown
- Applicants must submit a viable business plan
- Personal liability applies — start-up loans are personal loans for business use

## Eligibility

- UK-registered businesses (sole traders, partnerships, LLPs, limited companies)
- Minimum trading history of 6 months for unsecured loans (waived for start-up loans)
- Satisfactory credit history for all directors/partners
- Business must demonstrate ability to service the debt from existing or projected cash flow

## How to Apply

1. Use the loan calculator at togglebank.co.uk/business-loans to estimate repayments
2. Apply online, by phone (0345 678 9012), or in a ToggleBank business centre
3. Upload or provide required documentation
4. Receive a decision — within 48 hours for unsecured and start-up loans
5. On approval, funds are transferred to your ToggleBank Business Current Account
