## Overview

Although digital payments have grown significantly, cheques remain a valid payment method in the UK. ToggleBank supports cheque deposits through multiple channels and continues to issue cheque books for eligible accounts.

## Depositing a Cheque

### Mobile Cheque Imaging (Deposit via App)

Deposit cheques from your phone using the ToggleBank Mobile App:

1. Open the app and tap **Deposit cheque**.
2. Select the account you wish to deposit into.
3. Photograph the **front** and **back** of the cheque (ensure good lighting, flat surface, dark background).
4. Enter the **amount** and confirm.

| Limit | Amount |
|---|---|
| Per cheque | Up to £1,000 |
| Daily deposit limit | £2,500 |
| Monthly deposit limit | £10,000 |

Higher limits may apply for Premium and Private Banking customers. If your cheque exceeds the mobile limit, please deposit it in-branch or via ATM.

### In-Branch Cheque Deposits

- Hand the cheque to a cashier or use the **self-service paying-in machine** (available at larger branches).
- You will receive a receipt with the expected clearing date.
- No deposit limits for in-branch deposits.

### ATM Cheque Deposits

- Insert your ToggleBank debit card and select **Deposit cheque**.
- Place the cheque in the deposit slot (do not use an envelope).
- The ATM will scan and confirm the cheque details on screen.
- Available at ToggleBank ATMs marked with the **cheque deposit** symbol.

## Cheque Clearing Times

Under the UK **Image Clearing System (ICS)**, cheque clearing is faster than ever:

| Stage | Timescale |
|---|---|
| Funds available to use | By **23:59 on the next working day** after deposit |
| Certainty of funds (cheque cannot normally be returned) | **6 working days** after deposit |

"Working day" means Monday to Friday, excluding bank holidays. Cheques deposited after **15:30** (in-branch) or **20:00** (via app) are treated as deposited on the next working day.

**Important:** In rare cases, a cheque can be returned after the 6-day certainty period if fraud is suspected.

## Writing a Cheque

When writing a cheque from your ToggleBank cheque book:

- Write the **date** (day/month/year).
- Write the **payee name** clearly on the "Pay" line.
- Write the **amount in words** on the next line, followed by "only" (e.g. "One hundred and fifty pounds only").
- Write the **amount in figures** in the box (e.g. £150.00).
- **Sign** the cheque with your usual signature.
- Do not leave gaps before or after the payee name or amount — draw a line to fill any blank space.

## Ordering Cheque Books

- Cheque books are available **free of charge** for most current accounts.
- Order via **Online Banking**, the **Mobile App**, or by calling **0345 678 9012**.
- Delivered to your registered address within **5–7 working days**.
- Each cheque book contains **25 cheques**.
- Business accounts may order larger cheque books (50 or 100 cheques) — additional charges may apply.

## Banker's Drafts

A banker's draft is a cheque guaranteed by ToggleBank, commonly used for high-value transactions such as property purchases or vehicle sales.

| Detail | Information |
|---|---|
| Fee | £15 per draft |
| Availability | In-branch only (same-day if ordered before 14:00) |
| Maximum amount | No limit (subject to available funds) |
| Validity | 6 months from date of issue |
| Cancellation | Can be cancelled if unused — original draft must be returned; refund within 5 working days |

## Returned Cheques (Bounced Cheques)

A cheque may be returned unpaid ("bounced") if:

- The payer has **insufficient funds**.
- The cheque is **out of date** (more than 6 months old).
- The cheque has been **altered** or the signature does not match.
- A **stop** has been placed on the cheque by the payer.

If a cheque you deposited is returned, the funds will be debited from your account and you will be notified by letter and in-app notification. ToggleBank does **not** charge a fee for returned cheques deposited by you.

## Cheque Validity

- A cheque is valid for **6 months** from the date written on it.
- After 6 months, banks are not obliged to honour it (though they may at their discretion).
- **Post-dated cheques** (dated in the future) should not be presented before the date shown — ToggleBank will hold them until that date.

## Stopping a Cheque

If you need to cancel a cheque you have written:

- Contact ToggleBank by phone (**0345 678 9012**) or via Online Banking/Mobile App.
- Provide the **cheque number**, **amount**, **payee name**, and **date**.
- Fee: **£5** per stopped cheque.
- A stop can be placed at any time before the cheque is cleared.
