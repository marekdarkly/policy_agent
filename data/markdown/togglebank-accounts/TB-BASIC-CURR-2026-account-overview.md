# ToggleBank Everyday Current Account

**Product Code:** TB-BASIC-CURR-2026
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026
**Account Type:** Everyday Current Account

---

## Overview

The ToggleBank Everyday Current Account is our free, no-frills current account that gives you everything you need for day-to-day banking. There is no monthly fee, no minimum income requirement, and no minimum balance — just straightforward banking that works.

## Key Features

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Monthly fee | **Free** |
| Debit card | Visa Debit (contactless-enabled) |
| UK ATM withdrawals | Free at all UK ATMs displaying the LINK logo |
| Mobile banking | Full-featured app with instant spending notifications |
| Online banking | 24/7 access via browser |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay | Fully supported |
| Contactless limit | £100 per transaction |
| Cheque book | Available on request |

## Everyday Banking Made Simple

- **Instant notifications** — receive real-time alerts for every transaction, direct debit, and standing order
- **Spending insights** — automatic categorisation of your spending with monthly summaries
- **Savings pots** — set money aside within your account for specific goals
- **Scheduled payments** — set up standing orders and manage direct debits easily from the app
- **Freeze your card** — instantly freeze and unfreeze your debit card from the mobile app if you misplace it
- **Cheque imaging** — deposit cheques by taking a photo in the app (up to £1,000 per day)

## Arranged Overdraft

An arranged overdraft is available on the Everyday Current Account, subject to status and affordability checks.

| Overdraft Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Arranged overdraft rate | 39.9% EAR (variable) |
| Unarranged overdraft buffer | £25 interest-free buffer |
| Overdraft limit range | £100 – £5,000 |
| Overdraft alerts | Automatic SMS/push notification |

You can apply for an overdraft when you open the account or at any time afterwards via the app or online banking. We assess all applications based on your individual circumstances.

## Eligibility

- Aged **18 or over**
- UK resident with a valid UK address
- Must pass identity verification checks
- **No minimum income** requirement
- **No credit check** for basic account opening (credit check required for overdraft)

## How to Apply

1. **Online** — Apply at togglebank.co.uk in under 10 minutes with video ID verification
2. **Mobile app** — Download the ToggleBank app and apply with photo ID
3. **In branch** — Visit any ToggleBank branch with photo ID and proof of address
4. **Current Account Switch Service** — Switch from your existing bank in 7 working days with CASS
