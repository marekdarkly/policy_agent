## Overview

ToggleBank invests heavily in protecting your money and personal information. This guide explains our security measures and how you can protect yourself from fraud.

## Our Security Measures

| Measure | Description |
|---|---|
| Two-factor authentication | Every login and sensitive action requires a second verification step (app approval or SMS code) |
| Biometric login | Fingerprint and facial recognition on the Mobile App |
| Real-time fraud monitoring | 24/7 automated transaction monitoring using AI and behavioural analytics |
| Instant transaction alerts | Push notifications for every transaction on your account |
| In-app card freeze | Freeze your card instantly if something looks wrong |
| Confirmation of Payee | Real-time name-checking when you set up a new payee — warns you if the name doesn't match |
| Strong Customer Authentication (SCA) | PSD2-compliant authentication for online payments and account access |
| End-to-end encryption | All data transmitted using TLS 1.3; data at rest encrypted with AES-256 |

## Common Scam Types

Be vigilant against these common fraud tactics:

- **Authorised Push Payment (APP) fraud** — You are tricked into voluntarily sending money to a fraudster's account (e.g. fake invoice, impersonation of a solicitor).
- **Phishing** — Fraudulent emails designed to look like they are from ToggleBank, asking you to click a link and enter your login details.
- **Vishing** — Phone calls from someone claiming to be from ToggleBank or the police, pressuring you to share account details or transfer money.
- **Smishing** — Fraudulent SMS messages containing links to fake websites.
- **Romance scams** — Building a fake relationship online to manipulate you into sending money.
- **Investment scams** — Promises of unrealistic returns on investments in cryptocurrency, forex, or other assets.
- **Impersonation scams** — Fraudsters posing as HMRC, the police, Amazon, Microsoft, or other trusted organisations.

## ToggleBank Will Never

It is critical to remember that ToggleBank will **never**:

- Ask you for your **full PIN** or **Online Banking password** by phone, email, or text.
- Ask you to **transfer money to a "safe account"** — safe accounts do not exist.
- Send you an **SMS with a link** asking you to enter your login credentials.
- Ask you to **download remote access software** (e.g. TeamViewer, AnyDesk).
- Send someone to your home to **collect your card, PIN, or cash**.
- Rush you into making a financial decision. **A genuine bank will never pressure you.**

## What to Do If You Suspect Fraud

1. **Stop** — Do not make any further payments or share any information.
2. **Freeze** — Freeze your card immediately via the Mobile App.
3. **Report** — Contact ToggleBank as quickly as possible:

| Reporting Channel | Details |
|---|---|
| In-app | Tap **Help > Report fraud** (24/7) |
| Phone | **0345 678 9099** (24/7, select option 1 for fraud) |
| Online Banking | Secure message to the Fraud Team |
| Action Fraud | [actionfraud.police.uk](https://actionfraud.police.uk) or call **0300 123 2040** |

4. **Preserve evidence** — Keep copies of emails, texts, screenshots, and any communication with the fraudster.

## Refund Policy for Unauthorised Transactions

- If your account is used for a transaction you **did not authorise**, ToggleBank will refund the full amount — typically **by the end of the next business day** after you report it.
- You will **not** be held liable for unauthorised transactions unless ToggleBank can demonstrate that you acted with **gross negligence** (e.g. sharing your PIN or password willingly).
- For **Authorised Push Payment (APP) fraud**, ToggleBank follows the Contingent Reimbursement Model (CRM) Code. Reimbursement depends on whether reasonable steps were taken by both you and the bank. Most victims who have acted appropriately will receive a full refund.

## Keeping Yourself Safe

- **Use unique passwords** — Never reuse your Online Banking password elsewhere.
- **Enable biometric login** — Fingerprint or face recognition adds a layer of protection.
- **Keep software updated** — Ensure your phone operating system and the ToggleBank app are up to date.
- **Be sceptical** — If something feels wrong, hang up, close the message, and contact us directly on a number you trust.
- **Check payee details** — Always verify payment details independently before transferring money, especially for large sums.
- **Register for transaction alerts** — Ensure push notifications are enabled for all transactions.
