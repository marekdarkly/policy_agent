## Product Summary

A ToggleBank Personal Loan gives you a fixed lump sum with fixed monthly repayments, so you always know exactly what you'll pay and when the loan will be cleared. Borrow from £1,000 to £25,000 over 1 to 7 years, with no early repayment fees if you want to pay it off sooner.

| Repayment type | Fixed monthly repayments |
| Early repayment fees | None |

## What Can I Use a Personal Loan For?

You can use a ToggleBank Personal Loan for almost any personal purpose:

- **Car purchase** — buy a new or used vehicle
- **Home improvements** — kitchen, bathroom, extension, or decorating
- **Debt consolidation** — combine existing debts into one manageable payment (consider whether this is right for you — you may pay more overall if you extend the repayment term)
- **Wedding** — cover venue, catering, and everything else
- **Holiday** — fund a trip or special occasion
- **Large purchases** — furniture, appliances, or technology

Personal Loans are not available for business purposes, property purchase, or speculative investment. For business borrowing, see our Business Loan products.

## Eligibility

- Aged 18 or over (and the loan term must end before your 75th birthday)
- UK resident with a permanent UK address
- Minimum annual income of £10,000
- Satisfactory credit history — a credit check will be performed
- Must hold (or be willing to open) a UK bank account for repayments
- Existing ToggleBank current account holders may qualify for preferential rates

## How to Apply

1. **Check your rate** — Use our online eligibility checker at togglebank.co.uk/loans. This uses a soft credit search that won't affect your credit score and shows you the rate you're likely to receive.
2. **Apply** — Complete a full application online (takes approximately 10 minutes), call us on 0345 600 7000, or visit any ToggleBank branch.
3. **Get a decision** — Online applications receive a decision in as fast as **60 seconds**.
4. **Receive your funds** — Existing ToggleBank current account holders can receive funds **the same day**. New customers typically receive funds on the **next working day**.

## Managing Your Loan

- **View your loan** in Online Banking or the ToggleBank app at any time
- **Make overpayments** or repay in full at any time with **no early repayment fees**
- **Set up a Direct Debit** to ensure you never miss a payment
- **Payment holiday** — you may be eligible to defer one monthly payment per year after the first 6 months (interest continues to accrue; this will increase the total amount repayable)
- **Missed payments** — if you're struggling, contact us as early as possible. We can discuss options including temporary reduced payments

## Things to Consider

- Missing payments can harm your credit score and may result in additional charges
- A personal loan is a commitment — make sure the monthly repayments are affordable within your budget
- If consolidating existing debts, ensure you close the credit facilities you're repaying to avoid re-borrowing
- Compare the total cost of borrowing, not just the monthly payment, when choosing a loan term
