# ToggleBank Data Protection & Privacy Policy

**Document Reference:** TB-REF-DATA-PROTECTION-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Classification:** Customer-Facing

---

## Overview

ToggleBank is committed to protecting your personal data. This document explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information in compliance with the **UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR)** and the **Data Protection Act 2018**.

ToggleBank is the **data controller** for the personal data we process. Our Data Protection Officer can be contacted at **dpo@togglebank.co.uk**.

## What Data We Collect

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Identity data** | Full name, date of birth, gender, nationality, passport/driving licence details |
| **Contact data** | Home address, email address, telephone numbers |
| **Financial data** | Income, employment details, credit history, account balances, transaction history |
| **Technical data** | IP address, browser type, device information, login timestamps |
| **Behavioural data** | Spending patterns, product usage, app interaction data |
| **Communications data** | Secure messages, call recordings (for training and quality assurance), complaint correspondence |

## Why We Use Your Data

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

- **Contractual necessity** — To operate your accounts, process transactions, and provide the services you have requested.
- **Legal obligation** — To comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations, tax reporting (e.g. FATCA, CRS), and regulatory requirements from the FCA and PRA.
- **Legitimate interests** — To detect and prevent fraud, manage risk, improve our products and services, and conduct internal analytics.
- **Consent** — For marketing communications and sharing data with third parties where your explicit consent is required.

## Data Sharing

ToggleBank shares personal data with the following categories of third party, where required or permitted by law:

### Credit Reference Agencies

We share account and payment data with the three main UK credit reference agencies:

| Agency | Contact |
|---|---|
| **Experian** | [experian.co.uk](https://experian.co.uk) |
| **Equifax** | [equifax.co.uk](https://equifax.co.uk) |
| **TransUnion** | [transunion.co.uk](https://transunion.co.uk) |

Data shared includes: account opening, account balances (monthly), payment history, defaults, and credit searches. This data is used to assess creditworthiness and is visible to other lenders.

### Fraud Prevention Agencies

| Agency | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **CIFAS** | National fraud prevention database; records are shared to detect and prevent fraud |
| **National Hunter** | Cross-industry application fraud checking |

If fraud is identified, a marker may be placed on your record at CIFAS. This could affect your ability to obtain credit, insurance, or other financial services. You will be informed if a marker is placed.

### Other Third Parties

- **HMRC** — Tax reporting obligations (interest earned, FATCA, Common Reporting Standard)
- **Regulators** — FCA, PRA, Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), Financial Ombudsman Service
- **Law enforcement** — When legally compelled (e.g. court orders, suspicious activity reports to the National Crime Agency)
- **Service providers** — IT infrastructure, printing, postage, customer support partners (all bound by data processing agreements)

## Marketing Preferences

- You can manage your marketing preferences at any time via:
  - **Online Banking:** Settings > Marketing preferences
  - **Mobile App:** Settings > Communications
  - **Phone:** 0345 678 9012

## Your Data Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

| Right | Description |
|---|---|
| **Access** | Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you (Subject Access Request) |
| **Rectification** | Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data |
| **Erasure** | Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements) |
| **Restriction** | Request that we limit how we use your data |
| **Portability** | Receive your data in a machine-readable format |
| **Objection** | Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for marketing purposes |
| **Automated decision-making** | Request human review of automated decisions that significantly affect you |

## Subject Access Request (SAR)

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data ToggleBank holds about you:

- **Cost:** Free of charge.
- **How to request:** Email **sar@togglebank.co.uk**, call **0345 678 9012**, write to us, or submit via Online Banking (Settings > Data requests).
- **Response time:** Within **30 calendar days** of receiving your request and verifying your identity.
- **Extensions:** If the request is complex or voluminous, we may extend the deadline by up to 2 additional months — we will notify you within the first 30 days if this is necessary.
- **Format:** Provided electronically (secure download link) unless you request a paper copy.

## Right to Erasure

You can request that we delete your personal data. However, we may be unable to comply fully in certain circumstances:

- **Regulatory requirements** — We must retain transaction records for at least **7 years** under anti-money laundering regulations.
- **Legal proceedings** — Data relevant to ongoing or anticipated legal action.
- **Legitimate interests** — Fraud prevention records (CIFAS markers may be retained for up to 6 years).

We will explain clearly which data we can and cannot delete, and why.

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account and transaction records | 7 years after account closure |
| Credit search records | 2 years (at credit reference agencies: 12 months hard search, 2 years soft search) |
| Call recordings | 7 years |
| Complaint records | 5 years after resolution |
| Marketing consent records | Duration of relationship + 2 years |
| CCTV (in-branch) | 90 days |
