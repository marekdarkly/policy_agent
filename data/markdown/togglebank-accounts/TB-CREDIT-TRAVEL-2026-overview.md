| Cover | Limit |

| Emergency Medical Expenses | Up to **£10,000,000** |
| Trip Cancellation | Up to **£5,000** |
| Trip Curtailment | Up to **£5,000** |
| Baggage Loss or Damage | Up to **£2,500** |

| Travel Delay (after 8 hours) | Up to **£200** |
