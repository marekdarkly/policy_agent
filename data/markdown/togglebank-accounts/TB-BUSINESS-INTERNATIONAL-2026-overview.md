## Overview

ToggleBank International Business Services supports UK businesses that trade, pay suppliers, or receive income across borders. From multi-currency accounts and foreign exchange to trade finance and country risk advisory, ToggleBank provides end-to-end international banking for importers, exporters, and globally operating companies.

## Foreign Exchange Services

ToggleBank offers competitive FX solutions for businesses managing currency exposure.

**Spot Contracts:**
- Convert currencies at the prevailing market rate
- Competitive margins from 0.3% on amounts over £50,000
- Execute online or via your dedicated dealer

**Forward Contracts:**
- Lock in an exchange rate for a future date (up to 24 months ahead)
- Protect profit margins against currency fluctuations
- Minimum contract value: £10,000 equivalent
- Initial margin deposit may be required (typically 5–10%)

**Market Orders:**
- Set a target exchange rate; ToggleBank executes automatically when the rate is reached
- Available 24/5 across all supported currency pairs

## Trade Finance

ToggleBank provides a comprehensive suite of trade finance instruments for importers and exporters.

| Instrument | Purpose | Typical Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Documentary Letter of Credit** | Payment assurance for the exporter | Large or first-time international orders |
| **Standby Letter of Credit** | Performance or payment guarantee | Contract bids, rental guarantees |
| **Documentary Collection** | Bank-mediated document exchange | Trusted trading relationships |
| **Bank Guarantee** | Financial guarantee to a third party | Advance payment, contract performance |

**Fees:**
- Letters of credit: 0.15% per month on the value (minimum £150)
- Documentary collections: £75 per set of documents
- Bank guarantees: 1–2% per annum on the guaranteed amount

## Export and Import Financing

- **Export finance:** pre-shipment and post-shipment funding to bridge the gap between dispatch and payment
- **Import finance:** fund purchase orders before goods arrive and are sold
- **International factoring:** sell your export receivables to accelerate cash flow; available for invoices to buyers in 40+ countries
- **Invoice discounting:** draw down against unpaid international invoices (up to 85% of face value)
