## Overview

The ToggleBank Premium Current Account is our flagship everyday banking product, designed for customers who want a superior banking experience with premium benefits included as standard. For a monthly fee of **£15**, you receive a comprehensive suite of financial perks, insurance cover, and dedicated support.

| Feature | Detail |

## Included Insurance Benefits

- **Comprehensive worldwide travel insurance** — single trip and annual multi-trip cover for you and your family, including winter sports and cruise cover
- **Mobile phone insurance** — cover for loss, theft, damage, and unauthorised calls up to £1,000 per claim for up to two phones registered to the account
- **European motor breakdown cover** — roadside assistance, recovery, and onward travel across the UK and Europe
- **Sentinel card protection** — if your wallet is lost or stolen, one call cancels all registered cards

## Dedicated Relationship Manager

All Premium Current Account holders are assigned a named relationship manager based in the UK. Your relationship manager can assist with:

- Day-to-day banking queries and account management
- Product recommendations tailored to your financial goals
- Priority access to new products and preferential rates on mortgages, loans, and savings
- Appointment booking at any ToggleBank branch

## Eligibility Requirements

To open a Premium Current Account, you must meet **at least one** of the following criteria:

- **Annual income:** £75,000 or more (sole or joint), paid into a ToggleBank account
- **Savings and investments:** £100,000 or more held across ToggleBank savings, ISAs, or investment accounts

Additional requirements:

- You must be aged 18 or over
- You must be a UK resident with a UK address
- You must pass our identity verification and credit checks

## Interest Details

Interest is calculated daily on the cleared credit balance and paid monthly. The rate of **1.5% AER (variable)** applies to balances up to £5,000. Balances above £5,000 earn 0.10% AER. Interest rates may change; we will give you at least 60 days' personal notice before any reduction.

## Overdraft

An arranged overdraft facility is available subject to status. See **TB-PREM-CURR-2026-fees-and-charges** for full overdraft pricing. Overdraft lending is subject to affordability assessment.

## Switching to ToggleBank

We support the **Current Account Switch Service (CASS)**. Your switch will be completed within 7 working days, and we guarantee to move your direct debits, standing orders, and salary payments automatically.
