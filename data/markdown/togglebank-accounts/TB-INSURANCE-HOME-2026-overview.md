# ToggleBank Home Insurance

## Product Overview

ToggleBank Home Insurance provides flexible **buildings**, **contents**, or **combined cover** to protect your home and belongings. With new-for-old replacement, a 24/7 claims line, and discounts for existing ToggleBank customers, you can protect what matters most at a competitive price.

**Product Code:** TB-INSURANCE-HOME-2026
**Underwritten by:** ToggleBank Insurance Services Ltd (authorised and regulated by the FCA, FRN 345678)

|---|---|---|
| **Buildings Insurance** | **£12/month** | Structure of your home, fixtures and fittings, permanent outbuildings, walls, gates, fences, paths, drives |

---

## Standard Cover Includes

- **New-for-old replacement** on all contents (except clothing and household linen, where a deduction for wear and tear applies)
- **Accidental loss or damage to underground pipes and cables** serving the home
- **Trace and access** cover for locating the source of a leak (up to £5,000)
- **Alternative accommodation** costs if your home is uninhabitable due to an insured event (up to £50,000 or 20% of buildings sum insured)
- **Legal expenses cover** included as standard — up to £50,000 for property disputes, personal injury claims, and employment matters
- **Locks replacement** following theft or loss of keys (up to £1,000)

## Eligibility

- Available to **UK residents** aged 18 or over
- Property must be located in **England, Wales, Scotland, or Northern Ireland**
- Property must be your primary residence (separate products available for landlords and holiday homes)
- Buildings cover requires a valid rebuild cost estimate (tools available at togglebank.co.uk/rebuild-calculator)
