# ToggleBank Children's Savings Account

**Product Code:** TB-KIDS-SAVINGS-2026
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026
**Account Type:** Children's Savings Account

---

## Overview

The ToggleBank Children's Savings Account is designed to help young people learn the value of saving, with a competitive interest rate and engaging in-app features that make saving fun. Parents and guardians manage the account for younger children, while teenagers gain the independence to manage their own money.

## Key Features

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Interest rate | **3.50% AER (variable)** |
| Eligible age | **0–17** |
| Minimum balance | **No minimum** |
| Maximum balance | £50,000 |
| Who can open | Parent or legal guardian (ages 0–15) / Child directly (ages 16–17) |
| Withdrawals | Permitted (see age-based access below) |
| Interest payment | Annual (credited to the account) |
| FSCS protection | Yes — up to £85,000 |

## Age-Based Access and Management

| Age Group | Who Opens | Who Manages | Withdrawal Access |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0–10** | Parent/guardian | Parent/guardian only | Parent/guardian only |
| **11–15** | Parent/guardian | Parent/guardian (child can view in app) | Parent/guardian only |
| **16–17** | Child can open independently | Child manages directly | Child has full access |

- Parents/guardians retain visibility and notification controls until the child turns 18.
- At age 18, the account can be converted to a ToggleBank Easy Access Savings Account or Cash ISA.

## Learn-to-Save Features in the App

The ToggleBank app includes dedicated features designed to help children develop good financial habits:

### Goal-Setting Tools

- **Set savings goals** — name a goal (e.g., "New Bike", "Holiday Spending Money"), set a target amount, and track progress with visual progress bars
- **Multiple goals** — set up to 5 savings goals at once
- **Celebrate milestones** — the app celebrates when a goal is reached with animations and badges
- **Goal history** — see completed goals to build a sense of achievement

### Savings Challenges

| Challenge | Description | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Penny Challenge | Save 1p on day 1, 2p on day 2, etc. | 30 or 365 days |
| Round-Up Challenge | Round up pocket money to the nearest £1 | Ongoing |
| No-Spend Week | Try not to spend for a full week | 7 days |
| Birthday Saver | Save a set amount each month towards birthday gifts | 12 months |

### Educational Content

- Age-appropriate articles and videos about saving, budgeting, and money management
- Interactive quizzes with badges for completion
- "Money Moments" — bite-sized tips that appear when the child logs in

## Parental Controls and Notifications

Parents and guardians have access to a comprehensive set of controls:

| Control | Description |
|---|---|
| **Transaction notifications** | Instant push notification for every deposit and withdrawal |
| **Balance alerts** | Set custom alerts when the balance drops below or exceeds a threshold |
| **Spending limits** | Set daily/weekly withdrawal limits (for ages 11–15 with parental-approved access) |
| **View transactions** | Full visibility of all account activity in the parent's ToggleBank app |
| **Freeze account** | Instantly freeze and unfreeze the child's account |
| **Manage standing orders** | Set up regular pocket money payments from the parent's current account |

| Method | Detail |

## Interest Details

- The current rate is **3.50% AER (variable)**, applied to the full balance.
- Interest is calculated daily on the cleared balance and credited annually on the account anniversary.
- The rate is variable and may change. We will provide at least **60 days' notice** of any reduction.

## Tax Considerations

- Children have a **Personal Savings Allowance** and a personal tax-free allowance, so most children will not pay tax on savings interest.
- However, if a **parent** contributes and the interest earned from parental gifts exceeds **£100 per year**, the full interest amount (not just the excess) is taxed as the parent's income.
- Contributions from grandparents, other family members, and friends are not subject to this rule.
- For fully tax-free children's savings, consider the ToggleBank **Junior Cash ISA**.

## Eligibility

- The child must be **aged 0–17**
- The parent/guardian must be a **UK resident** with a valid UK address
- The parent/guardian must hold or open a ToggleBank current account
- For ages 16–17: the young person can open the account independently with photo ID

## How to Open

1. **Online** — Apply at togglebank.co.uk/kids-savings (parent/guardian)
2. **Mobile app** — Open through the ToggleBank app
3. **In branch** — Visit any ToggleBank branch with the child's birth certificate and parent's photo ID
