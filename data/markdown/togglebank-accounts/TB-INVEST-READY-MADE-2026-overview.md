# ToggleBank Ready-Made Investment Portfolios

**Product Code:** TB-INVEST-READY-MADE-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Regulated by:** Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)

## Overview

ToggleBank Ready-Made Portfolios offer a simple, hands-off way to invest. Choose a portfolio that matches your risk appetite, and the ToggleBank Investment Management team takes care of the rest — selecting funds, monitoring performance, and rebalancing to keep your portfolio on track.

## Portfolio Options

Five risk-rated portfolios are available, each with a standard version and an ESG/Sustainable alternative:

| Portfolio | Risk Level | Target Equity | Target Bonds | Target Alternatives | Target Cash |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Defensive** | Low | 20% | 65% | 10% | 5% |
| **Cautious** | Low–Medium | 35% | 50% | 10% | 5% |
| **Balanced** | Medium | 55% | 30% | 10% | 5% |
| **Growth** | Medium–High | 75% | 15% | 7% | 3% |
| **Adventurous** | High | 90% | 3% | 5% | 2% |

Each portfolio is diversified across UK and international equities, government and corporate bonds, property, commodities, and cash.

## ESG/Sustainable Portfolios

Every risk level has a corresponding sustainable portfolio that follows the same asset allocation but uses ESG-screened and impact-focused funds:

- Exclusions: companies deriving significant revenue from fossil fuels, tobacco, controversial weapons, or gambling
- Positive screening: preference for companies with strong environmental, social, and governance practices
- Impact holdings: allocations to green bonds, renewable energy funds, and social housing REITs
- Same target allocations and rebalancing frequency as standard portfolios
- ESG portfolios carry the same total annual cost

## How the Portfolios Are Managed

- **Managed by ToggleBank Investment Management** — a dedicated team of portfolio managers and analysts
- **Quarterly rebalancing** — portfolios are reviewed and rebalanced every quarter to maintain target allocations
- **Annual strategy review** — the investment committee formally reviews the asset allocation and fund selection each year, with adjustments published in the annual portfolio update
- **Ongoing monitoring** — markets are monitored daily, and tactical adjustments may be made between quarterly reviews in exceptional circumstances

## Available Account Wrappers

Ready-Made Portfolios can be held within any of the following ToggleBank accounts:

| Account | Tax Treatment | Annual Limit |
|---|---|---|
| **Stocks & Shares ISA** | Tax-free growth and income | £20,000 per tax year |
| **Self-Invested Personal Pension (SIPP)** | Tax relief on contributions; income taxed at withdrawal | £60,000 per tax year |
| **General Investment Account (GIA)** | No tax wrapper; CGT and dividend tax may apply | No limit |
| **Junior ISA** | Tax-free; accessible at age 18 | £9,000 per tax year |

You can hold the same or different portfolios across multiple wrappers.

## Performance

ToggleBank publishes portfolio performance on a quarterly basis at togglebank.co.uk/ready-made-performance. Past performance data includes:

- Cumulative returns (1 year, 3 years, 5 years, since inception)
- Calendar year returns
- Comparison against a composite benchmark for each risk level
- Risk metrics (volatility, maximum drawdown)

**Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results.**

## Switching Portfolios

- You can switch between risk levels or between standard and ESG portfolios at any time
- Switching is free of charge
- When switching within an ISA or SIPP, there are no tax implications
- Switching within a GIA may trigger a Capital Gains Tax event — consult your tax adviser

## How to Invest

1. Complete the ToggleBank risk questionnaire at togglebank.co.uk/risk-profile or in the app
2. Review your recommended portfolio (you may choose a different risk level if preferred)
3. Select standard or ESG/Sustainable
4. Choose your account wrapper (ISA, SIPP, GIA, or Junior ISA)
5. Fund your investment by lump sum, regular monthly contribution, or both
6. Your portfolio is invested within 2 business days
