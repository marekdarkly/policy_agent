# ToggleBank Mobile App Guide

**Document Reference:** TB-GUIDE-MOBILE-APP-2026  
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026  
**Classification:** Customer-Facing

---

## Overview

The ToggleBank Mobile App puts your banking in your pocket. Available free on iOS and Android, it offers full account management with features designed around speed, security, and simplicity.

## Device Requirements

| Platform | Minimum Version | Download |
|---|---|---|
| Apple iOS | iOS 15.0 or later | App Store |
| Android | Android 10.0 or later | Google Play |

The app requires approximately **120 MB** of storage. A stable internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data) is required.

## Login & Security

- **Biometric login** — Sign in instantly using **fingerprint** (Touch ID / Android fingerprint) or **facial recognition** (Face ID / Android face unlock).
- **6-digit passcode** — Fallback login method if biometrics are unavailable.
- **Strong Customer Authentication (SCA)** — Required for sensitive actions such as adding new payees or changing security settings.

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| Instant spending notifications | Real-time push alerts for every debit and credit transaction |
| Card freeze/unfreeze | Instantly lock or unlock your debit or credit card with one tap |
| Cheque imaging | Deposit cheques by photographing front and back (up to £1,000 per cheque, £2,500 daily limit) |
| Branch & ATM finder | Locate the nearest ToggleBank branch or free ATM using GPS |
| Budgeting tools | Automatic categorisation of spending (groceries, transport, entertainment, etc.) |
| Spending insights | Monthly and weekly summaries with trend analysis and comparisons |
| Savings goals | Set named goals (e.g. "Holiday 2026"), track progress, auto-save with round-ups |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay setup | Link your ToggleBank card in under 60 seconds |
| Secure messaging | Chat with your bank directly through the app |

## Widgets & Quick Actions

- **Balance widget** — Add a home screen widget for at-a-glance balance checks (iOS and Android). Balances are masked by default and revealed with biometric authentication.
- **Quick actions** — Long-press the app icon to access shortcuts:
  - Pay a contact
  - Freeze card
  - View recent transactions
  - Scan a cheque

## Display Settings

- **Dark mode** — Automatic (follows system setting) or manual toggle in app preferences.
- **Text size** — Supports dynamic type / system font scaling for accessibility.
- **Language** — Available in English and Welsh.

## Notifications & Alerts

Customise which notifications you receive:

- Transaction alerts (on/off, or threshold-based e.g. "only above £50")
- Direct Debit reminders (1 day before collection)
- Low balance warnings (configurable threshold)
- Security alerts (always on, cannot be disabled)
- Marketing and offers (opt-in only)

## Payments & Transfers

- **Faster Payments** — Send up to £25,000 to any UK bank account (typically arrives within seconds).
- **Scheduled payments** — Set up one-off future-dated payments or standing orders.
- **Pay a contact** — Send money using a phone number or email if the recipient has enrolled in Paym.
- **QR payments** — Scan a QR code to pay ToggleBank merchants instantly.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|---|---|
| App not loading | Check internet connection; force-close and reopen |
| Biometric login not working | Ensure biometrics are enabled in device settings and app settings |
| Not receiving notifications | Check notification permissions in device settings |
| Cheque image rejected | Ensure cheque is on a dark, flat surface with good lighting |
| App update required | Download the latest version from App Store / Google Play |
