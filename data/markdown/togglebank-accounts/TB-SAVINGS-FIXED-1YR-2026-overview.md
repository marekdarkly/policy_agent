# ToggleBank 1-Year Fixed Rate Bond

**Product Code:** TB-SAVINGS-FIXED-1YR-2026
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2026
**Account Type:** Fixed Rate Savings Bond (12 Months)

---

## Overview

The ToggleBank 1-Year Fixed Rate Bond offers a **guaranteed interest rate** for a full 12-month term, giving you certainty over your returns regardless of market fluctuations. Lock in today's competitive rate and know exactly what you'll earn at the end of the term.

## Key Features

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Interest rate (maturity) | **4.50% AER (fixed)** |
| Interest rate (monthly) | **4.41% AER (fixed)** if interest paid monthly |
| Term | **12 months** (fixed) |
| Minimum deposit | **£1,000** |
| Maximum deposit | **£1,000,000** |
| Withdrawals during term | **Not permitted** (see exceptions below) |
| Interest payment options | At maturity or monthly |
| FSCS protection | Yes — up to £85,000 |

## Interest Options

You choose how you would like your interest paid when you open the account:

| Option | Rate | How It Works |
|---|---|---|
| **At maturity** | 4.50% AER | Interest is calculated daily and paid as a lump sum when the bond matures. This option maximises your return. |
| **Monthly** | 4.41% AER | Interest is paid monthly into a nominated ToggleBank current or savings account. The slightly lower AER reflects the earlier payment of interest. |

Once selected, the interest payment option cannot be changed during the term.

## Deposit Rules

- You may fund your bond with a **single deposit or multiple deposits** within the first **14 days** of opening.
- After the 14-day funding window, no further deposits are accepted.
- Funds must come from a UK bank account in your name.

## Withdrawal Restrictions

- **No withdrawals are permitted** during the 12-month fixed term.
- Exceptions apply only in the following circumstances:
  - **Death** of the account holder — funds released to the estate
  - **Serious financial hardship** — assessed on a case-by-case basis at our discretion
  - **Court order** — where required by law

If early access is granted in exceptional circumstances, an early closure charge equivalent to **90 days' interest** may be applied.

## What Happens at Maturity

We will write to you at least **14 days before maturity** with your options:

1. **Reinvest** into a new Fixed Rate Bond at the prevailing rate
2. **Transfer** the balance to a ToggleBank savings or current account
3. **Withdraw** the balance to an external bank account

If you take no action, the maturity proceeds (capital plus interest, if applicable) will be transferred to a ToggleBank Easy Access Savings Account where it will earn the current Easy Access rate.

## Eligibility

- Aged **18 or over**
- **UK resident** with a valid UK address
- Must hold or open a ToggleBank current account (for identification and maturity proceeds)

## 14-Day Cancellation Period

You have the right to cancel your Fixed Rate Bond within **14 days** of opening. If you cancel during this period, your full deposit will be returned with any interest earned to that point. No penalties apply.

## Tax on Savings Interest

Interest is paid **gross** (without tax deducted). Depending on your tax band and Personal Savings Allowance, you may owe income tax on the interest earned. See HMRC guidance or speak to a tax advisor for details.
