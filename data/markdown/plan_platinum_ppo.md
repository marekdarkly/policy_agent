## Plan Overview

The ToggleHealth Platinum PPO is our premium health insurance plan offering maximum flexibility and the lowest out-of-pocket costs. This plan is ideal for members who want the freedom to see any specialist without a referral and access to the largest provider network.

### Key Features

- **No Primary Care Physician (PCP) Required** - See any in-network provider without designation
- **No Referrals Needed** - Direct access to specialists
- **Out-of-Network Coverage** - 60% coverage for out-of-network providers
- **Largest Provider Network** - 14,789 specialists across five states
- **Premium Concierge Service Included** - Dedicated care navigation
- **Acupuncture Covered** - Up to 20 visits per year

## Services NOT Covered (Exclusions)

The Platinum PPO plan (TH-PPO-PLATINUM-2024) does NOT cover the following services:

### Not Covered by Platinum PPO

- **Cosmetic procedures** not medically necessary (elective plastic surgery, cosmetic dentistry, etc.)
- **Experimental or investigational treatments** not approved by FDA or recognized medical organizations
- **Dental care** (except for accidental injury to natural teeth)
- **Routine vision care** (except for annual eye exams - frames and lenses not covered)
- **Hearing aids** - Limited coverage may be available through discount programs
- **Private duty nursing**
- **Custodial care** (assistance with activities of daily living when not medically necessary)
- **Services received outside service area** (except emergency care)

**Note:** The Platinum PPO has fewer exclusions than other plans. For example, acupuncture IS covered under Platinum PPO but not under other ToggleHealth plans.

## Claims and Reimbursement

### In-Network Claims

When you see in-network providers, they will file claims directly with ToggleHealth. You are responsible only for copays, coinsurance, and deductibles.

### Out-of-Network Claims

When you see out-of-network providers, you may need to file a claim for reimbursement:

**How to File a Claim:**
1. Pay the provider in full
2. Request an itemized bill
3. Complete a claim form (download at my.togglehealth.com)
4. Submit to: ToggleHealth Claims Department, PO Box 98234, Seattle, WA 98109

**Required Documentation:**
- Itemized bill or receipt
- Provider name and tax ID
- Date of service
- Diagnosis code (ICD-10)
- Procedure code (CPT)
- Member ID number

**Claim Filing Deadline:** 365 days from date of service

**Processing Time:**
- Electronic claims: 14-21 days
- Paper claims: 30-45 days

### Appeals Process

If your claim is denied, you have the right to appeal:

**First Level Appeal:**
- Timeframe: Must file within 180 days of denial
- Address: ToggleHealth Appeals, PO Box 98235, Seattle, WA 98109
- Response Time: 30 days

**Second Level Appeal:**
- Timeframe: Must file within 60 days of first level denial
- Review Type: Independent external review
- Response Time: 45 days

## Frequently Asked Questions - Platinum PPO

**Q: Do I need to choose a primary care physician with the Platinum PPO?**
A: No. The Platinum PPO does not require you to select a PCP. You have the freedom to see any in-network provider.

**Q: Do I need a referral to see a specialist?**
A: No. You can see any specialist directly without a referral.

**Q: Can I see out-of-network providers?**
A: Yes. The Platinum PPO covers out-of-network providers at 60% after you meet the out-of-network deductible ($1,500 individual / $3,000 family).

**Q: What if I need care while traveling?**
A: Emergency care is covered anywhere in the United States at in-network rates. For non-emergency care, you may use out-of-network benefits or see in-network providers in the PPO-PREMIER network across five states.

**Q: How does the Premium Concierge Service work?**
A: All Platinum PPO members receive a dedicated care navigator who can help with appointment scheduling, finding specialists, coordinating care, and answering benefits questions. Call 1-800-TOGGLE-1 and ask for Concierge Services.

**Q: What's the difference between Platinum PPO and Gold HMO?**
A: Platinum PPO offers more flexibility (no PCP, no referrals, out-of-network coverage), lower out-of-pocket costs, and Premium Concierge Service. Gold HMO has lower premiums but requires a PCP and referrals for specialists.
