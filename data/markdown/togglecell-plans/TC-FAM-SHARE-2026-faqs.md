# TC-FAM-SHARE-2026 — Frequently Asked Questions

## 1. How do I add a new line to my Family Share plan?

You can add a new line at any time through the **ToggleCell Family app**, online at [togglecell.co.uk/my-account](https://togglecell.co.uk/my-account), or by calling 0800 064 2026. The additional line costs **£12/month** and is added to your existing bill. You can add up to **4 additional lines** (5 lines total). A new SIM or eSIM will be activated within 24 hours.

## 2. Can I remove a line from the plan without cancelling the whole thing?

Yes. Individual lines can be removed from the Family Share plan at any time with **30 days' notice**. Removing a line does not affect the remaining lines or the primary account. The base charge of £35/month always applies for the primary line. If you remove a line mid-contract, no early termination fee applies for that individual line — only the base plan is subject to the 24-month minimum term.

## 3. How is the 80GB data pool shared between lines?

All lines draw from the same **80GB shared pool**. By default, any line can use as much data as it needs until the pool is exhausted. However, the account holder can set **individual data caps** for any line via the ToggleCell Family app. This prevents one user from consuming the entire pool.

## 4. Can I set parental controls on a child's line?

Yes. The Family Share plan includes comprehensive **parental controls** at no extra cost. Through the ToggleCell Family app, you can set content filters (three levels), app time limits, bedtime mode (data off between set hours), spend caps, and call/text blocking. Content filtering is enabled by default on Strict for lines assigned to users under 18.

## 5. Who is responsible for paying the bill?

The **primary account holder** (designated bill payer) is responsible for the full monthly bill, which covers all lines. Only one bill is issued per Family Share plan. The bill provides a breakdown of usage and charges for each individual line, making it easy to track who is using what.

## 6. What happens if we use all 80GB before the end of the month?

If the shared data pool is exhausted, data access is paused on all lines until the next billing cycle. You can purchase a **data bolt-on** (10GB for £8 or 20GB for £15) to top up the shared pool. Only the account holder can purchase bolt-ons. Alternatively, you can enable mobile data over WiFi to continue using the internet without consuming pool data.

## 7. Is there a minimum age for adding a line?

There is **no minimum age** for additional lines — children of any age can be added to the plan. However, the **primary account holder must be aged 18 or over** and is legally responsible for all charges on the account. Parental controls are automatically applied to lines assigned to users under 18.

## 8. Can family members keep their existing phone numbers?

Yes. Each family member can port their existing number to ToggleCell by providing a **PAC code** from their current network (text **PAC to 65075**). Each number port typically completes within **1 business day**. All ports can be processed simultaneously — there's no need to wait for one to finish before starting another.
