# TC-5G-UNLIM-2026 — Device Financing

## Device Eligibility

As a **5G Unlimited** customer, you are eligible to purchase any device in the ToggleCell range on finance. This includes the latest smartphones from Apple, Samsung, Google, Sony, and OnePlus, as well as tablets and smartwatches.

## 0% APR Finance

All device purchases on the 5G Unlimited plan qualify for **0% APR representative** financing over your choice of term:

| Term | Monthly device cost example (iPhone 16 Pro, £1,199 RRP) |
|---|---|
| **24 months** | £49.96/month |
| **36 months** | £33.31/month |

Your device cost is added to your monthly plan bill as a separate line item. There is no deposit required for customers with an approved credit check.

## Credit Check

A credit check is required for all device finance agreements. ToggleCell uses a soft credit search for eligibility checks, which does not affect your credit score. A hard search is performed only when you confirm your order.

## Trade-In Programme

ToggleCell's **device trade-in programme** allows you to offset the cost of a new device by trading in your old one. Key details:

- **Up to £600 credit** depending on device model and condition
- **200+ devices accepted** — including iPhones, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, and more
- **Instant valuation** via the ToggleCell app or website
- **Data wiping service** included at no charge
- **Free postage** — a prepaid shipping label is provided
- **Valuation valid for 14 days** from the date of quote

Trade-in credit can be applied as a lump sum to your device finance balance or spread as a monthly bill credit.

## Flex Upgrade

With **Flex Upgrade**, 5G Unlimited customers can upgrade to a new device **after just 12 months**, even on a 24-month or 36-month finance agreement. To use Flex Upgrade:

1. You must have made at least **12 consecutive on-time payments**
2. Your current device must be returned in **good working condition** (no cracked screen, water damage, or hardware faults)
3. Any remaining balance on your current device is waived when you start a new finance agreement

Flex Upgrade is included at no extra cost with the 5G Unlimited plan.

## Device Insurance

Protect your device with **ToggleCell Device Cover**:

| Cover level | Monthly cost | What's covered |
|---|---|---|
| **Essential** | £8/month | Accidental damage, mechanical breakdown (after warranty) |
| **Complete** | £12/month | Accidental damage, theft, loss, mechanical breakdown |

- Excess fee: **£49** for screen repair, **£79** for other claims
- Claims handled within **48 hours** — replacement or repair
- **AppleCare+** can be added to any iPhone purchase at the point of sale as an alternative to ToggleCell Device Cover
- Cover can be added within **30 days** of device purchase
