# TC-5G-UNLIM-2026 — Extras and Add-Ons

## Included Extras

The **5G Unlimited** plan comes with the following extras at no additional cost:

- **ToggleCell Cloud 200GB** — Back up your photos, videos, contacts, and app data to ToggleCell's secure UK-based cloud. Accessible via the ToggleCell app on iOS, Android, and web. Additional storage available at £3/month for 1TB.
- **Apple TV+ (6 months free)** — Enjoy Apple's streaming service free for 6 months from activation. After the promotional period, the subscription continues at £8.99/month unless cancelled. Available to new Apple TV+ subscribers only.
- **Spotify Premium (3 months free)** — Stream ad-free music for 3 months at no charge. After the trial, Spotify Premium continues at £10.99/month unless cancelled. Available to new Spotify Premium subscribers only.

## Broadband Bundle Discount

Combine your 5G Unlimited plan with **ToggleCell Fibre broadband** and receive a **£10/month discount** on your broadband bill. This applies to all three broadband tiers:

| Broadband tier | Standard price | With mobile bundle discount |
|---|---|---|
| TC Fibre (36 Mbps) | £25/month | **£15/month** |
| TC Fibre Plus (150 Mbps) | £35/month | **£25/month** |
| TC Fibre Ultra (900 Mbps) | £45/month | **£35/month** |

The discount applies for the duration of your mobile contract and broadband contract. Both services must remain active to retain the discount.

## Bolt-On Packages

Enhance your plan with optional bolt-ons, which can be added or removed at any time via the ToggleCell app:

### International Calls Bolt-On
- **£5/month** — Includes **100 minutes** to 50+ international destinations (landlines and mobiles)
- Covers popular destinations including the USA, Canada, India, Pakistan, Australia, France, Germany, Spain, and Poland
- Minutes do not roll over; unused minutes expire at the end of each billing cycle

### Extra Hotspot Data
- **£3/month** — Additional **20GB** mobile hotspot data on top of your included 100GB

### ToggleCell Cloud Upgrade
- **£3/month** — Upgrade from 200GB to **1TB** cloud storage

## Family Discount

Add a second (or third, fourth, or fifth) ToggleCell mobile line to your account and receive a **15% discount** on each additional line's monthly plan cost. This applies to any ToggleCell plan, not just 5G Unlimited. The discount is applied automatically when lines are linked under the same account holder.

For example:
- Primary line: 5G Unlimited at **£45/month**
- Second line: SIM Only Flex at £20/month → **£17/month** (15% off)
- Third line: SIM Only Flex at £20/month → **£17/month** (15% off)
