# ToggleCell eSIM and SIM Guide

## Overview

ToggleCell supports both **physical nano-SIM** and **eSIM** across all plans (5G Unlimited, SIM Only Flex, Family Share, and Pay As You Go). This guide covers ordering, activation, device compatibility, and switching between SIM types.

## eSIM

### What Is eSIM?

An **eSIM (embedded SIM)** is a digital SIM built into your device. Instead of inserting a physical card, you activate your plan by scanning a QR code. eSIM offers several advantages:

- **Instant activation** — No waiting for postal delivery
- **Dual SIM support** — Use two numbers on one device (e.g., personal and work)
- **No physical card to lose** — Your SIM is securely embedded in your device

### Compatible Devices

eSIM is supported on the following devices (and later models):

- **Apple**: iPhone XS, iPhone XR, iPhone SE (2nd generation), all iPad Pro/Air with cellular
- **Samsung**: Galaxy S20, Galaxy Z Flip, Galaxy Z Fold2, Galaxy Note20
- **Google**: Pixel 3, Pixel 3a, Pixel 4, Pixel 5, Pixel 6, Pixel 7, Pixel 8
- **Other**: Microsoft Surface Pro, Motorola Razr, Oppo Find X5 Pro, Sony Xperia 1 IV

For a full compatibility list, check [togglecell.co.uk/esim](https://togglecell.co.uk/esim).

### How to Activate eSIM

1. **Order or upgrade** — Choose eSIM during sign-up or upgrade in the ToggleCell app
2. **Receive your QR code** — A QR code will appear in the ToggleCell app and be sent to your email
3. **Scan the QR code** — On your device, go to **Settings > Mobile/Cellular > Add eSIM** and scan the code
4. **Activation completes** — Your eSIM will be active within minutes. If porting a number, activation completes when the port is finalised (typically within 1 business day)

### Dual SIM Usage

With eSIM, you can use **two phone numbers on one device**:

- One eSIM + one physical SIM
- Some newer devices support two eSIMs simultaneously (iPhone 14 and later, Pixel 7 and later)

You can choose which number to use for calls, texts, and data in your device settings.

## Physical Nano-SIM

### Ordering a Physical SIM

If you prefer a traditional SIM card:

- **Online/app orders**: A free nano-SIM is delivered by **next-day Royal Mail** (orders placed before 8pm Monday–Friday)
- **In-store**: Walk into any ToggleCell store and receive a SIM immediately
- **Replacement SIMs**: Free of charge — order in the app or in store

### SIM Activation

Physical SIMs are activated **within 24 hours** of being inserted into your device and connected to the ToggleCell network. If you are porting a number, activation is tied to the port completion time (typically 1 business day).

## Switching Between Physical SIM and eSIM

You can switch from a physical SIM to an eSIM (or vice versa) at any time:

1. Open the **ToggleCell app**
2. Go to **Account > SIM Management > Switch SIM Type**
3. Follow the on-screen instructions

The switch typically takes **up to 2 hours** to complete. During the transition, you may experience a brief period without service (usually under 30 minutes). Your phone number, plan, and all settings are preserved.

## Keeping Your Number (Porting)

To bring your existing number from another network to ToggleCell:

1. **Request a PAC code** from your current provider — text **PAC to 65075** from your current phone
2. **Provide the PAC code** to ToggleCell during sign-up (online, in-app, or in-store)
3. **Wait for the port** — Number porting typically completes within **1 business day**
4. **Confirmation** — You'll receive an SMS on your new ToggleCell SIM/eSIM when the port is complete

Your old SIM will stop working once the port is finalised. All incoming calls and texts to your number will be routed to your new ToggleCell SIM from that point forward.

## Lost or Stolen SIM

If your SIM is lost or stolen:

1. **Block your SIM immediately** — Call **0800 064 2026** or use the ToggleCell app to suspend your line
2. **Order a replacement** — A new SIM (physical or eSIM) can be activated within 24 hours
3. **Your number is preserved** — Your phone number transfers to the replacement SIM

There is **no charge** for a replacement SIM or eSIM.
