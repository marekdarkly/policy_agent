# TC-SIM-FLEX-2026 — SIM Only Flex Plan Overview

## Plan Summary

The **SIM Only Flex** plan is ToggleCell's no-commitment mid-range plan, offering excellent value with the freedom to leave at any time. With 30GB of data, unlimited calls and texts, and no long-term contract, it's perfect for customers who want flexibility without sacrificing features.

## Key Details

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Monthly price** | £20/month |
| **Contract type** | 30-day rolling (no minimum term) |
| **Data** | 30GB (4G and 5G) |
| **Calls** | Unlimited UK landline and mobile |
| **Texts** | Unlimited UK SMS and MMS |
| **Mobile hotspot** | 20GB per month |
| **EU roaming** | Included — 15GB data in 48 destinations |
| **Data rollover** | Up to 10GB rolls to next month |

## Included Features

- **4G and 5G ready** — Access ToggleCell's 5G network where available, with 4G fallback nationwide. No extra charge for 5G access.
- **Data rollover** — Unused data (up to **10GB**) automatically rolls over to the following month. Rolled-over data is used first and cannot be rolled over a second time.
- **eSIM support** — Activate instantly via eSIM on compatible devices, or receive a free physical SIM by next-day delivery
- **WiFi calling** — Make calls over WiFi when mobile signal is limited, included at no extra cost
- **No credit check impact** — Rolling contracts use a soft credit check only, which does not appear on your credit file
- **Cancel anytime** — Give 30 days' notice and leave with no penalties or fees

## Who Is This Plan For?

The SIM Only Flex plan is ideal for customers who already own their handset, want a solid data allowance at a fair price, and value the ability to leave whenever they choose. It's also a great option for new customers who want to try ToggleCell before committing to a longer contract.

## How to Get Started

Sign up online at [togglecell.co.uk](https://togglecell.co.uk), through the ToggleCell app, or in store. You can keep your existing number — porting typically completes within **1 business day**. Your SIM (or eSIM) will be activated within **24 hours** of your order.
