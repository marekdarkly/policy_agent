# TC-SIM-FLEX-2026 — Data and Coverage

## Data Allowance

The **SIM Only Flex** plan includes **30GB of data** per month, usable on both ToggleCell's 4G and 5G networks.

| Feature | Allowance |
|---|---|
| **Monthly data** | 30GB |
| **Mobile hotspot** | 20GB (included in 30GB total) |
| **Data rollover** | Up to 10GB to next month |
| **Overage rate** | £1.00/GB (if data cap is off) |

## Data Rollover

Unused data automatically rolls over to the following month, up to a maximum of **10GB**. Rolled-over data is consumed **before** your fresh monthly allowance. Data can only roll over once — any unused rolled-over data expires at the end of the second month.

**Example**: If you use 22GB out of 30GB in March, 8GB rolls over to April, giving you up to 38GB available in April (8GB rollover + 30GB fresh allowance).

## Hotspot (Tethering)

You can use up to **20GB** of your monthly 30GB allowance as mobile hotspot data. This allows you to share your mobile connection with laptops, tablets, and other devices. The hotspot allowance is drawn from your main data pool — it is not an additional 20GB on top.

## Data Usage Management

ToggleCell provides several tools to help you manage your data:

- **Usage alerts** — Automatic SMS at **80%** and **100%** of your allowance
- **Data cap** — Optional feature to block data once your allowance is used, preventing overage charges
- **Real-time usage tracker** — View your data consumption in the ToggleCell app, updated every 30 minutes
- **Bolt-ons** — Add 5GB (£5) or 10GB (£8) instantly if you need more data before your next cycle

## Estimated Speeds

| Network | Typical download speed | Typical upload speed |
|---|---|---|
| **5G** | Up to 300 Mbps | Up to 50 Mbps |
| **4G LTE** | Up to 60 Mbps | Up to 20 Mbps |

Speeds vary by location, device capability, and network congestion. 5G is available in 120+ UK cities; 4G covers 99% of the UK outdoor population.
