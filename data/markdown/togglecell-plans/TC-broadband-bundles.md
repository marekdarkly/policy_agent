# ToggleCell Fibre Broadband Bundles

## Overview

**ToggleCell Fibre** is ToggleCell's home broadband service, available across the UK. Combine any ToggleCell mobile plan with a ToggleCell Fibre broadband package and save on your monthly bill. All broadband plans include a **free wireless router**, unlimited data, and no setup fee.

## Broadband Tiers

| Plan | Download speed | Upload speed | Monthly price | With mobile bundle |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **TC Fibre** | 36 Mbps (average) | 9 Mbps | **£25/month** | **£15/month** |
| **TC Fibre Plus** | 150 Mbps (average) | 30 Mbps | **£35/month** | **£25/month** |
| **TC Fibre Ultra** | 900 Mbps (average) | 110 Mbps | **£45/month** | **£35/month** |

All speeds are averages based on Ofcom testing methodology. Actual speeds depend on your location, line quality, and in-home setup.

## Contract Terms

- **Contract length**: 18 months
- **Early termination fee**: Remaining months × monthly charge
- **No setup fee**: Installation is free of charge
- **Router**: ToggleCell WiFi Hub included at no cost (must be returned if you cancel within 18 months)

## Mobile Bundle Discount

Any ToggleCell mobile customer (on any plan, including PAYG with an active bundle) receives a **£10/month discount** on their broadband bill for the duration of their mobile and broadband contracts. Both services must remain active to retain the discount. The discount applies to one broadband connection per mobile account.

## Installation

- **Standard installation**: Within **2 weeks** of order. A ToggleCell engineer will visit your property to install the broadband connection and set up your router.
- **Self-install**: Available at some addresses. A self-install kit is sent by post, and you connect the router yourself. Self-install typically takes 10 minutes.
- **Installation window**: You will be given a choice of morning (8am–1pm) or afternoon (1pm–6pm) slots.

## What's Included

- **Unlimited data** — No download caps or usage limits on any broadband tier
- **ToggleCell WiFi Hub** — Dual-band router with WiFi 6 support, suitable for homes up to 150m²
- **Parental controls** — Network-level content filtering, managed via the ToggleCell app
- **ToggleCell Broadband app** — Monitor speed, connected devices, and manage settings
- **24/7 technical support** — Dedicated broadband support line at 0800 064 2027

## Availability

ToggleCell Fibre is available at over **25 million UK premises**. Check availability at your address at [togglecell.co.uk/broadband](https://togglecell.co.uk/broadband). TC Fibre Ultra (900 Mbps) is available at addresses served by full-fibre (FTTP) infrastructure, covering approximately 12 million premises.

## How to Order

Order online at [togglecell.co.uk/broadband](https://togglecell.co.uk/broadband), via the ToggleCell app, or in any ToggleCell store. Existing mobile customers can add broadband to their account in the app under **Account > Add Broadband**.
