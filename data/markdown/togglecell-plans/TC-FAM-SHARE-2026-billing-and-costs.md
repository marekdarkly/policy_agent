# TC-FAM-SHARE-2026 — Billing and Costs

## Monthly Charges

The **Family Share** plan uses a base-plus-per-line pricing model:

| Component | Cost |
|---|---|
| **Base charge** (includes primary line) | £35.00/month |
| **Each additional line** | £12.00/month |

### Cost Examples

| Family size | Calculation | Total monthly cost |
|---|---|---|
| 2 lines | £35 + (1 × £12) | **£47/month** |
| 3 lines | £35 + (2 × £12) | **£59/month** |
| 4 lines | £35 + (3 × £12) | **£71/month** |
| 5 lines | £35 + (4 × £12) | **£83/month** |

## Single Consolidated Bill

All lines are billed together on a **single monthly bill**. The bill is broken down by line, making it easy to see each family member's usage and any additional charges. The **designated bill payer** (primary account holder) is responsible for the full amount.

## Payment Methods

- **Direct Debit** — Recommended for automatic monthly payments
- **Debit or credit card** — Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **In-app payment** — Via the ToggleCell app using stored card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay

## Individual Data Caps

The account holder can configure **individual data caps** for each line to control how the 80GB shared pool is divided. For example:

- Parent 1: No cap (uses remaining pool)
- Parent 2: No cap
- Child 1: 10GB cap
- Child 2: 10GB cap

When a capped line reaches its limit, data access is paused for that line until the next billing cycle. The remaining shared pool is still available to uncapped lines.

## Spending Limits

The account holder can set **per-line spending limits** for out-of-plan charges, including:

- Data bolt-ons
- International calls
- Premium-rate services
- In-app purchases billed to the mobile account

Spending limits can be set from **£0 to £50/month** per line and are managed via the ToggleCell Family app.

## Content Filters

**Content filtering** is enabled by default on all lines assigned to users under 18. Adult content, gambling sites, and other age-restricted material are blocked. The account holder can adjust filter levels for each line.

## Early Termination

The Family Share plan has a **24-month minimum term**. Early termination fee:

> **Remaining months × £10** per active line

For example, cancelling a 4-line plan with 8 months remaining: 8 × £10 × 4 = **£320**.

Individual lines can be removed from the plan without affecting other lines, but the base charge (£35) always applies for the primary line.

## Late Payment

A **late payment fee of £7.50** applies if your bill is not paid by the due date. Service may be suspended after 30 days of non-payment, affecting all lines on the account.
