# TC-5G-UNLIM-2026 — Billing and Costs

## Monthly Charges

The **5G Unlimited** plan costs **£45.00 per month**, billed in advance on your chosen bill date. There is **no one-time activation fee** for this plan.

## Bill Date Options

When you sign up, you can choose your preferred bill date:

- **1st of each month**
- **15th of each month**

Your first bill will be pro-rated if your activation date differs from your chosen bill date.

## Payment Methods

ToggleCell accepts the following payment methods for monthly plan charges:

- **Direct Debit** — Recommended; payments are taken automatically from your bank account
- **Debit or credit card** — Visa, Mastercard, and American Express accepted
- **In-app payment** — Pay directly through the ToggleCell app using stored card or Apple Pay / Google Pay

## Late Payment

If your payment fails or is not received by the due date, a **late payment fee of £7.50** will be applied to your next bill. If payment remains outstanding for more than 30 days, your service may be suspended. ToggleCell will attempt to contact you by email and SMS before any suspension.

## Early Termination

The TC-5G-UNLIM-2026 plan has a **24-month minimum term**. If you cancel before the end of your contract, an early termination fee applies:

> **Early termination fee** = remaining months × £15.00

For example, if you cancel with 10 months remaining, the fee would be **£150.00**.

## International Calls

International calls are charged on a per-minute basis, starting from **3p/min** depending on destination. A full rate card is available in the ToggleCell app under **Settings > Call Rates**. Premium-rate numbers (09xx, 118x) are **blocked by default** for your protection. You can unblock them via the app or by contacting support.

## Additional Charges

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Replacement SIM (physical) | £0.00 |
| Replacement eSIM | £0.00 |
| Paper bill (by post) | £1.50/month |
| International calls | From 3p/min |
| Premium number calls | Blocked by default |
| Directory enquiries (118) | Blocked by default |
| Late payment fee | £7.50 per occurrence |

## Viewing Your Bill

You can view, download, and manage your bills at any time through the **ToggleCell app** or at [togglecell.co.uk/my-account](https://togglecell.co.uk/my-account). Bills are also sent to your registered email address as a PDF attachment.
