# TC-PAYG-BASIC-2026 — Frequently Asked Questions

## 1. How do I top up my PAYG credit?

You can top up through the **ToggleCell app** (using card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay), online at [togglecell.co.uk/top-up](https://togglecell.co.uk/top-up), at any **ToggleCell store**, or at **40,000+ PayPoint retailers** across the UK. Available top-up amounts are £5, £10, £20, £30, and £50. You can also set up **auto top-up** to add credit automatically when your balance is low.

## 2. When does my credit expire?

Your PAYG credit expires **180 days after your last top-up**. To keep your credit active, you need to top up at least once every 180 days. ToggleCell sends SMS reminders at 30 days and 7 days before expiry. If your credit expires, your balance is forfeited, but your number is retained for a further 90 days.

## 3. How do I check my balance?

Dial ***#100#** from your ToggleCell phone — it's free and shows your current credit balance. You can also check your balance instantly in the **ToggleCell app** (shown on the home screen) or by logging into your account online at [togglecell.co.uk/my-account](https://togglecell.co.uk/my-account).

## 4. Can I switch from PAYG to a monthly plan?

Yes. You can upgrade to any ToggleCell monthly plan (SIM Only Flex, 5G Unlimited, or Family Share) at any time through the ToggleCell app, in store, or by calling 0800 064 2026. Your remaining PAYG credit will be **converted to a bill credit** on your new monthly plan. Your phone number stays the same — no porting required.

## 5. Can I make emergency calls if I have no credit?

Yes. **Emergency calls to 999 and 112 are always free**, even if your credit balance is zero. You can also call the **Samaritans (116 123)** and **Childline (0800 1111)** for free on ToggleCell, regardless of your credit balance.

## 6. How do I keep my number if I'm a light user?

To retain your ToggleCell PAYG number, you must **top up at least once every 180 days**. Even a minimum top-up of **£5** resets the 180-day timer. If you go 180 days without topping up, your credit is forfeited. If you go 270 days without a top-up, your number may be recycled and assigned to another customer.

## 7. What coverage does the PAYG plan get?

The PAYG plan uses the ToggleCell **4G network**, which covers **99% of the UK outdoor population**. 5G is not available on the PAYG plan. WiFi calling is supported on compatible devices, allowing you to make calls over WiFi when mobile signal is limited. Check your coverage at [togglecell.co.uk/coverage](https://togglecell.co.uk/coverage).

## 8. Is eSIM available for PAYG?

Yes. The PAYG plan supports **eSIM activation** on compatible devices (iPhone XS+, Samsung S20+, Google Pixel 3+). You can activate an eSIM instantly by scanning a QR code in the ToggleCell app. If you prefer a physical SIM, one will be delivered free by next-day post. You can switch between physical SIM and eSIM at any time by contacting support.
