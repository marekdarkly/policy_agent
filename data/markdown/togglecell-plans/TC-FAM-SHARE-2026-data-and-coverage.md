# TC-FAM-SHARE-2026 — Data and Coverage

## Shared Data Pool

The **Family Share** plan includes an **80GB shared data pool** that is divided among all lines on the account. Every line draws from the same pool unless the account holder sets individual data caps.

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Total shared data** | 80GB per month |
| **Network access** | 4G and 5G |
| **Hotspot per line** | 10GB (drawn from shared pool) |
| **EU roaming per line** | 10GB data |

## Individual Data Caps

The account holder can set a **data cap for each line** via the ToggleCell Family app. This is particularly useful for managing children's usage:

- **Example configuration** for a family of 4:
  - Parent 1: Uncapped
  - Parent 2: Uncapped
  - Child 1: 10GB cap
  - Child 2: 10GB cap

When a capped line reaches its limit, data is paused for that line. Other lines continue to access the remaining pool. Caps can be adjusted at any time.

## Bonus School Holiday Data

During official school holiday periods (based on the England and Wales schedule), an additional **20GB** is added to the shared data pool at no extra charge. This brings the total pool to **100GB** during holidays. The bonus data is applied automatically and is reflected in the ToggleCell app.

Holiday periods typically include:

- Easter (2 weeks)
- Summer (6 weeks, late July to early September)
- Half-terms (1 week each, October, February, May)
- Christmas (2 weeks)

## Hotspot (Tethering)

Each line can use up to **10GB per month** for mobile hotspot (tethering). This allowance is drawn from the shared 80GB pool — it is not additional data. Once a line reaches its 10GB hotspot limit, hotspot functionality is paused for that line until the next billing cycle.

## Estimated Speeds

| Network | Typical download | Typical upload |
|---|---|---|
| **5G** | 100–500 Mbps | 15–60 Mbps |
| **4G LTE** | 20–60 Mbps | 8–20 Mbps |

All lines on the Family Share plan have equal network priority. Speeds vary by location, time of day, and device.

## Coverage

- **5G**: Available in 120+ UK cities and major towns
- **4G**: 99% UK outdoor population coverage
- **WiFi calling**: Included on all lines at no extra cost
- **Coverage checker**: [togglecell.co.uk/coverage](https://togglecell.co.uk/coverage)

## EU Roaming

Each line includes **10GB of EU roaming data** and unlimited calls and texts in 48 EU and EEA destinations. If a line exceeds 10GB while roaming, a surcharge of £3/GB applies. Rest of world roaming packs are available from £6/day per line.
