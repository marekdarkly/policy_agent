# TC-5G-UNLIM-2026 — Data and Coverage

## Data Allowance

The **5G Unlimited** plan includes **truly unlimited data** with no monthly cap, no speed throttling, and no deprioritisation during normal usage. Your data works on both the ToggleCell 5G and 4G networks.

- **Mobile data**: Unlimited
- **Mobile hotspot (tethering)**: 100GB per month
- **Speed caps**: None — full network speeds at all times

## Estimated Speeds

Actual speeds depend on your location, network traffic, and device capability.

| Network | Typical download speed | Typical upload speed |
|---|---|---|
| **5G** | 150–1000 Mbps | 20–100 Mbps |
| **4G LTE** | 30–80 Mbps | 10–30 Mbps |

These are estimated ranges. In optimal conditions, 5G speeds can exceed 1 Gbps. Indoor performance may vary.

## Network Priority

As a 5G Unlimited customer, you receive **network priority during congestion**. When the network is busy, your data traffic is prioritised ahead of lower-tier plans. This means more consistent performance during peak hours in busy areas such as city centres, stadiums, and transport hubs.

## 5G Coverage

ToggleCell's 5G network is live in **120+ UK cities and major towns**, including:

- London, Manchester, Birmingham, Leeds, Glasgow, Edinburgh, Bristol, Liverpool, Sheffield, Nottingham, Newcastle, Cardiff, Belfast, Leicester, Brighton, Southampton, and more

The 5G rollout is expanding continuously, with **200+ additional towns** planned by the end of 2026. Check your coverage at [togglecell.co.uk/coverage](https://togglecell.co.uk/coverage).

## 4G Fallback

When 5G is not available, your device will automatically fall back to the ToggleCell **4G LTE network**, which covers **99% of the UK outdoor population**. The transition between 5G and 4G is seamless and handled automatically by your device.

## WiFi Calling

WiFi calling is **included and enabled by default** on the 5G Unlimited plan. When your mobile signal is weak — for example, in basements, older buildings, or rural areas — your phone will route calls and texts over your WiFi connection instead. WiFi calls are charged at the same rate as standard UK calls (i.e., included in your unlimited allowance).

## Indoor Coverage

For improved indoor coverage, ToggleCell recommends:

- Enabling **WiFi calling** in your device settings
- Using the ToggleCell **Smart Signal** app for signal diagnostics
- Connecting to 4G or 5G bands supported by your device

If you experience persistent indoor coverage issues, contact ToggleCell support to discuss a **ToggleCell Signal Booster** (available free of charge for eligible addresses).
