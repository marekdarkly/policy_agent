# TC-FAM-SHARE-2026 — Family Share Plan Overview

## Plan Summary

The **Family Share** plan is ToggleCell's multi-line plan designed for families and households. It provides a shared data pool, individual line management, and built-in parental controls — all on a single bill managed by one account holder.

## Key Details

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Monthly price** | £35/month (base) + £12/month per additional line |
| **Maximum lines** | 5 (1 primary + 4 additional) |
| **Contract length** | 24 months |
| **Shared data pool** | 80GB across all lines |
| **Calls per line** | Unlimited UK landline and mobile |
| **Texts per line** | Unlimited UK SMS and MMS |
| **Mobile hotspot** | 10GB per line |
| **EU roaming** | 10GB data per line in 48 destinations |

## Included Features

- **Shared data pool** — 80GB of 4G/5G data shared across all lines on the plan. The account holder can set individual data caps for each line.
- **Parental controls** — Built-in content filtering, app time limits, spend caps, and bedtime mode for children's lines, all managed via the **ToggleCell Family app**
- **Family locator** — Optional location sharing between family members through the ToggleCell Family app (requires consent from each member aged 16+)
- **Shared bill** — One consolidated monthly bill for all lines, managed by the designated bill payer
- **Individual spending limits** — Set per-line caps on additional charges (bolt-ons, premium calls, international calls)
- **Bonus data during school holidays** — An extra **20GB** is added to the shared pool during official school holiday periods (England and Wales schedule)
- **WiFi calling** — Available on all lines at no extra cost

## Who Is This Plan For?

The Family Share plan is designed for families with 2–5 members who want the convenience of a single plan, shared data, and the peace of mind that comes with parental controls and spending limits. It's particularly popular with parents who want to manage their children's mobile usage.

## Eligibility

The primary account holder must be **aged 18 or over**. Additional lines can be assigned to family members of any age. A credit check is performed on the primary account holder only.

## How to Get Started

Sign up at [togglecell.co.uk](https://togglecell.co.uk), via the ToggleCell app, or in any ToggleCell store. You can port existing numbers for all family members — each port typically completes within **1 business day**.
