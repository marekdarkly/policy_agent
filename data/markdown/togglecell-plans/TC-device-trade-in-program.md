# ToggleCell Device Trade-In Programme

## Overview

The **ToggleCell Device Trade-In Programme** lets you offset the cost of a new device — or simply earn credit on your account — by trading in your old phone, tablet, or smartwatch. ToggleCell accepts over **200 devices** and provides instant valuations through the app.

## How It Works

1. **Get a valuation** — Open the ToggleCell app or visit [togglecell.co.uk/trade-in](https://togglecell.co.uk/trade-in). Select your device model, storage size, and answer a few questions about its condition.
2. **Accept the offer** — If you're happy with the valuation, confirm the trade-in. Your quote is **valid for 14 days**.
3. **Send your device** — ToggleCell provides a **free prepaid postage label**. Pack your device securely and drop it at any Royal Mail post office or collection point.
4. **Receive your credit** — Once ToggleCell receives and inspects your device, credit is applied to your account within **3 business days**.

## Accepted Devices

ToggleCell accepts trade-ins for **200+ device models**, including:

- **Apple**: iPhone 8 and later, iPad (6th generation and later), Apple Watch Series 3 and later
- **Samsung**: Galaxy S10 and later, Galaxy A series (A51 and later), Galaxy Z Flip/Fold series, Galaxy Tab S6 and later
- **Google**: Pixel 3 and later
- **OnePlus**: OnePlus 7 and later
- **Sony**: Xperia 1 series, Xperia 5 series
- **Other**: Huawei P30+, Xiaomi 11+, and select Motorola and Oppo models

A full list of accepted devices and current valuations is available in the ToggleCell app.

## Valuation Factors

Your trade-in value depends on:

- **Device model and storage capacity** — Newer models and higher storage variants receive higher valuations
- **Condition** — Devices are graded as Excellent, Good, Fair, or Poor
  - **Excellent**: No scratches, fully functional, battery health above 80%
  - **Good**: Minor cosmetic marks, fully functional
  - **Fair**: Visible scratches or scuffs, fully functional
  - **Poor**: Cracked screen, hardware faults, or significant cosmetic damage (reduced valuation)
- **Market value** — Valuations reflect current market rates and may change over time

## How Credit Is Applied

You choose how your trade-in credit is used:

- **Bill credit** — Applied as a lump sum to your next monthly bill(s)
- **Device finance offset** — Applied to reduce the balance of a new device finance agreement
- **New device purchase** — Applied as a discount at the point of sale when buying a new device outright

## Data Wiping

ToggleCell provides a **free data wiping service** for all traded-in devices. Before sending your device, we recommend performing a factory reset yourself. Upon receipt, ToggleCell performs a certified data wipe that meets **ADISA (Asset Disposal & Information Security Alliance)** standards, ensuring all personal data is permanently removed.

## What If the Device Doesn't Match the Valuation?

If ToggleCell's inspection finds that the device condition differs from your self-assessment, you will be offered a **revised valuation**. You can:

- **Accept** the revised offer and receive the adjusted credit
- **Reject** the revised offer and have your device **returned free of charge** within 5 business days

You will be notified by email and app notification of any revised valuation.
