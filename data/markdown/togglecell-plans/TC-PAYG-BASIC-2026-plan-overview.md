# TC-PAYG-BASIC-2026 — Pay As You Go Plan Overview

## Plan Summary

The **Pay As You Go (PAYG)** plan is ToggleCell's most flexible and budget-friendly option. There is no monthly fee, no contract, and no commitment. You simply top up credit and use it for calls, texts, and data as you go. It's ideal for light users, children, second phones, or anyone who wants full control over their spending.

## Key Details

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Monthly fee** | None (£0) |
| **Contract** | None — pay as you go |
| **Calls** | 8p/min to UK landlines and mobiles |
| **Texts** | 4p per SMS |
| **Data (standard rate)** | £1/day for 500MB (activated on first data use each day) |
| **Network** | 4G only |
| **Top-up amounts** | £5, £10, £20, £30, £50 |
| **Credit expiry** | 180 days from last top-up |

## How It Works

1. **Get a SIM** — Order a free ToggleCell PAYG SIM online or pick one up in store
2. **Top up** — Add credit via the ToggleCell app, online, or at any PayPoint retailer
3. **Use your phone** — Calls, texts, and data are deducted from your credit balance
4. **Buy bundles (optional)** — Get better value by purchasing prepaid bundles for calls, texts, and data

## Standard Rates

| Service | Rate |
|---|---|
| UK calls (landline and mobile) | **8p/min** |
| UK texts (SMS) | **4p each** |
| MMS | **20p each** |
| Data (daily pass) | **£1/day for 500MB** |
| International calls | From **15p/min** |

Data is charged via a daily pass model: on any day you use mobile data, **£1 is deducted** from your credit, and you receive **500MB** of data valid until midnight. If you use more than 500MB in a day, an additional £1 is charged for another 500MB block.

## Bundles for Better Value

For regular users, prepaid **bundles** offer significantly better value than standard PAYG rates. Bundles can be purchased through the ToggleCell app and auto-renew every 30 days if desired. See the **TC-PAYG-BASIC-2026-topup-and-bundles** document for full bundle details.

## Who Is This Plan For?

The PAYG plan suits customers who use their phone infrequently, want a low-cost option for a child or secondary device, or prefer not to commit to a monthly plan. It's also a good choice for visitors to the UK who need temporary mobile service.

## eSIM and SIM

PAYG SIMs are available as **physical nano-SIM** (free, next-day delivery) or **eSIM** (instant activation via QR code in the ToggleCell app). eSIM is supported on compatible devices.

## Keeping Your Number

You can port your existing number to ToggleCell PAYG by providing a PAC code. You can also port from ToggleCell PAYG to any other ToggleCell plan at any time.
