## 4G LTE Coverage

ToggleCell's 4G LTE network covers **99% of the UK outdoor population**, providing reliable connectivity across urban, suburban, and most rural areas. Key details:

- **Outdoor coverage**: 99% of UK population
- **Indoor coverage**: 95% of UK population (estimated, varies by building construction)
- **Geographic coverage**: 90% of UK landmass
- **Typical download speeds**: 30–80 Mbps
- **Typical upload speeds**: 10–30 Mbps

## WiFi Calling

For areas where indoor mobile signal is limited, ToggleCell offers **WiFi calling** at no extra cost on all plans (except PAYG standard — available with bundles). WiFi calling allows you to make and receive calls and texts using your home or office WiFi connection.

### How to Enable WiFi Calling

1. Go to **Settings > Mobile/Cellular > WiFi Calling** on your device
2. Toggle **WiFi Calling on**
3. Confirm your registered address for emergency services

WiFi calls are charged at the same rate as standard UK calls (included in your allowance on contract plans).

## Indoor Coverage Solutions

If you experience poor indoor signal, ToggleCell offers several solutions:

- **WiFi calling** — Use your broadband connection for calls and texts (free, available on all contract plans)
- **ToggleCell Signal Booster** — A femtocell device that creates a local mobile signal using your broadband. Available **free of charge** for eligible addresses (applied for via the app)
- **Smart Signal app** — Diagnose signal issues and receive personalised recommendations for improving coverage at your location

## Network Status and Outages

Check the current status of the ToggleCell network at [togglecell.co.uk/network-status](https://togglecell.co.uk/network-status). This page shows:

- Any current network outages or maintenance
- Estimated resolution times
- Affected areas and services

You can also sign up for **network status alerts** via SMS or email to be notified of outages in your area.

## Reporting Coverage Issues

If you experience persistent coverage problems, report them through:

- The **ToggleCell app** under **Help > Report a Coverage Issue**
- Calling **0800 064 2026**
- Using **live chat** in the app or on the website

ToggleCell investigates all coverage reports and may dispatch a network engineer to assess the area. Reports contribute to coverage improvement planning.
