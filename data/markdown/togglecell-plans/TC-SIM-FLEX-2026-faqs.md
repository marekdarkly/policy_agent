# TC-SIM-FLEX-2026 — Frequently Asked Questions

## 1. Can I really cancel at any time with no penalty?

Yes. The SIM Only Flex plan is a **30-day rolling contract** with no minimum term. To cancel, simply give **30 days' notice** through the ToggleCell app, by calling 0800 064 2026, or in store. There is no early termination fee or cancellation charge.

## 2. How does data rollover work?

If you don't use all 30GB in a billing cycle, up to **10GB of unused data** automatically rolls over to the next month. Rolled-over data is used first. Any rolled-over data that remains unused at the end of the second month expires — it cannot roll over a third time.

## 3. Can I switch from this plan to a contract plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade to any ToggleCell contract plan (such as the 5G Unlimited or Family Share) at any time. Your current billing cycle will be completed, and the new plan will start from your next billing date. Any rolled-over data from the Flex plan does not carry over to contract plans.

## 4. Does this plan support eSIM?

Yes. The SIM Only Flex plan supports **eSIM activation** on compatible devices, including iPhone XS and later, Samsung Galaxy S20 and later, and Google Pixel 3 and later. You can activate your eSIM by scanning a QR code in the ToggleCell app. If you prefer a physical SIM, one will be delivered free by next-day post.

## 5. Can I keep my current phone number?

Absolutely. To bring your existing number to ToggleCell, request a **PAC code** from your current provider by texting **PAC to 65075**. Provide the code when you sign up, and your number will be ported within **1 business day**. Your old SIM will stop working once the port completes, and your new ToggleCell SIM will take over.

## 6. Can I upgrade to unlimited data without changing plan?

You can purchase data bolt-ons (5GB for £5 or 10GB for £8) for the current billing cycle. If you consistently need more than 30GB, we recommend upgrading to the **5G Unlimited plan (£45/month)**, which offers truly unlimited data. You can switch plans at any time in the ToggleCell app.

## 7. Can I use this plan abroad?

Yes. The SIM Only Flex plan includes **EU roaming** with **15GB of data** and unlimited calls and texts in 48 EU and EEA destinations. For travel outside the EU, daily roaming packs are available from £6/day. Check the ToggleCell app for destination-specific rates and roaming pack options.

## 8. Is there a credit check?

ToggleCell performs a **soft credit check** for rolling SIM-only plans, which **does not affect your credit score** or appear on your credit file. This is different from contract plans, which may require a hard credit check. Most applicants are approved instantly.
