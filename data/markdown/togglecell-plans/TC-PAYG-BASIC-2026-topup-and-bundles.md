# TC-PAYG-BASIC-2026 — Top-Up and Bundles

## Top-Up Options

ToggleCell PAYG customers can top up their credit using any of the following methods:

### Top-Up Amounts

| Amount | Available via |
|---|---|
| **£5** | App, online, in-store, PayPoint |
| **£10** | App, online, in-store, PayPoint |
| **£20** | App, online, in-store, PayPoint |
| **£30** | App, online, in-store |
| **£50** | App, online, in-store |

### Top-Up Methods

- **ToggleCell app** — Add credit instantly using a stored debit/credit card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay
- **Online** — Top up at [togglecell.co.uk/top-up](https://togglecell.co.uk/top-up) using any Visa or Mastercard
- **In-store** — Top up at any ToggleCell store or at **40,000+ PayPoint retailers** across the UK (look for the PayPoint sign in newsagents, convenience stores, and petrol stations)
- **Top-up voucher** — Purchase a ToggleCell top-up voucher in store and enter the code in the app

### Auto Top-Up

Set up **auto top-up** to automatically add credit when your balance falls below a chosen threshold:

- **Trigger amount**: Choose from £2, £5, or £10
- **Top-up amount**: Choose from £10, £20, or £30
- **Payment**: Charged to your stored debit/credit card
- **Manage**: Enable, disable, or adjust in the ToggleCell app under **Account > Auto Top-Up**

Auto top-up ensures you never run out of credit unexpectedly.

## Credit Expiry

Your PAYG credit **expires 180 days after your last top-up**. To keep your credit and number active, simply top up at least once every 180 days, even with the minimum amount of £5.

If your credit expires:
- Your **balance is forfeited**
- Your **number is retained for a further 90 days** (total 270 days from last top-up)
- After 270 days without a top-up, your number may be recycled

ToggleCell will send SMS reminders at 30 days and 7 days before credit expiry.

## Prepaid Bundles

Bundles offer much better value than standard PAYG rates. They can be purchased via the ToggleCell app and are valid for **30 days** from activation.

### Available Bundles

| Bundle | Data | UK minutes | UK texts | Price | Validity |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Essentials** | 2GB | 200 mins | Unlimited | **£10** | 30 days |
| **Value** | 5GB | 500 mins | Unlimited | **£15** | 30 days |
| **Data Only S** | 3GB | — | — | **£7** | 30 days |
| **Data Only L** | 10GB | — | — | **£12** | 30 days |

### Bundle Details

- Bundles are deducted from your **PAYG credit balance** when purchased
- If you run out of bundle allowance, you revert to **standard PAYG rates** for the remainder of the 30-day period
- Bundles **do not roll over** — unused allowance expires at the end of the 30-day validity period
- You can purchase a new bundle before your current one expires; the remaining allowance from the old bundle is forfeited

### Auto-Renew

You can enable **auto-renew** for any bundle. When enabled, the same bundle is automatically purchased every 30 days, provided you have sufficient credit. Auto-renew can be turned on or off at any time in the ToggleCell app under **Account > Bundles > Auto-Renew**.

## Checking Your Balance

You can check your current PAYG credit balance at any time:

- **Dial** ***#100#** from your ToggleCell phone (free)
- **ToggleCell app** — Balance shown on the home screen
- **Online** — Log in at [togglecell.co.uk/my-account](https://togglecell.co.uk/my-account)
