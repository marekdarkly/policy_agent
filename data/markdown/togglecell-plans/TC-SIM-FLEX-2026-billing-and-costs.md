# TC-SIM-FLEX-2026 — Billing and Costs

## Monthly Charges

The **SIM Only Flex** plan costs **£20.00 per month**, billed in advance on a 30-day rolling basis. There is **no activation fee** and **no early termination fee** — you can cancel at any time with 30 days' notice.

## Payment Methods

ToggleCell accepts the following payment methods:

- **Direct Debit** — Recommended for hassle-free automatic payments
- **Debit or credit card** — Visa, Mastercard, and American Express
- **In-app payment** — Pay via the ToggleCell app using stored card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay

## Bills and Statements

Bills are delivered digitally by default:

- **Email** — PDF bill sent to your registered email address each billing cycle
- **ToggleCell app** — View, download, and manage all current and past bills
- **Paper bills** — Available on request for £1.50/month

## International Calls

International calls are not included in the plan allowance and are charged per minute:

- **Standard international rate**: From **5p/min** depending on destination
- Rate cards are available in the ToggleCell app under **Settings > Call Rates**
- Premium-rate numbers (09xx, 118x) are **blocked by default** to prevent unexpected charges

## Data Bolt-Ons

If you need more data during a billing cycle, you can purchase bolt-ons instantly via the ToggleCell app:

| Bolt-on | Price | Validity |
|---|---|---|
| **5GB extra data** | £5.00 | Current billing cycle |
| **10GB extra data** | £8.00 | Current billing cycle |

Bolt-on data is used after your standard 30GB allowance (and any rolled-over data) is consumed. Unused bolt-on data does not roll over.

## Data Overage Charges

If you exceed your data allowance and have not purchased a bolt-on, out-of-bundle data is charged at **£1.00 per GB**. You can enable an **optional data cap** in the ToggleCell app to prevent any overage charges — once your allowance is used, data access will be paused until the next billing cycle.

## Data Usage Alerts

ToggleCell will send you automatic usage alerts by SMS when you reach:

- **80%** of your monthly data allowance
- **100%** of your monthly data allowance

These alerts help you manage your usage and decide whether to add a bolt-on or enable a data cap.
