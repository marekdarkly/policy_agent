# TC-FAM-SHARE-2026 — Parental Controls

## Overview

The **Family Share** plan includes comprehensive parental controls at no extra cost, designed to give parents peace of mind while allowing children safe access to mobile services. All controls are managed through the **ToggleCell Family app**, available on iOS and Android.

## Content Filtering

ToggleCell provides **three levels of content filtering** that can be applied per line:

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| **Strict** | Blocks adult content, social media, gambling, dating, and violence. Suitable for children under 10. |
| **Moderate** | Blocks adult content, gambling, and dating. Allows social media and most websites. Suitable for children aged 10–15. |
| **Light** | Blocks adult content and gambling only. Suitable for teenagers aged 16–17. |

Content filtering is **enabled by default on Strict** for any line assigned to a user under 18. The account holder can change the filter level at any time via the ToggleCell Family app.

## Web Filtering

In addition to content categories, the account holder can:

- **Block specific websites** by URL
- **Allow specific websites** that may be blocked by the chosen filter level (whitelist)
- View a **browsing activity summary** (domains visited, not full URLs) for child lines

Web filtering operates at the network level and works across all apps and browsers, not just the device's default browser.

## App Time Limits

Set **daily time limits** for mobile data usage or specific app categories on each child line:

- **Social media**: e.g., 1 hour/day
- **Gaming**: e.g., 30 minutes/day
- **Video streaming**: e.g., 2 hours/day
- **Overall screen time**: e.g., 4 hours/day

When a limit is reached, data access for that category is paused. Calls and texts remain available. Limits reset at midnight each day.

## Bedtime Mode

**Bedtime mode** disables mobile data access during specified hours, typically **9:00 PM to 7:00 AM**. While bedtime mode is active:

- Mobile data is disabled
- Calls and texts **remain available** for safety
- WiFi is not affected (this is a mobile network control only)
- Emergency calls (999, 112) are always available

The account holder sets bedtime hours per line and can adjust them for weekends and holidays.

## Spend Caps

Prevent unexpected charges by setting **per-line spending caps**:

- Range: **£0 to £50/month**
- Covers: Data bolt-ons, international calls, premium services, directory enquiries
- When the cap is reached, out-of-plan spending is blocked for that billing cycle
- The account holder receives a notification when a line reaches 80% of its spend cap

## Call and Text Blocking

The account holder can block specific phone numbers on child lines:

- Block **incoming and/or outgoing** calls to/from specified numbers
- Block **all premium-rate numbers** (enabled by default)
- Block **international calls** (enabled by default on child lines)
- Block list is managed via the ToggleCell Family app

## Location Sharing (Family Locator)

The optional **Family Locator** feature allows family members to share their real-time location:

- Visible on a map within the ToggleCell Family app
- Requires **explicit consent** from each family member aged 16 or over
- For children under 16, the account holder can enable location sharing
- Location history is stored for **7 days** and then automatically deleted
- Location sharing can be paused by any member at any time

## Managing Controls

All parental controls are managed through the **ToggleCell Family app**:

1. Download the app from the App Store or Google Play
2. Sign in with your ToggleCell account (primary account holder)
3. Navigate to **Family > Controls**
4. Select the line you want to manage
5. Configure filters, limits, and settings

Changes take effect within **5 minutes**. The account holder receives a weekly summary email of activity and alerts for each child line.
