# TC-5G-UNLIM-2026 — Frequently Asked Questions

## 1. Is the data really unlimited? Are there any speed restrictions?

Yes, the 5G Unlimited plan provides **genuinely unlimited data** with no monthly cap. There are no speed restrictions, throttling, or deprioritisation under normal usage. You can stream, download, and browse without worrying about hitting a limit. The 100GB mobile hotspot allowance is the only data feature with a cap.

## 2. How do I know if I can get 5G in my area?

ToggleCell's 5G network is currently live in **120+ UK cities and major towns**. You can check whether your home, workplace, or other locations have 5G coverage using the coverage checker at [togglecell.co.uk/coverage](https://togglecell.co.uk/coverage). If 5G is not yet available in your area, your device will automatically connect to our 4G network, which covers 99% of the UK outdoor population.

## 3. Can I switch to this plan from another ToggleCell plan?

Yes. Existing ToggleCell customers can upgrade or switch to the 5G Unlimited plan at any time. If you are currently in a contract, you may need to pay an early termination fee on your existing plan. You can check your current contract status and any applicable fees in the ToggleCell app under **Account > Contract Details**.

## 4. What happens if I want to cancel before my 24-month contract ends?

If you cancel before the end of your 24-month minimum term, an **early termination fee** applies. This is calculated as the number of remaining months multiplied by £15. For example, cancelling with 6 months remaining would cost £90. Any outstanding device finance balance must also be paid in full.

## 5. Can I split my bill between plan and device costs?

Yes. Your monthly bill shows the **plan charge (£45) and device finance payment as separate line items**. This makes it clear how much you are paying for your airtime versus your device. If you are on a SIM-only arrangement, only the plan charge appears.

## 6. What is the hotspot limit, and what happens if I exceed it?

Your plan includes **100GB of mobile hotspot (tethering) data** per month. If you reach this limit, hotspot functionality is paused until your next billing cycle. Your standard mobile data (unlimited) continues to work normally on your phone. You can purchase an additional 20GB hotspot bolt-on for £3/month if you need more.

## 7. Does this plan support eSIM?

Yes. The 5G Unlimited plan fully supports **eSIM activation** on compatible devices, including iPhone XS and later, Samsung Galaxy S20 and later, and Google Pixel 3 and later. You can activate your eSIM instantly by scanning a QR code in the ToggleCell app. You can also use a physical nano-SIM if you prefer.

## 8. How do I set up WiFi calling?

WiFi calling is **enabled by default** on the 5G Unlimited plan. To ensure it works on your device:

1. Go to your phone's **Settings > Mobile/Cellular > WiFi Calling**
2. Toggle **WiFi Calling on**
3. Confirm your registered address for emergency services purposes

Once enabled, your phone will automatically use WiFi for calls when your mobile signal is weak. WiFi calls are included in your unlimited call allowance and are charged at the same rate as standard UK calls.

## 9. How long does it take to port my number from another network?

Number porting typically completes within **1 business day**. To port your number, you will need a **PAC code (Porting Authorisation Code)** from your current network. You can request this by texting **PAC** to **65075** from your current phone. Once you provide the PAC code to ToggleCell, we will arrange the transfer. You will receive an SMS confirming when the port is complete.

## 10. How do I cancel my plan?

To cancel your 5G Unlimited plan, you can:

- Call **0800 064 2026** and speak to our retentions team
- Use the **live chat** feature in the ToggleCell app
- Visit a **ToggleCell store** in person

You must give **30 days' notice** to cancel. If you are still within your 24-month minimum term, an early termination fee will apply (see question 4). Any outstanding device finance must be settled before cancellation is finalised. Once cancelled, your number can be transferred to another network using a PAC code.
