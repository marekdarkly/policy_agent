# TC-5G-UNLIM-2026 — Roaming

## EU Roaming — Included

The **5G Unlimited** plan includes roaming across **48 EU and EEA destinations** at no extra cost. You can use your phone abroad just like you do at home, subject to fair usage limits.

### What's Included in EU Roaming

| Feature | Allowance |
|---|---|
| **Data** | 50GB per month |
| **Calls** | Unlimited — make and receive calls across EU destinations |
| **Texts** | Unlimited — send and receive texts across EU destinations |
| **Receiving calls** | Free |

Roaming is **activated by default** on your account. You do not need to enable it before travelling.

### Fair Usage Policy

A **fair usage policy (FUP)** of **50GB per month** applies to data usage while roaming in the EU. If you exceed 50GB in a calendar month while abroad, a surcharge of **£3.00 per GB** will apply for additional data. You will receive an SMS notification when you reach 80% and 100% of your roaming data allowance.

### Covered EU Destinations

Your roaming allowance is valid in all 27 EU member states plus the following EEA and included territories: Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Switzerland, Monaco, Vatican City, San Marino, Andorra, Gibraltar, Guernsey, Jersey, Isle of Man, and others. A full list is available in the ToggleCell app under **Roaming > Destinations**.

## Rest of World Roaming

For travel outside the EU, ToggleCell offers **daily roaming packs**:

- **Roam Daily Pass**: **£6.00/day** — Includes 500MB data, 60 minutes of calls, and unlimited texts in 40+ destinations (USA, Canada, Australia, UAE, Japan, and more)
- **Roam Plus Pass**: **£10.00/day** — Includes 1GB data, 120 minutes of calls, and unlimited texts in 80+ destinations

Daily passes are activated automatically when you use your phone abroad. You can opt out of automatic activation in the ToggleCell app.

### Cruise Ship and In-Flight Rates

Roaming on **cruise ships** and **in-flight WiFi** services is not included in any roaming allowance. Charges on maritime and aeronautical networks are billed at the following rates:

- **Calls**: £3.00/min (making and receiving)
- **Texts**: £0.50 each
- **Data**: £7.50/MB

ToggleCell strongly recommends disabling mobile data and enabling flight mode while on board to avoid unexpected charges. You can set a roaming spend cap in the ToggleCell app to protect against bill shock.

## Roaming Spend Cap

All ToggleCell customers have a default **roaming spend cap of £45/month** for out-of-bundle charges. You can adjust this cap (up to £200/month) or remove it entirely via the app or by calling support.
