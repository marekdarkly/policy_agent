## ToggleFit Rewards (All Plans)

**Available to:** All ToggleHealth members

### Program Benefits

- **Up to $500 Annual Rewards** - Earn points for healthy activities
- **Gym Membership Discounts** - Discounted rates at participating gyms
- **Fitness Tracker Discounts** - Save on fitness wearables
- **Healthy Food Delivery Discounts** - Savings on meal kit services

### How to Earn Rewards

- Complete annual physical: 100 points
- Complete biometric screening: 100 points
- Log exercise (3x/week): 10 points per week
- Complete health assessment: 50 points

**Points Value:** 100 points = $25 reward
