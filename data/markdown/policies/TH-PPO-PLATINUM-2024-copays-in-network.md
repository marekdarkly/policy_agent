## Copays (In-Network)

### Medical Visit Copays - Platinum PPO

| Service Type | Copay Amount |
|-------------|--------------|
| **Primary Care Visit** | $20 |
| **Specialist Visit** | $35 |
| **Urgent Care Visit** | $50 |
| **Emergency Room Visit** | $250 |

**Note:** Copays apply even before the deductible is met. After copay, plan pays 90% of additional costs after deductible.
