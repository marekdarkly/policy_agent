## Pharmacy Networks

### Retail Pharmacy Network

**Network Name:** ToggleHealth Pharmacy Network  
**Number of Pharmacies:** 1,876 locations  
**Coverage Area:** Washington, Oregon, California, Idaho, Montana

**Included Pharmacies:**
- ToggleHealth Pharmacy locations
- Major national chains (participating locations)
- Independent pharmacies (participating locations)

### Mail Order Pharmacy

**Pharmacy Name:** ToggleHealth Mail Order  
**Phone:** 1-800-TOGGLE-4  
**Fax:** 206-555-6099  
**Online Ordering:** my.togglehealth.com or ToggleHealth Mobile app

**Benefits:**
- 90-day supply of medications
- Free standard shipping (7-10 business days)
- Automatic refills available
- 24/7 pharmacist support
- Lower cost than retail (pay 2x monthly copay for 3-month supply)

### Specialty Pharmacy

**Pharmacy Name:** ToggleHealth Specialty Pharmacy  
**Phone:** 1-800-TOGGLE-4  
**Required for:** All Tier 4 (specialty) medications

**Services:**
- Specialty medication management
- Prior authorization assistance
- Coordination with physicians
- Patient education and training
- Side effect monitoring
- Compliance support
