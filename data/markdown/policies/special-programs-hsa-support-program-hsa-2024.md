## HSA Support Program (HSA-2024)

**Program ID:** HSA-2024
**Program Name:** HSA Support Program

### Eligibility

**Exclusive to Bronze HDHP Members** - Available to all members enrolled in TH-HDHP-BRONZE-2024

### Program Benefits

- **Free HSA Account Setup** - No account opening fees or annual fees
- **HSA Contribution Matching:**
  - Individual coverage: Up to $750 per year
  - Family coverage: Up to $1,500 per year
- **Financial Planning Consultations** - Sessions with financial advisors on HSA strategies
- **Online HSA Management Tools** - Easy-to-use portal for managing HSA
- **Educational Resources** - Guides on maximizing HSA benefits
- **Tax Planning Assistance** - Help understanding HSA tax advantages
- **Investment Options** - Access to HSA investment accounts after minimum balance

### HSA Contribution Schedule

ToggleHealth employer contributions are deposited quarterly:
- **Individual:** $187.50 per quarter
- **Family:** $375 per quarter

### How to Enroll

1. **Automatic Enrollment** - All Bronze HDHP members automatically eligible
2. **Set Up HSA Account** - Call 1-800-TOGGLE-1 to set up your HSA
3. **Start Contributing** - Begin making pre-tax contributions from your paycheck

### Program Features

- **HSA Debit Card** - Use for qualified medical expenses
- **Online Account Access** - View balance, transactions, and invest funds
- **Mobile App** - Manage HSA on the go
- **Receipts Management** - Store and track medical expense receipts
- **Tax Forms** - Automatic generation of HSA tax forms (Form 5498-SA, Form 1099-SA)
