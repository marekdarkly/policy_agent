## Covered Services Details

### Preventive Care - Gold HMO (TH-HMO-GOLD-2024)

**Coverage:** 100% covered (no cost sharing)
**Deductible:** Does not apply
**Annual Limit:** None
**Notes:** Must use in-network providers

**Includes:** Annual physicals, immunizations, screening tests, wellness visits

### Primary Care - Gold HMO (TH-HMO-GOLD-2024)

**Coverage:** 80% after deductible
**Copay:** $25 per visit
**Deductible:** Yes
**Referral Required:** No
**Annual Limit:** None

You must select a PCP from the TH-HMO-PRIMARY network. All primary care visits go through your designated PCP.
