## Covered Services

### Preventive Care - Bronze HDHP (TH-HDHP-BRONZE-2024)

**Coverage:** 100% covered
**Deductible:** Does NOT apply (covered before deductible)
**Annual Limit:** None

**This is the ONLY service not subject to the deductible.**

**Preventive Care Includes:**
- Annual physicals
- Immunizations
- Screening tests
- Wellness visits

### All Other Services - Bronze HDHP

**Coverage:** 100% after deductible
**Deductible:** YES - all services subject to $6,500 deductible
**Copay:** NONE

**Services Include:**
- Primary care visits
- Specialist visits
- Hospitalization (preauthorization required)
- Emergency services
- Mental health outpatient
- Mental health inpatient (preauthorization required)
- Substance abuse treatment (preauthorization required)
- Maternity care
- Diagnostic tests (preauthorization required for advanced imaging)
- Physical therapy (30 visits/year limit)
- Chiropractic care (12 visits/year limit)
- Durable medical equipment (preauthorization required)
- Home health care (40 visits/year limit, preauthorization required)
- Hospice care (100% covered, no deductible)

**Important:** Except for preventive care and hospice, ALL services are subject to the full deductible. After you've paid $6,500 (individual) or $13,000 (family), the plan pays 100% for the rest of the year.
