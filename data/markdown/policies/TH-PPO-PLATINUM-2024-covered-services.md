## Covered Services

### Preventive Care - Platinum PPO (TH-PPO-PLATINUM-2024)

**Coverage:** 100% covered (no cost sharing)
**Deductible Applies:** No
**Annual Limit:** None
**In-Network and Out-of-Network:** Covered at 100% for both

**Preventive Care Services Include:**
- Annual physical examinations
- Routine immunizations and vaccinations
- Screening tests (mammograms, colonoscopies, etc.)
- Well-child visits
- Wellness visits
- Preventive counseling

**Important:** Preventive care is the only service covered at 100% regardless of network status. All other out-of-network services are covered at 60%.

### Primary Care - Platinum PPO (TH-PPO-PLATINUM-2024)

**Coverage:** 90% after deductible
**Copay:** $20 per visit
**Deductible Applies:** Yes
**Annual Limit:** None

- Office visits and consultations
- Routine medical care
- Chronic disease management
- Acute illness treatment

**Important:** Out-of-network emergency services are covered at the same rates as in-network services. The $250 copay applies to all ER visits. Copay is waived if admitted to the hospital.
