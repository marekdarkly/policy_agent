## Annual Cost Sharing

### In-Network Cost Sharing (TH-PPO-PLATINUM-2024)

| Cost Component | Individual | Family |
|----------------|-----------|---------|
| **Annual Deductible** | $500 | $1,000 |
| **Out-of-Pocket Maximum** | $4,500 | $9,000 |
| **Coinsurance Rate** | 10% (Plan pays 90%) | 10% (Plan pays 90%) |

### Out-of-Network Cost Sharing (TH-PPO-PLATINUM-2024)

| Cost Component | Individual | Family |
|----------------|-----------|---------|
| **Annual Deductible** | $1,500 | $3,000 |
| **Out-of-Pocket Maximum** | $9,000 | $18,000 |
| **Coinsurance Rate** | 40% (Plan pays 60%) | 40% (Plan pays 60%) |

**Important:** Out-of-network deductibles and out-of-pocket maximums are separate from in-network amounts. Members may see out-of-network providers but will pay higher costs.
