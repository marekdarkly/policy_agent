## Pharmacy Benefits

### Prescription Drug Coverage - Platinum PPO

The Platinum PPO plan includes comprehensive prescription drug coverage through ToggleHealth PharmaCare.

**Pharmacy Network:** ToggleHealth Pharmacy Network
**Mail Order Pharmacy:** ToggleHealth Mail Order
**Specialty Pharmacy:** ToggleHealth Specialty Pharmacy

### Formulary Tiers and Copays

| Tier | Description | Retail Copay (30-day) | Mail Order Copay (90-day) |
|------|------------|----------------------|--------------------------|
| **Tier 1** | Generic medications | $5 | $10 |
| **Tier 2** | Preferred brand-name | $25 | $50 |
| **Tier 3** | Non-preferred brand-name | $50 | $100 |
| **Tier 4** | Specialty medications | $100 | Not available |

### Example Medications by Tier

**Tier 1 (Generic) - $5 copay:**
- Lisinopril (blood pressure)
- Metformin (diabetes)
- Atorvastatin (cholesterol)
- Levothyroxine (thyroid)
- Sertraline (depression)

**Tier 2 (Preferred Brand) - $25 copay:**
- Januvia (diabetes)
- Synthroid (thyroid)
- Advair Diskus (asthma)
- Eliquis (blood thinner)

**Tier 3 (Non-Preferred Brand) - $50 copay:**
- Nexium (heartburn)
- Celebrex (arthritis)
- Crestor (cholesterol)

**Tier 4 (Specialty) - $100 copay:**
- Humira (autoimmune)
- Enbrel (rheumatoid arthritis)
- Copaxone (multiple sclerosis)

### Mail Order Pharmacy Benefits

- **90-day supply for chronic medications**
- **Copay = 2x retail copay (save one month's copay)**
- **Free standard shipping**
- **Automatic refills available**
- **24/7 pharmacist support**

**How to Order:** Call 1-800-TOGGLE-4 or order online at my.togglehealth.com

### Specialty Pharmacy

Specialty medications (Tier 4) must be obtained through ToggleHealth Specialty Pharmacy.

**Requirements:**
- Prior authorization required for all specialty medications
- Must use ToggleHealth Specialty Pharmacy (no retail option)
- 30-day supply maximum
- Coordination with physician required

**To Fill Specialty Prescriptions:** Call 1-800-TOGGLE-4
