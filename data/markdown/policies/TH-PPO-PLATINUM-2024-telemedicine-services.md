### ToggleHealth Virtual Care - Platinum PPO

**Cost:** $0 copay for Platinum PPO members
**Availability:** 24/7, 365 days per year
**Platform:** ToggleHealth Mobile app or my.togglehealth.com

**Telemedicine Services Available:**
- General medical consultations
- Mental health counseling

- Nutritionist consultations

**How It Works:** Download the ToggleHealth Mobile app or log in to my.togglehealth.com. Request a virtual visit and connect with a licensed provider within minutes.

**Important:** Platinum PPO members get telemedicine at NO COST. Other plans may have copays.
