## Maternity Support Program (MSP-2024)

**Program ID:** MSP-2024
**Program Name:** Maternity Support Program

### Eligibility

Available to pregnant members from pregnancy confirmation through 3 months postpartum.

**Available on Plans:** All ToggleHealth plans

### Program Benefits by Plan

#### Platinum PPO

- **24/7 Nurse Hotline** - Experienced obstetric nurses available anytime
- **Prenatal Vitamins Covered 100%** - No cost for prescription prenatal vitamins
- **Breast Pump Provided at No Cost** - Hospital-grade or personal breast pump
- **Postpartum Depression Screening and Support** - Comprehensive mental health screening
- **Doula Services:** Up to $1,000 coverage for certified doula services

#### Gold HMO

- **24/7 Nurse Hotline** - Experienced obstetric nurses available anytime
- **Prenatal Vitamins Covered 100%** - No cost for prescription prenatal vitamins
- **Breast Pump Provided at No Cost** - Hospital-grade or personal breast pump
- **Postpartum Depression Screening and Support** - Comprehensive mental health screening

#### Silver EPO

- **24/7 Nurse Hotline** - Experienced obstetric nurses available anytime
- **Prenatal Vitamins Covered 80%** - Subject to regular prescription coverage
- **Breast Pump Rental Program** - Rental at discounted rate
- **Postpartum Depression Screening** - Basic screening provided

#### Bronze HDHP

- **24/7 Nurse Hotline** - Experienced obstetric nurses available anytime
- **Prenatal Vitamins:** Subject to deductible
- **Breast Pump:** Subject to deductible
- **Postpartum Depression Screening** - Subject to deductible

### How to Enroll

**Automatic Enrollment** - When you report your pregnancy to ToggleHealth, you are automatically enrolled in the Maternity Support Program.

**To Report Pregnancy:**
1. Call Member Services: 1-800-TOGGLE-1
2. Notify online at my.togglehealth.com
3. Have your OB/GYN submit notification

### Program Features

- **Personalized Maternity Care Plan**
- **Prenatal Education Materials** - Books, videos, online resources
- **High-Risk Pregnancy Management** - Additional support for complications
- **Lactation Consulting** - Breastfeeding support from certified lactation consultants
- **Newborn Care Education** - Classes and resources for caring for baby
- **Postpartum Check-Ins** - Follow-up at 1 week, 1 month, and 3 months postpartum
- **Mental Health Support** - Screening and treatment for postpartum depression/anxiety
- **Return-to-Work Support** - Resources for working mothers
