## Specialty Pharmacy Services

### What are Specialty Medications?

Specialty medications are high-cost drugs used to treat complex, chronic conditions. They often require:
- Special handling (refrigeration)
- Patient training for administration
- Close monitoring for side effects
- Prior authorization

### Specialty Pharmacy Requirements

**All Tier 4 medications MUST be obtained through ToggleHealth Specialty Pharmacy.**

- Phone: 1-800-TOGGLE-4
- Available: Monday-Friday 8 AM - 8 PM Pacific
- After-hours support: Available for urgent issues

### Specialty Pharmacy Services Include

- **Prior Authorization Assistance** - Help obtaining required approvals
- **Financial Assistance** - Help finding manufacturer copay assistance programs
- **Patient Education** - Training on how to use/administer medications
- **Injection Training** - For self-injectable medications
- **Side Effect Monitoring** - Regular check-ins on how you're doing
- **Adherence Support** - Reminders and support to take medications correctly
- **Coordination with Physician** - Regular updates to your doctor
- **Home Delivery** - Medications shipped to you with appropriate packaging

### Common Specialty Medication Conditions

- Rheumatoid arthritis and autoimmune diseases
- Multiple sclerosis
- Cancer
- Hepatitis C
- HIV/AIDS
- Cystic fibrosis
- Growth hormone deficiency
- Hemophilia
- Pulmonary arterial hypertension
