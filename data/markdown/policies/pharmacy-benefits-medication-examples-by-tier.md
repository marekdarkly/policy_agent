## Medication Examples by Tier

### Tier 1 - Generic Medications

**Blood Pressure:**
- Lisinopril (ACE inhibitor)
- Amlodipine (calcium channel blocker)
- Hydrochlorothiazide (diuretic)
- Metoprolol (beta blocker)

**Diabetes:**
- Metformin (diabetes)
- Glipizide (diabetes)
- Insulin (certain generics)

**Cholesterol:**
- Atorvastatin (statin)
- Simvastatin (statin)

**Mental Health:**
- Sertraline (antidepressant)
- Fluoxetine (antidepressant)
- Escitalopram (antidepressant)
- Bupropion (antidepressant)

**Other Common Generics:**
- Levothyroxine (thyroid)
- Omeprazole (acid reflux)
- Gabapentin (nerve pain)
- Albuterol (asthma inhaler)

### Tier 2 - Preferred Brand-Name

**Diabetes:**
- Januvia (diabetes)
- Jardiance (diabetes)
- Ozempic (diabetes)

**Thyroid:**
- Synthroid (thyroid)

**Respiratory:**
- Advair Diskus (asthma/COPD)
- Symbicort (asthma/COPD)
- Spiriva (COPD)

**Heart:**
- Eliquis (blood thinner)

**Pain:**
- Lyrica (nerve pain)

### Tier 3 - Non-Preferred Brand-Name

**Acid Reflux:**
- Nexium (heartburn)
- Dexilant (heartburn)

**Pain/Inflammation:**
- Celebrex (arthritis)

**Cholesterol:**
- Crestor (statin - generic available)

**Other:**
- Viagra (erectile dysfunction)
- Cialis (erectile dysfunction)

### Tier 4 - Specialty Medications

**Autoimmune/Rheumatoid Arthritis:**
- Humira (rheumatoid arthritis, Crohn's)
- Enbrel (rheumatoid arthritis)
- Stelara (psoriasis, Crohn's)
- Cosentyx (psoriasis)

**Multiple Sclerosis:**
- Copaxone (multiple sclerosis)
- Tecfidera (multiple sclerosis)
- Gilenya (multiple sclerosis)

**Cancer:**
- Gleevec (leukemia)
- Revlimid (multiple myeloma)
- Ibrance (breast cancer)

**Hepatitis C:**
- Harvoni (hepatitis C)
- Mavyret (hepatitis C)

**Pulmonary:**
- Trikafta (cystic fibrosis)

**Note:** All specialty medications require prior authorization and must be obtained through ToggleHealth Specialty Pharmacy.
