## Mail Order Pharmacy Benefits

### Advantages of Mail Order

✅ **Cost Savings** - Pay 2x retail copay for 3-month supply (save one month)  
✅ **Convenience** - Delivered to your home  
✅ **Automatic Refills** - Never run out of maintenance medications  
✅ **Free Shipping** - Standard shipping included  
✅ **24/7 Pharmacist Support** - Call anytime with questions

### Best for Mail Order

- Maintenance medications for chronic conditions
- Medications you take regularly
- Stable dosages (not frequently changing)

**Examples:**
- Blood pressure medications
- Cholesterol medications
- Diabetes medications
- Thyroid medications
- Antidepressants
