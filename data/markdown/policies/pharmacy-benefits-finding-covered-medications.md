## Finding Covered Medications

### Online Formulary Search

**Website:** my.togglehealth.com/formulary

**Search by:**
- Medication name
- Condition being treated
- Therapeutic class

**Tool Shows:**
- Which tier the medication is in
- Cost at your plan level
- Whether prior authorization is needed
- Generic alternatives available

### Formulary Updates

The ToggleHealth formulary is updated quarterly (January, April, July, October).

**Members are notified if:**
- Your current medication is removed from formulary
- Your medication moves to a different tier
- New prior authorization requirements added

**You will receive:**
- 60 days advance notice
- Information about alternative medications
- Time to work with your doctor on changes
