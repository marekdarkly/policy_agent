## Special Programs

### Diabetes Management Program (DMP-2024)

**Eligibility:** Silver EPO members with diabetes

**Benefits:**
- Quarterly care coordinator check-ins
- Discounted glucose monitor and test strips
- 100% coverage for diabetes education classes
- 4 nutritionist consultations per year at no cost

### Maternity Support Program (MSP-2024)

**Eligibility:** Pregnant Silver EPO members

**Benefits:**
- 24/7 nurse hotline
- Prenatal vitamins covered 80%
- Breast pump rental program
- Postpartum depression screening
