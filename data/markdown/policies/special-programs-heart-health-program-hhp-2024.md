## Heart Health Program (HHP-2024)

**Program ID:** HHP-2024
**Program Name:** Heart Health Program

### Eligibility

Available to members with diagnosed cardiovascular disease including:
- Coronary artery disease
- Heart failure / congestive heart failure
- Previous heart attack (myocardial infarction)
- Previous cardiac procedures (stent, bypass surgery, etc.)
- Arrhythmias requiring management
- Cardiomyopathy

**Available on Plans:** Gold HMO and Platinum PPO only

**Not available on:** Silver EPO or Bronze HDHP

### Program Benefits by Plan

#### Platinum PPO Members

- **Monthly Nurse Hotline Access** - Dedicated cardiac nurse available for questions
- **Cardiac Rehabilitation Coverage:** Up to 48 sessions (12 more than standard coverage)
- **Free Blood Pressure Monitor AND Fitness Tracker** - Provided at no cost
- **Waived Copays for Cardiac Specialist Visits** - $0 copay for cardiologist visits

#### Gold HMO Members

- **Monthly Nurse Hotline Access** - Dedicated cardiac nurse available for questions
- **Cardiac Rehabilitation Coverage:** Up to 36 sessions
- **Free Blood Pressure Monitor** - Provided at no cost
- **Waived Copays for Cardiac Specialist Visits** - $0 copay for cardiologist visits (normally $45)

### How to Enroll

1. **Ask Your Cardiologist** - Request referral to Heart Health Program
2. **Call Member Services:** 1-800-TOGGLE-1
3. **Automatic Referral** - Some members automatically referred after cardiac events

### Program Features

- **Personalized Care Plan** - Individualized cardiac care management plan
- **Medication Adherence Support** - Help managing cardiac medications
- **Lifestyle Coaching** - Diet, exercise, and stress management
- **Blood Pressure Monitoring** - Home monitoring support and tracking
- **Weight Management** - For members needing to lose weight for heart health
- **Tobacco Cessation** - Specialized support for quitting smoking
- **Depression Screening** - Mental health screening (common after cardiac events)
- **Hospital-to-Home Transition** - Extra support after hospitalization
