## Member Resources

### Online Portal
- **URL:** my.togglehealth.com
- **Features:** View claims, find providers, check coverage, request ID cards

### Mobile App
- **Name:** ToggleHealth Mobile
- **Available:** iOS and Android
- **Features:** Telemedicine, provider search, digital ID card, claims status

### Nurse Hotline
- **Phone:** 1-800-TOGGLE-3
- **Available:** 24/7, 365 days per year
- **Services:** Health questions, symptom assessment, care guidance

### Care Navigation
- **Available:** For Gold and Platinum members
- **Services:** Help finding specialists, coordinating care, understanding benefits
