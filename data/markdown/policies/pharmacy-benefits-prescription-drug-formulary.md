## Prescription Drug Formulary

ToggleHealth maintains a four-tier prescription drug formulary. Medications are placed in tiers based on cost-effectiveness and clinical evidence.

### Four-Tier Formulary Structure

| Tier | Description | Member Cost |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Tier 1** | Generic medications | Lowest |
| **Tier 2** | Preferred brand-name medications | Low |
| **Tier 3** | Non-preferred brand-name medications | Moderate |
| **Tier 4** | Specialty medications | Highest |
