## Special Programs Available

### Diabetes Management Program (DMP-2024)

**Eligibility:** Gold HMO members with Type 1 or Type 2 diabetes

**Benefits:**
- Quarterly care coordinator check-ins
- Free glucose monitor and test strips
- 100% coverage for diabetes education classes
- 6 nutritionist consultations per year at no cost

**Enroll:** Call 1-800-TOGGLE-1

### Heart Health Program (HHP-2024)

**Eligibility:** Gold HMO members with cardiovascular disease

**Benefits:**
- Monthly nurse hotline access
- Cardiac rehabilitation coverage up to 36 sessions
- Free blood pressure monitor
- Waived copays for cardiac specialist visits

**Enroll:** Ask your PCP or call 1-800-TOGGLE-1

### Maternity Support Program (MSP-2024)

**Eligibility:** Pregnant Gold HMO members

**Benefits:**
- 24/7 nurse hotline
- Prenatal vitamins covered 100%
- Breast pump provided at no cost
- Postpartum depression screening and support

**Enroll:** Automatic when pregnancy is reported
