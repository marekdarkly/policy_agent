## Plan Overview

The ToggleHealth Platinum PPO is our premium health insurance plan offering maximum flexibility and the lowest out-of-pocket costs. This plan is ideal for members who want the freedom to see any specialist without a referral and access to the largest provider network.

### Key Features

- **No Primary Care Physician (PCP) Required** - See any in-network provider without designation
- **No Referrals Needed** - Direct access to specialists
- **Out-of-Network Coverage** - 60% coverage for out-of-network providers
- **Largest Provider Network** - 14,789 specialists across five states
- **Premium Concierge Service Included** - Dedicated care navigation
- **Acupuncture Covered** - Up to 20 visits per year
