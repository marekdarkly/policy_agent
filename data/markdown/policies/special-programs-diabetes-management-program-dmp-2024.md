## Diabetes Management Program (DMP-2024)

**Program ID:** DMP-2024
**Program Name:** Diabetes Management Program

### Eligibility

Available to ToggleHealth members diagnosed with:
- Type 1 Diabetes
- Type 2 Diabetes
- Gestational Diabetes (for duration of pregnancy and postpartum period)

**Available on Plans:** All ToggleHealth plans (Gold HMO, Platinum PPO, Silver EPO, Bronze HDHP)

### Program Benefits

#### All Plans Include

- **Quarterly Care Coordinator Check-Ins** - Dedicated diabetes care coordinator contacts you every 3 months to review blood sugar control, medications, and lifestyle
- **100% Coverage for Diabetes Education Classes** - Accredited diabetes self-management education classes covered at 100% regardless of deductible
- **Nutritionist Consultations** - One-on-one sessions with registered dietitian nutritionist

#### Benefits by Plan

| Plan | Glucose Monitor & Supplies | Nutritionist Visits |
|------|---------------------------|---------------------|
| **Platinum PPO** | Free glucose monitor and unlimited test strips | 12 sessions per year at no cost |
| **Gold HMO** | Free glucose monitor and unlimited test strips | 6 sessions per year at no cost |
| **Silver EPO** | Discounted glucose monitor and test strips | 4 sessions per year at no cost |
| **Bronze HDHP** | Subject to deductible | Subject to deductible |

### Participating Facilities

**Pacific Northwest Diabetes Center**
- Address: 3500 Endocrine Way, Seattle, WA 98109
- Phone: 206-555-8001
- Services: Diabetes education, insulin pump training, continuous glucose monitoring, endocrinology consultations, nutrition counseling, diabetes foot care

### How to Enroll

1. **Call Member Services:** 1-800-TOGGLE-1
2. **Have Your Doctor Refer You:** Ask your doctor to submit referral
3. **Automatic Enrollment:** Members with diabetes diagnosis codes are automatically enrolled

### Program Features

- **Personalized Care Plan** - Individualized diabetes management plan
- **Medication Management** - Review of diabetes medications and insulin therapy
- **Continuous Glucose Monitoring Support** - Training and support for CGM devices
- **Insulin Pump Training** - For members using insulin pump therapy
- **Foot Care Screening** - Annual diabetic foot examinations
- **Eye Exam Coordination** - Assistance scheduling annual diabetic eye exams
- **A1C Tracking** - Monitor hemoglobin A1C levels quarterly
- **Complication Prevention** - Education on preventing diabetes complications
