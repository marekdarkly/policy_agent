## Prescription Drug Coverage

### Before Deductible is Met

**You Pay:** 100% of prescription costs

**Plan Pays:** 0%

**Costs Count Toward:** Your annual deductible

### After Deductible is Met

**You Pay:** $0

**Plan Pays:** 100%

**All Prescription Tiers:**
- Generic: 100% after deductible
- Preferred Brand: 100% after deductible
- Non-Preferred Brand: 100% after deductible
- Specialty: 100% after deductible
