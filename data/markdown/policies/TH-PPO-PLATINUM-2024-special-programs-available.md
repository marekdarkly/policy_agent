## Special Programs Available

### Diabetes Management Program (DMP-2024)

**Eligibility:** Platinum PPO members diagnosed with Type 1 or Type 2 diabetes

**Program Benefits:**
- Quarterly care coordinator check-ins
- Free glucose monitor and test strips
- 100% coverage for diabetes education classes
- 12 nutritionist consultations per year at no cost

**Participating Facility:** Pacific Northwest Diabetes Center (Seattle, WA)

**How to Enroll:** Call Member Services at 1-800-TOGGLE-1

### Heart Health Program (HHP-2024)

**Eligibility:** Platinum PPO members with diagnosed cardiovascular disease

**Program Benefits:**
- Monthly nurse hotline access
- Cardiac rehabilitation coverage up to 48 sessions (more than standard coverage)
- Free blood pressure monitor and fitness tracker
- Waived copays for cardiac specialist visits

**Participating Facility:** Heart & Vascular Institute of Seattle

**How to Enroll:** Ask your cardiologist for a referral or call 1-800-TOGGLE-1

### Maternity Support Program (MSP-2024)

**Eligibility:** Pregnant Platinum PPO members

**Program Benefits:**
- 24/7 nurse hotline for pregnancy questions
- Prenatal vitamins covered at 100%
- Breast pump provided at no cost
- Postpartum depression screening and support
- Doula services covered up to $1,000

**Participating Facility:** Women's Health & Maternity Center (Bellevue, WA)

**How to Enroll:** Automatic enrollment when pregnancy is reported

### Premium Concierge Service (PCS-2024)

**Eligibility:** All Platinum PPO members (exclusive benefit)

**Program Benefits:**
- Dedicated care navigator assigned to you
- Appointment scheduling assistance
- Second opinion coordination
- Travel assistance for specialized care
- 24/7 concierge support line

**How to Access:** Call 1-800-TOGGLE-1 and ask for Concierge Services
