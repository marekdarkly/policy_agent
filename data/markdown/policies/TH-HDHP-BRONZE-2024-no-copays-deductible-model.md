## No Copays - Deductible Model

**Important:** The Bronze HDHP does NOT have copays like other plans.

| Service | Cost Before Deductible | Cost After Deductible |
|---------|----------------------|---------------------|
| **Primary Care Visit** | You pay 100% | Plan pays 100% |
| **Specialist Visit** | You pay 100% | Plan pays 100% |
| **Urgent Care** | You pay 100% | Plan pays 100% |
| **Emergency Room** | You pay 100% | Plan pays 100% |
| **Prescriptions** | You pay 100% | Plan pays 100% |
| **Hospital Stay** | You pay 100% | Plan pays 100% |
| **Preventive Care** | Plan pays 100% | Plan pays 100% |
