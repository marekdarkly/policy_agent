## Premium Concierge Service (PCS-2024)

**Program ID:** PCS-2024
**Program Name:** Premium Concierge Service

### Eligibility

**Exclusive to Platinum PPO Members** - Available to all members enrolled in TH-PPO-PLATINUM-2024

### Program Benefits

- **Dedicated Care Navigator** - Personal care navigator assigned to you
- **Appointment Scheduling Assistance** - Help booking appointments with specialists
- **Second Opinion Coordination** - Assistance obtaining second opinions from top specialists
- **Travel Assistance for Specialized Care** - Help coordinating care at distant specialized facilities
- **24/7 Concierge Support Line** - Direct line to concierge team anytime
- **Medical Records Management** - Help obtaining and organizing medical records
- **Prescription Management** - Assistance with prescription refills and pharmacy issues
- **Claims Assistance** - Help understanding and resolving claims issues
- **Provider Research** - Detailed information about providers' qualifications and experience

### How to Access

**Phone:** 1-800-TOGGLE-1 and ask for Concierge Services
**Available:** 24 hours a day, 7 days a week

### Service Examples

- **Finding Specialists** - "I need a top neurologist who specializes in multiple sclerosis"
- **Coordinating Complex Care** - "I'm seeing 5 different specialists and need help coordinating"
- **Second Opinions** - "I want a second opinion on my cancer diagnosis"
- **Travel for Care** - "I need to see a specialist at Mayo Clinic - can you help?"
- **Appointment Scheduling** - "I need an appointment with a cardiologist this week"
