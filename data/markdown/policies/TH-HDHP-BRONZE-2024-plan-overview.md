## Plan Overview

The ToggleHealth Bronze HDHP is designed for healthy members who want the lowest premiums and the ability to save with a Health Savings Account (HSA). This plan has a high deductible but 100% coverage once the deductible is met.

### Key Features

- **Health Savings Account (HSA) Eligible**
- **Employer HSA Contribution:** $750 (individual) / $1,500 (family)
- **Lowest Monthly Premium** - Save on premiums
- **No Copays** - All services subject to deductible (except preventive care)
- **100% Coverage After Deductible** - No coinsurance once deductible is met
- **No PCP or Referrals Required**
