## Cost Example Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Healthy Year (Minimal Care)

**Services Used:**
- Annual physical (preventive)
- 1 urgent care visit
- 2 prescriptions

**Your Cost:**
- Annual physical: $0 (preventive care covered 100%)
- Urgent care: $120 (counts toward deductible)
- Prescriptions: $80 (counts toward deductible)
- **Total Out-of-Pocket:** $200
- **Plus Premium:** $245 x 12 = $2,940
- **Total Annual Cost:** $3,140

**HSA Contribution:** $750 from employer helps offset

### Scenario 2: Moderate Year (Some Medical Needs)

**Services Used:**
- Annual physical
- 4 doctor visits
- Surgery ($8,000 billed)
- Prescriptions

**Your Cost:**
- Annual physical: $0
- Doctor visits: $600 (toward deductible)
- First $5,900 of surgery: $5,900 (meets deductible)
- Remaining surgery: $0 (deductible met, plan pays 100%)
- Prescriptions after deductible: $0
- **Total Out-of-Pocket:** $6,500 (deductible)
- **Plus Premium:** $2,940
- **Total Annual Cost:** $9,440

**With HSA:** $750 employer contribution reduces effective cost to $8,690

### Scenario 3: Major Medical Event

**Services Used:**
- Major surgery
- Hospital stay
- Follow-up care
- Total billed: $100,000

**Your Cost:**
- First $6,500: You pay (deductible)
- Next $93,500: Plan pays 100%
- **Total Out-of-Pocket:** $6,500
- **Plus Premium:** $2,940
- **Total Annual Cost:** $9,440

**With HSA:** $750 reduces to $8,690

**Plan Protects You:** No matter how high medical bills go, you never pay more than $6,500 + premiums.
