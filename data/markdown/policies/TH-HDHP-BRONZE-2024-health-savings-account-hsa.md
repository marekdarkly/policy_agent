## Health Savings Account (HSA)

### HSA Eligibility

The Bronze HDHP (TH-HDHP-BRONZE-2024) is HSA-qualified. You can contribute pre-tax dollars to an HSA to pay for qualified medical expenses.

### 2024 HSA Contribution Limits

| Coverage Type | IRS Maximum | Catch-Up (55+) | With Employer Contribution |
|--------------|-------------|----------------|---------------------------|
| **Individual** | $4,150 | $1,000 | $3,400 (after $750 employer) |
| **Family** | $8,300 | $1,000 | $6,800 (after $1,500 employer) |

### ToggleHealth Employer HSA Contribution

**Individual Coverage:** $750 per year

**Family Coverage:** $1,500 per year

**Deposit Schedule:** Quarterly ($187.50 individual / $375 family)

### HSA Advantages

✅ **Triple Tax Advantage:**
1. **Tax-deductible contributions** - Reduce your taxable income
2. **Tax-free growth** - Earnings grow tax-free
3. **Tax-free withdrawals** - For qualified medical expenses

✅ **Your Money:** HSA funds roll over year to year. You keep them even if you change jobs or insurance.

✅ **Investment Options:** After reaching minimum balance, invest HSA funds for growth.

✅ **Qualified Expenses:** Use for medical, dental, vision, prescriptions, and more.

### HSA Support Program (HSA-2024)

**Eligibility:** All Bronze HDHP members

**Benefits:**
- Free HSA account setup
- HSA contribution matching up to $750 (individual) or $1,500 (family)
- Financial planning consultations
- Online HSA management tools

**Enroll:** Call 1-800-TOGGLE-1
