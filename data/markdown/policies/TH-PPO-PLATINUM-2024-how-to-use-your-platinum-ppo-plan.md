## How to Use Your Platinum PPO Plan

### Finding a Provider

**In-Network Providers:**
1. Visit my.togglehealth.com and use the provider directory
2. Call Member Services at 1-800-TOGGLE-1
3. Use the ToggleHealth Mobile app

**Out-of-Network Providers:**
- You may see any licensed provider, but will pay higher costs (60% coverage)
- Out-of-network services have separate, higher deductibles

### No Referrals Needed

With the Platinum PPO plan, you can see specialists directly without a referral from a primary care physician. Simply find an in-network specialist and make an appointment.

### Getting Preauthorization

Some services require preauthorization before receiving care:

**Services Requiring Preauthorization:**
- Inpatient hospital admissions
- Surgical procedures
- Advanced imaging (MRI, CT, PET scans)
- Home health care
- Durable medical equipment
- Mental health inpatient treatment
- Substance abuse inpatient treatment
- Transplant services
- Bariatric surgery
- Sleep studies
- Genetic testing
- Specialty medications

**How to Get Preauthorization:**
- **Phone:** 1-800-TOGGLE-2
- **Fax:** 1-206-555-0199
- **Online:** portal.togglehealth.com/preauth

**Processing Time:**
- Standard requests: 3-5 business days
- Urgent requests: 24-48 hours
