# Copays and Cost-Sharing

## Plan Information
**Plan Name:** ToggleHealth HMO Gold Plan
**Plan ID:** TH-HMO-GOLD-2024
**Plan Type:** HMO

## Office Visit Copays
**Primary Care Physician (PCP):** $25 per visit
**Specialist Visit:** $50 per visit
**Urgent Care Visit:** $75 per visit
**Emergency Room:** $350 per visit (waived if admitted)
**Telehealth Visit:** $0 (no copay for virtual PCP visits)

## Diagnostic Services Copays
**Laboratory Services:** $15 per visit
**X-Ray Imaging:** $50 per service
**Advanced Imaging (MRI/CT/PET Scan):** $200 per service
**Ultrasound:** $75 per service

## Therapy Services Copays
**Physical Therapy:** $40 per visit (up to 30 visits per year)
**Occupational Therapy:** $40 per visit (up to 30 visits per year)
**Speech Therapy:** $40 per visit (up to 30 visits per year)
**Mental Health Therapy:** $40 per visit (unlimited visits)
**Chiropractic Care:** $40 per visit (up to 20 visits per year)

## Hospital Services
**Inpatient Hospital Stay:** $500 per admission
**Outpatient Surgery:** $250 per procedure
**Skilled Nursing Facility:** $150 per day (up to 100 days per year)
**Home Health Care:** $0 copay (up to 100 visits per year)

## Coinsurance
After meeting your annual deductible ($1,500 individual / $3,000 family), you pay **20% coinsurance** for most covered services until you reach your out-of-pocket maximum.

## Important Notes
- All copays and coinsurance amounts apply toward your out-of-pocket maximum
- Emergency room copay is waived if you are admitted to the hospital
- Services must be obtained from in-network providers (except emergency care)
- PCP referral required for specialist visits and advanced imaging
