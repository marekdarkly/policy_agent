# Health Insurance Plan Overview

## Plan Identification
**Plan Name:** ToggleHealth HMO Gold Plan
**Plan ID:** TH-HMO-GOLD-2024
**Plan Type:** HMO (Health Maintenance Organization)
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**End Date:** December 31, 2024

## Coverage Details
**Coverage Type:** Individual and Family
**Network:** ToggleHealth Preferred Network
**Primary Care Physician (PCP) Required:** Yes
**Referrals Required for Specialists:** Yes

## Annual Financial Information
**Annual Deductible:**
- Individual: $1,500
- Family: $3,000

**Out-of-Pocket Maximum:**
- Individual: $6,000
- Family: $12,000

**Monthly Premium:**
- Individual: $450
- Family: $1,200

## Plan Features
- No deductible for preventive care
- PCP selection required within 30 days of enrollment
- Specialist visits require PCP referral
- Emergency care covered nationwide
- Prescription drug coverage included
- Telehealth services available
