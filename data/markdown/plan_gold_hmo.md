## Plan Overview

The ToggleHealth Gold HMO provides comprehensive coordinated care through a primary care physician with lower out-of-pocket costs than the Silver EPO plan. This plan is ideal for members who value coordinated care and don't mind getting referrals for specialist visits.

### Key Features

- **Primary Care Physician (PCP) Required** - Select your PCP to coordinate all your care
- **Referrals Required for Specialists** - Your PCP will refer you to specialists when needed
- **In-Network Only** - Coverage only for in-network providers (except emergencies)
- **Coordinated Care Model** - Your PCP manages your overall healthcare
- **Comprehensive Disease Management Programs**
- **Lower Out-of-Pocket Costs** - Better cost sharing than Silver EPO

The Gold HMO plan does NOT cover:

- Cosmetic procedures not medically necessary
- Experimental or investigational treatments
- Fertility treatments and services
- Weight loss programs and surgery (unless medically necessary)

- Vision care (except for eye exams)
- Hearing aids
- Private duty nursing
- Custodial care
- **Acupuncture** (not covered under Gold HMO)
- Services received outside service area (except emergency)
- Services from out-of-network providers without referral

## How to Use Your Gold HMO Plan

### Select Your Primary Care Physician (PCP)

**Required:** You must select a PCP from the TH-HMO-PRIMARY network.

**How to Select:**
1. Visit my.togglehealth.com and search for PCPs
2. Call Member Services at 1-800-TOGGLE-1
3. Use the ToggleHealth Mobile app

**Your PCP will:**
- Provide primary medical care
- Coordinate all your healthcare
- Provide referrals to specialists
- Manage your overall health

### Getting Referrals to Specialists

**Referral Required:** You must get a referral from your PCP before seeing a specialist (except for OB/GYN and emergency care).

**How It Works:**
1. See your PCP for evaluation
2. PCP determines if specialist care is needed
3. PCP provides written or electronic referral
4. You schedule appointment with in-network specialist
5. Specialist reports back to your PCP

**No Referral Needed For:**
- OB/GYN visits (women's health)
- Emergency care
- Preventive care

### Getting Preauthorization

**Services Requiring Preauthorization (Gold HMO):**
- Inpatient hospital admissions
- Surgical procedures
- Advanced imaging (MRI, CT, PET scans)
- Home health care
- Durable medical equipment over $500
- Mental health inpatient treatment
- Substance abuse inpatient treatment
- Transplant services
- Bariatric surgery
- Sleep studies
- Genetic testing
- Specialty medications

**How to Get Preauthorization:**
- Phone: 1-800-TOGGLE-2
- Fax: 1-206-555-0199
- Online: portal.togglehealth.com/preauth

**Processing Time:**
- Standard: 3-5 business days
- Urgent: 24-48 hours

**Important:** Your PCP or specialist will usually request preauthorization for you. Always confirm preauthorization has been obtained before receiving services.

## Claims Process

### In-Network Claims

When you see in-network providers (providers in the TH-HMO-PRIMARY network), they will file claims directly with ToggleHealth. You are responsible only for copays, coinsurance, and deductibles.

**You don't need to file claims for in-network care.**

### Out-of-Network Services

**Important:** The Gold HMO plan does NOT cover out-of-network services except for emergency care. If you see an out-of-network provider for non-emergency care, you will be responsible for the full cost.

## Frequently Asked Questions - Gold HMO

**Q: What's the difference between Gold HMO and Platinum PPO?**
A: Gold HMO requires a PCP and referrals, is in-network only, and has lower premiums. Platinum PPO offers more flexibility with no PCP, no referrals, out-of-network coverage, but higher premiums.

**Q: Can I change my PCP?**
A: Yes. You can change your PCP at any time by calling Member Services at 1-800-TOGGLE-1 or online at my.togglehealth.com. Changes are effective the 1st of the following month.

**Q: What if I need to see a specialist?**
A: Contact your PCP first. They will evaluate your condition and provide a referral if specialist care is needed. You cannot see a specialist without a referral (except OB/GYN).

**Q: What happens if I see a specialist without a referral?**
A: The visit will not be covered and you will be responsible for the full cost of the visit.

**Q: Can I see out-of-network providers?**
A: Only for emergency care. The Gold HMO plan does not cover out-of-network care except for emergencies.

**Q: What if I receive emergency care while traveling?**
A: Emergency care is covered anywhere in the United States. You should receive care first, then notify ToggleHealth within 48 hours. Call 1-800-TOGGLE-1.

**Q: Does Gold HMO cover acupuncture?**
A: No. Acupuncture is NOT covered under the Gold HMO plan. It is only covered under the Platinum PPO plan.
