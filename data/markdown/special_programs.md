# ToggleHealth Special Programs

**Document Type:** Special Programs and Disease Management
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024

---

## Overview

ToggleHealth offers specialized disease management and wellness programs designed to help members manage chronic conditions, navigate complex healthcare situations, and achieve better health outcomes. These programs are included at no additional cost to eligible members.

---

## Diabetes Management Program (DMP-2024)

**Program ID:** DMP-2024
**Program Name:** Diabetes Management Program

### Eligibility

Available to ToggleHealth members diagnosed with:
- Type 1 Diabetes
- Type 2 Diabetes
- Gestational Diabetes (for duration of pregnancy and postpartum period)

**Available on Plans:** All ToggleHealth plans (Gold HMO, Platinum PPO, Silver EPO, Bronze HDHP)

### Program Benefits

#### All Plans Include

- **Quarterly Care Coordinator Check-Ins** - Dedicated diabetes care coordinator contacts you every 3 months to review blood sugar control, medications, and lifestyle
- **100% Coverage for Diabetes Education Classes** - Accredited diabetes self-management education classes covered at 100% regardless of deductible
- **Nutritionist Consultations** - One-on-one sessions with registered dietitian nutritionist

#### Benefits by Plan

| Plan | Glucose Monitor & Supplies | Nutritionist Visits |
|------|---------------------------|---------------------|
| **Platinum PPO** | Free glucose monitor and unlimited test strips | 12 sessions per year at no cost |
| **Gold HMO** | Free glucose monitor and unlimited test strips | 6 sessions per year at no cost |
| **Silver EPO** | Discounted glucose monitor and test strips | 4 sessions per year at no cost |
| **Bronze HDHP** | Subject to deductible | Subject to deductible |

### Participating Facilities

**Pacific Northwest Diabetes Center**
- Address: 3500 Endocrine Way, Seattle, WA 98109
- Phone: 206-555-8001
- Services: Diabetes education, insulin pump training, continuous glucose monitoring, endocrinology consultations, nutrition counseling, diabetes foot care

### How to Enroll

1. **Call Member Services:** 1-800-TOGGLE-1
2. **Have Your Doctor Refer You:** Ask your doctor to submit referral
3. **Automatic Enrollment:** Members with diabetes diagnosis codes are automatically enrolled

### Program Features

- **Personalized Care Plan** - Individualized diabetes management plan
- **Medication Management** - Review of diabetes medications and insulin therapy
- **Continuous Glucose Monitoring Support** - Training and support for CGM devices
- **Insulin Pump Training** - For members using insulin pump therapy
- **Foot Care Screening** - Annual diabetic foot examinations
- **Eye Exam Coordination** - Assistance scheduling annual diabetic eye exams
- **A1C Tracking** - Monitor hemoglobin A1C levels quarterly
- **Complication Prevention** - Education on preventing diabetes complications

## Heart Health Program (HHP-2024)

**Program ID:** HHP-2024
**Program Name:** Heart Health Program

### Eligibility

Available to members with diagnosed cardiovascular disease including:
- Coronary artery disease
- Heart failure / congestive heart failure
- Previous heart attack (myocardial infarction)
- Previous cardiac procedures (stent, bypass surgery, etc.)
- Arrhythmias requiring management
- Cardiomyopathy

**Available on Plans:** Gold HMO and Platinum PPO only

**Not available on:** Silver EPO or Bronze HDHP

### Program Benefits by Plan

#### Platinum PPO Members

- **Monthly Nurse Hotline Access** - Dedicated cardiac nurse available for questions
- **Cardiac Rehabilitation Coverage:** Up to 48 sessions (12 more than standard coverage)
- **Free Blood Pressure Monitor AND Fitness Tracker** - Provided at no cost
- **Waived Copays for Cardiac Specialist Visits** - $0 copay for cardiologist visits

#### Gold HMO Members

- **Monthly Nurse Hotline Access** - Dedicated cardiac nurse available for questions
- **Cardiac Rehabilitation Coverage:** Up to 36 sessions
- **Free Blood Pressure Monitor** - Provided at no cost
- **Waived Copays for Cardiac Specialist Visits** - $0 copay for cardiologist visits (normally $45)

### Participating Facility

**Heart & Vascular Institute of Seattle**
- Address: 1800 Cardiac Plaza, Seattle, WA 98101
- Phone: 206-555-8003
- Services: Cardiac catheterization, echocardiography, stress testing, cardiac rehabilitation, vascular ultrasound, pacemaker clinic
- Accreditation: ACC Accredited Chest Pain Center

### How to Enroll

1. **Ask Your Cardiologist** - Request referral to Heart Health Program
2. **Call Member Services:** 1-800-TOGGLE-1
3. **Automatic Referral** - Some members automatically referred after cardiac events

### Program Features

- **Personalized Care Plan** - Individualized cardiac care management plan
- **Medication Adherence Support** - Help managing cardiac medications
- **Lifestyle Coaching** - Diet, exercise, and stress management
- **Blood Pressure Monitoring** - Home monitoring support and tracking
- **Weight Management** - For members needing to lose weight for heart health
- **Tobacco Cessation** - Specialized support for quitting smoking
- **Depression Screening** - Mental health screening (common after cardiac events)
- **Hospital-to-Home Transition** - Extra support after hospitalization

## Maternity Support Program (MSP-2024)

**Program ID:** MSP-2024
**Program Name:** Maternity Support Program

### Eligibility

Available to pregnant members from pregnancy confirmation through 3 months postpartum.

**Available on Plans:** All ToggleHealth plans

### Program Benefits by Plan

#### Platinum PPO

- **24/7 Nurse Hotline** - Experienced obstetric nurses available anytime
- **Prenatal Vitamins Covered 100%** - No cost for prescription prenatal vitamins
- **Breast Pump Provided at No Cost** - Hospital-grade or personal breast pump
- **Postpartum Depression Screening and Support** - Comprehensive mental health screening
- **Doula Services:** Up to $1,000 coverage for certified doula services

#### Gold HMO

- **24/7 Nurse Hotline** - Experienced obstetric nurses available anytime
- **Prenatal Vitamins Covered 100%** - No cost for prescription prenatal vitamins
- **Breast Pump Provided at No Cost** - Hospital-grade or personal breast pump
- **Postpartum Depression Screening and Support** - Comprehensive mental health screening

#### Silver EPO

- **24/7 Nurse Hotline** - Experienced obstetric nurses available anytime
- **Prenatal Vitamins Covered 80%** - Subject to regular prescription coverage
- **Breast Pump Rental Program** - Rental at discounted rate
- **Postpartum Depression Screening** - Basic screening provided

#### Bronze HDHP

- **24/7 Nurse Hotline** - Experienced obstetric nurses available anytime
- **Prenatal Vitamins:** Subject to deductible
- **Breast Pump:** Subject to deductible
- **Postpartum Depression Screening** - Subject to deductible

### Participating Facility

**Women's Health & Maternity Center**
- Address: 2800 Women's Health Drive, Bellevue, WA 98006
- Phone: 425-555-8005
- Services: Prenatal care, high-risk pregnancy management, labor and delivery, postpartum care, lactation consulting, prenatal genetic counseling, 3D/4D ultrasound
- Delivery Beds: 24
- NICU: Level II

### How to Enroll

**Automatic Enrollment** - When you report your pregnancy to ToggleHealth, you are automatically enrolled in the Maternity Support Program.

**To Report Pregnancy:**
1. Call Member Services: 1-800-TOGGLE-1
2. Notify online at my.togglehealth.com
3. Have your OB/GYN submit notification

### Program Features

- **Personalized Maternity Care Plan**
- **Prenatal Education Materials** - Books, videos, online resources
- **High-Risk Pregnancy Management** - Additional support for complications
- **Lactation Consulting** - Breastfeeding support from certified lactation consultants
- **Newborn Care Education** - Classes and resources for caring for baby
- **Postpartum Check-Ins** - Follow-up at 1 week, 1 month, and 3 months postpartum
- **Mental Health Support** - Screening and treatment for postpartum depression/anxiety
- **Return-to-Work Support** - Resources for working mothers

## HSA Support Program (HSA-2024)

**Program ID:** HSA-2024
**Program Name:** HSA Support Program

### Eligibility

**Exclusive to Bronze HDHP Members** - Available to all members enrolled in TH-HDHP-BRONZE-2024

### Program Benefits

- **Free HSA Account Setup** - No account opening fees or annual fees
- **HSA Contribution Matching:**
  - Individual coverage: Up to $750 per year
  - Family coverage: Up to $1,500 per year
- **Financial Planning Consultations** - Sessions with financial advisors on HSA strategies
- **Online HSA Management Tools** - Easy-to-use portal for managing HSA
- **Educational Resources** - Guides on maximizing HSA benefits
- **Tax Planning Assistance** - Help understanding HSA tax advantages
- **Investment Options** - Access to HSA investment accounts after minimum balance

### HSA Contribution Schedule

ToggleHealth employer contributions are deposited quarterly:
- **Individual:** $187.50 per quarter
- **Family:** $375 per quarter

### How to Enroll

1. **Automatic Enrollment** - All Bronze HDHP members automatically eligible
2. **Set Up HSA Account** - Call 1-800-TOGGLE-1 to set up your HSA
3. **Start Contributing** - Begin making pre-tax contributions from your paycheck

### Program Features

- **HSA Debit Card** - Use for qualified medical expenses
- **Online Account Access** - View balance, transactions, and invest funds
- **Mobile App** - Manage HSA on the go
- **Receipts Management** - Store and track medical expense receipts
- **Tax Forms** - Automatic generation of HSA tax forms (Form 5498-SA, Form 1099-SA)
