## Plan Overview

The ToggleHealth Silver EPO offers affordable coverage with moderate deductibles and no referrals required. This plan provides access to a curated network of high-quality providers.

### Key Features

- **No Primary Care Physician (PCP) Required**
- **No Referrals Needed for Specialists** 
- **In-Network Only** - No out-of-network coverage (except emergencies)
- **Curated Provider Network** - High-quality, selected providers
- **Lower Premiums** - More affordable than Gold and Platinum

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I see out-of-network providers?**
A: No. The Silver EPO does NOT cover out-of-network services except for emergency care. All non-emergency care must be received from TH-EPO-SELECT network providers.

**Q: Do I need a referral to see a specialist?**
A: No. You can see any in-network specialist directly without a referral.

**Q: What's the difference between Silver EPO and Gold HMO?**
A: Silver EPO has no referral requirement and higher deductibles but lower premiums. Gold HMO requires a PCP and referrals but has better cost sharing.

**Q: Why is the network smaller than other plans?**
A: The EPO network is curated to include high-quality providers. This allows us to offer lower premiums while maintaining quality care.
