**Best for:** Healthy members who want lowest premiums and HSA benefits

**Key Features:**

- Coinsurance: 0% (100% coverage after deductible)

**Important Note:** All services except preventive care are subject to the full deductible. After the deductible is met, the plan pays 100%.

## How to Choose the Right Plan

### Choose Platinum PPO if you:
- Want the freedom to see any specialist without a referral
- Travel frequently and need nationwide coverage
- Want access to out-of-network providers
- Prefer the lowest out-of-pocket costs when you need care
- Want Premium Concierge Service

### Choose Gold HMO if you:
- Prefer coordinated care through a primary care physician
- Want lower premiums than Platinum PPO
- Don't mind getting referrals for specialists
- Stay within the network coverage area
- Want comprehensive disease management programs

### Choose Silver EPO if you:
- Want lower premiums and moderate deductibles
- Don't need out-of-network coverage
- Want to see specialists without referrals
- Live in King, Pierce, Snohomish, or Spokane counties (WA) or Multnomah County (OR)
- Want access to curated high-quality providers

### Choose Bronze HDHP if you:
- Are generally healthy with minimal healthcare needs
- Want to save money with an HSA
- Can afford the high deductible if needed
- Want the lowest possible monthly premium
- Want employer HSA contributions
